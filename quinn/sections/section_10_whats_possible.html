What is missing is the engine that converts all of that into consistent, scalable growth.

1
Quinn Law Group runs paid marketing campaigns across all seven office locations simultaneously — generating inbound leads for PI, workers comp, and medical malpractice in Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, South Jersey, and every market in between. 3

The 50–75 new cases per six months are not just on the scoreboard — they are on the income statement, with a clear line between cases signed and money in Sean's pocket, so that every growth decision is made with full financial visibility rather than intuition.
