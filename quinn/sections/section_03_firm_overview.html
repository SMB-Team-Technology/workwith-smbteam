Here Is Where Quinn Law Group Stands Today

Quinn Law Group is a Philadelphia-based personal injury firm led by nationally recognized trial lawyer Sean Quinn, with 25 years of practice and seven office locations spanning Pennsylvania and New Jersey — handling PI, workers compensation, and medical malpractice cases with a $15,000 minimum case value that reflects the seriousness of the work. The firm has built exceptional credibility: a 4.9-star Google rating and strong brand recognition across two states. The single most important finding is that Quinn Law Group does not appear to be running any owned paid digital marketing — no Google Ads, no LSA, and no Meta Ads — across any of its three practice areas, while well-funded local and national competitors actively capture every high-intent search in the Philadelphia market.

Stage 6 is where Quinn Law Group's seven offices become a coordinated revenue engine that generates cases consistently — where Sean is reviewing growth metrics instead of managing intake processes, and where the firm wins market share because its systems are faster and smarter than the competition's.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • Seven office locations across PA and NJ — but no owned digital marketing system generating inbound leads across any of them
  • After-hours intake is entirely manual — losing 2–3 confirmed qualified leads per month ($45,000/month) in a market where leads cost $100–$140 per click
  • No Google Ads, LSA, or Meta Ads running for PI, workers comp, or medical malpractice — while better-funded competitors dominate every digital channel
  • Follow-up requires 2–3 hours of intake manager time per day, and leads with no response within two weeks are dropped with no automated re-engagement

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between where Quinn Law Group is today and where it needs to be is not a credibility gap — the reputation is already there; it is a systems and marketing gap, and that kind of gap closes faster than most people expect when the right infrastructure is in place.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Quinn Law Group
Quinn Law Group is operating at Stage 4 — a multi-location firm with strong case values, an experienced managing partner, and a team with defined roles, but without the owned digital marketing systems and automated intake infrastructure that would allow it to consistently convert leads in one of the most competitive personal injury markets in the country. The most urgent gap is the complete absence of owned paid marketing: competitors with similar or lesser reputations are capturing leads at scale on Google, LSA, and Meta that Quinn is not pursuing at all. Until paid marketing and automated intake are in place, the goal of 50–75 new cases in six months will remain dependent on expensive external lead sources in a market where Quinn already has the credibility to compete on its own terms.
