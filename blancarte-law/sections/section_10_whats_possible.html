Here's What's Possible for Blancarte Law

Jesse and James Blancarte built a real law practice from the ground up in one of the most competitive markets in the country — $1 million in annual revenue from word of mouth and referrals alone is not luck, it is skill and dedication and the trust of the community they serve. What they have built is not the problem. What is missing is the system that takes everything they have already earned and turns it into a firm that gives them their lives back — one where a vacation is possible, a court day does not create a crisis, and growth is planned instead of hoped for. That system is what this plan delivers.

1
Jesse and James take two full weeks off — court calendar cleared, phones covered, intake running — and come back to a firm that kept moving without a single call asking them to intervene
2
Revenue reaches $150,000 a month and it is tracked, forecasted, and planned — not discovered at year end — because a financial dashboard shows exactly what is coming in, what is going out, and what needs to happen next
3
Paralegals are doing paralegal work, Patricia owns the day-to-day, intake converts around the clock in English and Spanish, and Jesse and James focus on the cases they choose — not the ones they are stuck with because no one else can handle them

This is not a fantasy. SMB Team can make this a reality for Blancarte Law.
