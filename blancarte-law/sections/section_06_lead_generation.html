Lead Generation

Blancarte Law currently generates almost all of its new clients through a single nonprofit referral source — a single point of failure with no marketing backup, no digital visibility, and no predictable growth engine. In one of the most competitive immigration law markets in the country, the firm does not appear in any Google search, runs no observed paid ads, and has zero online reviews to convert the prospects it cannot reach through referrals.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • The current blancartelaw.com is essentially a blank digital business card — phone number and address only, with no practice area descriptions, no attorney bios, no testimonials, no contact form, and no content of any kind
  • PageSpeed scores: could not be retrieved during automated research (tool requires JavaScript execution) — manual check required at pagespeed.web.dev before launch
  • No above-the-fold CTA beyond a phone number — visitors who do not call immediately have no other path to become a lead
  • No blog, no FAQ, no practice area landing pages — the site has zero SEO value and cannot rank for any search term in any practice area
Blind Spot: Competitors in the same Wilshire corridor — including Goldstein Immigration Lawyers 4 blocks away — are running paid search ads that capture visitors searching by location or practice area. These are prospects who may have called Blancarte Law if the firm had any digital presence to be found.
The Positive
  • A new website is actively in development (blancarte.wondermedia.dev) — the firm recognizes the gap and has taken the first step toward fixing it
The Gap
  • Every visitor who lands on the current site and finds no information leaves immediately and calls a competitor instead — the site is producing zero leads
  • With no practice area pages, the site cannot rank organically for any immigration or criminal defense keyword — the firm is missing every organic search lead in its market
  • No effective paid advertising is possible without a proper landing page — the marketing engine cannot be turned on until the website is ready to receive traffic
A website that converts visitors into consultations is not optional — it is the foundation every other marketing channel builds on, and right now Blancarte Law does not have one. The new site in development needs to launch alongside the marketing activation to maximize its impact.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • Zero verified reviews across every platform checked — Avvo, Yellow Pages, Martindale, and Lawyer.com all show "not yet reviewed" or "be the first to review"
  • Not visible in the local 3-pack for any tested immigration or criminal defense search in Los Angeles — the firm is absent from every local search result
  • At least 3 different addresses (915 Wilshire Ste 700, 600 Wilshire Ste 500, 600 Wilshire) and 3 different phone numbers ((213) 342-6622, (310) 205-9922, 213-629-7030) are listed across directories — NAP inconsistencies are actively suppressing local rankings
Blind Spot: Google uses NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across the web as a core signal for local search rankings. With conflicting listings on Avvo, Yellow Pages, and Lawyer.com, Google cannot confidently match the firm to a single verified location — which means it deprioritizes the firm in local results. This is fixable at zero cost and should be corrected before any other local SEO work begins.
The Positive
  • The firm is indexed on Avvo, FindLaw, Justia, LawInfo, and several other directories — a baseline directory presence exists that can be corrected and built upon rather than started from scratch
The Gap
  • With 0 reviews, Blancarte Law cannot compete in local pack rankings where every top immigration competitor has hundreds to nearly a thousand reviews
  • NAP inconsistencies must be corrected across all directories before any local SEO investment will produce meaningful results
Goldstein Immigration Lawyers — located 4 blocks away at 611 Wilshire Blvd — appears consistently in top local results for immigration attorney searches in downtown Los Angeles, backed by 585 Google reviews, a fully optimized Google Business Profile, and active content marketing across 7 platforms. Their local SEO foundation is years ahead of where Blancarte Law stands today.
Every month without an active review generation system is another month competitors extend their lead in local rankings — and every position they hold is a case that could have gone to Blancarte Law instead.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • Blancarte Law does not appear to be running Google Ads for immigration or criminal defense searches in Los Angeles — no ads were observed for any tested keyword. The firm may not be configured to run ads for these terms, or may be getting outbid by competitors with established Quality Scores and larger budgets.
  • With no existing website content or landing pages, launching ads immediately would require the new website to be ready first — making the site rebuild the critical path item before paid traffic can be turned on.
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Goldstein Immigration Lawyers — active paid search advertiser targeting immigration keywords in Los Angeles; a published case study documents how PPC campaigns directly doubled their client intake. They are bidding on the same keywords Blancarte Law should own.
  • Camisassa Law Group — prominently appearing in paid and organic results for "immigration lawyer Los Angeles" and "immigration attorney Los Angeles CA" with a dedicated, conversion-optimized bilingual website built to capture and convert paid traffic.
The Opportunity

  • Criminal defense keywords: "criminal defense attorney downtown Los Angeles," "DUI attorney Los Angeles 90017," "criminal lawyer Wilshire Blvd" — targeting the secondary practice area in the firm's immediate geographic corridor
  • Spanish-language campaigns: "abogado de inmigración Los Angeles," "abogado defensa criminal Los Angeles," "consulta de inmigración gratis" — reaching Blancarte Law's core bilingual immigrant audience at lower CPCs than English-only keywords
  • Competitor conquest: running ads on searches for well-known local competitors captures their research traffic at the moment a prospect is actively comparing attorneys
Blind Spot: Immigration CPCs in Los Angeles range from $15–$45+ per click and the market is actively contested. Every day without a Google Ads campaign is a day that high-intent leads — people actively searching for an immigration attorney right now — are going to Goldstein, Camisassa, and the other firms that are already spending. These are not future clients; they are today's clients.

Competitor LSA Landscape

The Opportunity

  • Building the review base needed for LSA qualification creates a compounding asset: reviews improve LSA ranking, local pack ranking, and organic credibility simultaneously — every review does triple duty
  • Getting Google Screened instantly positions Blancarte Law as a verified, trusted firm at the very top of every immigration and criminal defense search — a trust signal that compensates for the firm's current lack of review history while it is being built
