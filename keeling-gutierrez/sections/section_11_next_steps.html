Intake Script & Follow-Up Protocol
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
70 consultations per month produce only 30 signed cases — 40 warm, pre-qualified prospects leave without signing each month, and not a single one of them receives a follow-up call or message. At a $3,000 average case value, that is $120,000 in potential revenue walking out the door every 30 days.
The Opportunity
A defined intake script combined with a structured 3-touch follow-up sequence — contact within 24 hours, second touchpoint at 72 hours, final touchpoint at 7 days — could convert 10–15 of those 40 monthly non-converters into signed cases, adding $30,000–$45,000 in monthly revenue from leads the firm already has.
After-Hours Lead Coverage
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Bankruptcy decisions are stress-driven and often made at night or on weekends — exactly when the firm's current process routes calls to the partners' personal cell phones. Calls that go unanswered after hours are leads who move on to the next firm on their list before the office opens Monday morning.
The Opportunity
A dedicated after-hours answering service or protocol captures leads who call evenings and weekends — a segment that represents a significant and currently unmeasured portion of the firm's inbound volume. This also removes the personal burden from Yolanda and Ken and gives them hours back that are currently not their own.
NAP Citation Cleanup (Rebrand Correction)
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
The October 2025 rebrand from "Keeling Law" to "Keeling Gutierrez Debt Relief Attorneys" left two active Yelp listings under different names and YellowPages still showing the old firm name. Google uses NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency as a local SEO ranking signal — every mismatched citation dilutes the new brand's authority and drags down local search rankings.
The Opportunity
Consolidating all citations under the correct updated name — merging the Yelp duplicate and correcting YellowPages — recovers the SEO authority that accumulated under 40 years of consistent listings and redirects it fully to the new brand identity. This is a one-time cleanup with compounding benefit for every future SEO effort.
