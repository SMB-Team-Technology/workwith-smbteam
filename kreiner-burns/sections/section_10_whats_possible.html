Here's What's Possible for Kreiner Burns LLP

Kreiner Burns has spent years building something real — a multi-state litigation practice with skilled attorneys, a loyal client base, and a referral reputation strong enough to sustain $900,000 in revenue without a single dollar of marketing. The question is not whether this firm has what it takes to reach $2 million. It is whether the systems are in place to get there without Seth, Peter, and Tom having to personally drive every new client relationship. They are not yet. But they can be.

1
Kreiner Burns reaches $2 million in annual revenue — not because the partners worked harder, but because a consistent, predictable flow of commercial litigation and employment cases is coming in through marketing channels that run whether the partners are in the office or not.
2
Tom, Seth, and Peter take a week away and the firm's intake process keeps running — qualified leads are being captured, contacted, and moved toward consultations by a defined system, with no call to any of the founding partners required.
3
The 60–70% revenue concentration from 1–2 clients is replaced by a diversified, predictable client base across commercial litigation, employment, and family law — so the partners can invest confidently in associates, office expansion, and the next phase of growth without living in fear of a catastrophic client departure.

This is not a fantasy. SMB Team can make this a reality for Kreiner Burns LLP.
