Here Is Where Law Office of Steven Ellman Stands Today

Steven Ellman has practiced DUI and DWI defense in New Jersey for 39 years, operating from offices in both Medford and Moorestown with a small support team and an active weekly blog covering all 16 New Jersey counties. The firm has earned strong Google reviews (4.9 stars, 120 reviews) and a Martindale-Hubbell Client Champion Platinum 2026 designation — but with no paid advertising on any channel, every new client depends on organic search and referrals in a market where competitors are actively pulling ahead on every paid channel.

Stage 6 is the destination where cases come to you predictably — so you can focus on what you do best, defend your clients in court, without spending your time hustling for the next matter.

Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Law Office of Steven Ellman
Steven is operating at Stage 4 — a Small Business Manager who has built an impressive track record over nearly four decades but has not yet connected that reputation to a system that generates cases reliably and predictably. The single most urgent gap is lead generation: the firm is entirely dependent on organic search and referrals while direct competitors in Burlington County are investing in paid advertising, Local Service Ads, and social media where Ellman currently has no presence at all. Until paid channels are running and a consistent intake process is in place, the case volume Steven deserves — and has clearly earned — will remain subject to the unpredictability of organic rankings and word of mouth rather than a machine that feeds him cases while he is in court.
