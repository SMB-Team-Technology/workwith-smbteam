Lead Generation

The Law Office of Steven Ellman has built a 39-year reputation in New Jersey DUI defense — and that reputation drives real organic search visibility across 16 counties with active geo-targeting pages and a weekly blog. But with no Google Ads, no Local Service Ads, and no Meta campaigns running on any channel, the firm is competing with one hand tied behind its back while better-resourced competitors claim the top positions in every paid placement on every search.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • PageSpeed scores could not be retrieved via automated testing — a manual test at pagespeed.web.dev is required before optimization; scores below 50 on mobile would indicate critical performance issues affecting both conversion and Google ranking
  • No video content observed on the homepage — attorney video is among the highest-trust conversion elements for DUI prospects deciding whether to call, and its absence leaves credibility on the table
  • Address inconsistency across directories (715 Stokes Rd, Suite 102 on the website vs. 617 Stokes Rd, Suite 108 on BBB and Martindale) creates location confusion for Google's local ranking algorithm and suppresses local pack performance
  • Not listed on FindLaw for Medford NJ DUI searches — absent from a high-traffic legal directory that routes ready-to-hire prospects directly to competing attorneys
Blind Spot: The Avvo rating of 2.6 — driven by disciplinary history in California (2011) and Pennsylvania (2014) and a New Jersey suspension (2013) — is a visible trust risk for the subset of prospects who check Avvo before calling. Competitors with 9.0+ Avvo ratings will capture those fence-sitters. This is a reputation management priority that sits outside normal marketing spend but has a direct impact on conversion.
The Positive
  • Clean, modern website with a clear call-to-action — "REQUEST A FREE CONSULTATION" is visible above the fold with a prominent phone number, reducing friction for prospects who are ready to call
  • Active weekly blog (most recent post May 29, 2026) and geo-targeting pages covering 16 NJ counties and 7 specific cities provide a strong SEO content infrastructure that is already generating organic visibility
The Gap
  • Every directory listing with an incorrect address is sending a conflicting signal to Google, costing the firm local pack rankings that strong review volume and content have already earned
  • The absence of attorney video on the homepage means the firm cannot build trust at the speed competitors with video can — DUI cases are high-urgency, high-emotion decisions where trust is the deciding factor
  • Being absent from FindLaw while competitors are listed means a significant segment of ready-to-hire DUI prospects never encounters the firm at all, routing directly to attorneys with less experience and fewer reviews
Your 39 years of experience and 120 five-star reviews deserve to be visible everywhere a potential DUI client is searching — fixing these gaps makes sure they are.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • NAP inconsistency (street number 715 vs. 617, suite 102 vs. 108) across BBB and Martindale has likely suppressed local 3-pack rankings in Burlington County — the most valuable visibility position in local DWI search
  • The firm does not appear as a dominant local 3-pack result for "DUI lawyer Medford NJ" or "DWI attorney Burlington County NJ" — Christopher L. Baxter and Attorneys Hartman hold those positions despite the Ellman firm having comparable or superior review volume
  • Avvo rating of 2.6 with disciplinary history displayed is a credibility drag for prospects who research attorneys across multiple directories before making a decision
Blind Spot: NAP inconsistency does not just reduce local pack rankings — it can cause Google to filter a firm's listing entirely from 3-pack results even when its overall authority would otherwise qualify. The address discrepancy across BBB and Martindale should be the first technical SEO priority because it has an outsized impact relative to its fix cost.
The Positive
  • 120 Google reviews at 4.9 stars — the strongest review profile in the immediate Medford/Moorestown market and a solid foundation for both local pack improvement and LSA qualification
  • Geo-targeting pages covering 16 counties and 7 cities, combined with consistent weekly blogging, provide an organic content footprint that is already generating page-one visibility for long-tail DWI keywords across New Jersey
The Gap
  • Until the NAP inconsistency is corrected across all major directories, the firm's local pack performance will remain artificially suppressed — this is a solvable technical problem, not a content gap, and fixing it should produce visible results within 30–60 days
  • The gap in local 3-pack visibility for high-intent terms like "DUI attorney Burlington County NJ" means competitors with worse content and fewer reviews are capturing prospects that the Ellman firm has already earned the right to rank for
Law Offices of Christopher L. Baxter — 144 Google reviews at 5.0 stars, a 10.0 Avvo rating, and dominant local 3-pack presence for Burlington County DWI searches from a location 4 miles from Ellman's Moorestown office. Baxter is the benchmark this firm needs to surpass.
Fixing the NAP inconsistency and solidifying local pack presence turns the firm's existing reviews and content investment into a predictable source of inbound calls — without spending a dollar on advertising.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • The firm does not appear to be running Google search ads for DUI/DWI keywords in Burlington County — no ads from stevenellmanlaw.com were observed for "DUI lawyer Burlington County NJ," "DUI attorney Medford NJ," or related terms as of this research
  • For traffic violation keywords including "traffic ticket lawyer Burlington County" and "suspended license lawyer NJ," no ads from this firm were observed — competitors with broader practice profiles are capturing this search traffic from paid positions the firm is not contesting
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • SRIS, P.C. — runs a dedicated Burlington County DUI landing page (srislawyer.com/dui-lawyer-burlington-county/) consistent with active Google Ads targeting for this market, capturing high-intent search traffic at the top of the results page
  • Levow DWI Law — consistent organic and likely paid presence in South Jersey DWI searches, with a statewide brand and advertising infrastructure built to dominate multiple markets simultaneously
  • Attorneys Hartman (Moorestown) — confirmed ad presence in NJ DWI searches, actively capturing Burlington County traffic from paid positions above all organic results
The Opportunity
  • "DUI lawyer Burlington County NJ" and "DWI attorney Medford NJ" — high-intent keywords where prospects are in urgent hiring mode and this firm currently has zero paid presence despite being one of the most experienced DUI attorneys in the market
  • "Traffic ticket lawyer Burlington County" and "suspended license lawyer NJ" — secondary practice area keywords where the firm's existing geo-targeted landing pages would support strong Quality Scores and lower cost-per-click
  • Competitor name keywords (e.g., "Christopher Baxter DWI NJ") — a non-obvious opportunity to capture prospects who are researching competitors but have not yet signed, introducing them to Steven Ellman's 39 years of experience and 80%+ dismissal rate
  • "What happens if I get a DUI in NJ" and urgency-driven research queries — capturing prospects earlier in the decision process, before they have already chosen a competing attorney who was first to appear on their search results
Blind Spot: Competitors running Google Ads are also retargeting everyone who visits their websites — including people who previously visited stevenellmanlaw.com and didn't call. Without running ads, this firm has no way to follow up with that warm audience, and those prospects are being recaptured by competitor retargeting campaigns before they make a decision.
