Website Audit

The Problem
  • Site design appears functional but not modern — estimated 8–12 years old with incremental content updates rather than a full redesign; a dated appearance erodes trust with prospects comparing multiple firms
  • PageSpeed scores: unable to retrieve (site blocks automated crawlers and PageSpeed API was rate-limited during research). Manual check strongly recommended at pagespeed.web.dev before the proposal call — mobile scores are a critical conversion factor
  • No live chat or real-time engagement option observed — prospects landing on the site outside business hours have no immediate path to connect with the firm
  • Multiple phone numbers appear across directory listings — (614) 228-3664, (614) 796-4706, and (614) 332-4456 — creating NAP inconsistency that dilutes local SEO authority
Blind Spot: The firm's 403 block on automated crawlers prevents Google from fully indexing dynamic content — which may be suppressing rankings for pages that exist but cannot be consistently crawled. A technical SEO audit is warranted.
The Positive
  • Geo-targeting landing pages are already in place — confirmed pages for Columbus, Franklin County, West Columbus, and Delaware, Ohio; this is a structural SEO asset most competitors lack
  • Attorney bios and credentials are present at /attorneys/, a key trust signal for family law prospects evaluating multiple firms
  • Active blog with content updated as recently as February 2026, covering divorce, child custody, prenuptials, and high-asset divorce
The Gap
  • An outdated site design signals lower credibility to prospects comparing Lawrence Law Office against Joslyn Law Firm's modern, dedicated family law site or Wood & Long's clean brand — losing those comparisons means losing the case
  • NAP inconsistency across directories weakens the local search authority the firm has spent 30 years building; consolidating to one primary number and address format is a high-ROI fix
  • Without mobile PageSpeed scores confirmed, it's unknown whether the site is converting mobile visitors — who represent the majority of local search traffic — at an acceptable rate
A website that converts confidently — fast, mobile-optimized, and built to turn visitors into booked consultations — means Linda's 30-year reputation starts working for her around the clock, not just when she is available to answer the phone.
