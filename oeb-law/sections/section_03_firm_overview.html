Here Is Where OEB Law Stands Today

But that legacy has not translated into digital visibility: OEB Law does not appear in the local 3-pack or organic first-page results for a single core Personal Injury search in its own city, while newer competitors with a fraction of its history dominate those exact searches. With Managing Partner Tim Elrod difficult to reach due to court commitments and no revenue, case value, or team data yet confirmed, the firm's growth systems have not kept pace with its reputation.

Stage 6 is where OEB Law's decades of brand equity finally convert into a business Tim can step back from — systems doing the work that referrals and legacy recall alone cannot sustain.
