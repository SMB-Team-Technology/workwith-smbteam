Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Cooper & Cooper, P.A.
Cooper & Cooper, P.A. is operating at Stage 4 — a Small Business Manager with real lead generation systems in place (PPC, LSA, and Meta) but still deeply dependent on the owners for both casework and management. The single biggest thing holding this firm back right now is that doubled marketing spend has not produced doubled results: the current agency's lead quality, differentiated strategy, and direct conflict of interest with a competing firm are capping growth right at the moment the firm needs it most. Until intake conversion improves, Charlean is freed from casework into management, and the new Probate division gets a marketing engine of its own, the vacation Anthony and Charlean want by October — and the Florida-wide expansion they envision beyond it — will remain just out of reach.
