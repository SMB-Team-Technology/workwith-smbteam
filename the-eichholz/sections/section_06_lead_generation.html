Today, with advertising reduced or paused as part of a cost-cutting response to declining revenue, the firm's lead generation system is effectively dormant — and competitors are spending actively to fill the void.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • PageSpeed mobile scores for law firm sites with embedded hero video and large imagery commonly fall in the 45–65 range — at these scores Google actively penalizes mobile search rankings. Manual verification at pagespeed.web.dev is required before the proposal call to confirm exact scores for thejusticelawyer.com.
  • The site carries a YouTube-hosted video embed on the homepage — video embeds are one of the most common causes of mobile Core Web Vitals failures, adding multiple seconds to perceived load time for mobile visitors who arrive from a paid ad click.
  • The FindLaw profile shows only 3 reviews at 3.5 stars — significantly underperforming the firm's real Google presence and creating a misleading first impression for prospects who check multiple sources before calling.
  • No live chat is present on the homepage — meaning after-hours visitors who cannot find an instant response channel are leaving without a conversion path.
Blind Spot: Competitors are likely bidding on "Eichholz Law Firm" and "The Justice Lawyer" in Google Ads — capturing brand-intent searches from people who already know the firm by name. Every month without an active brand protection campaign is a month those prospects call a competitor after clicking the wrong ad.
The Positive
  • The site is mobile-responsive with a modern design updated within the last 2–3 years — it does not need a full rebuild, it needs performance optimization
  • Attorney bios are present with photos for David Eichholz, Fatima Zeidan, and Brooke Fitzgerald — a trust signal that many PI competitors skip
  • An extensive network of geo-targeting landing pages exists covering Savannah, Atlanta, and surrounding Georgia and South Carolina coastal communities — this infrastructure has real SEO value if supported by content and backlinks
  • A prominent "Get Your Free Case Evaluation" CTA appears above the fold with an embedded contact form — the conversion path is visible and accessible
The Gap
  • Mobile performance issues mean a significant portion of ad spend — if reactivated — would send paid traffic to a site that loads slowly on phones, directly increasing cost-per-lead and reducing conversion rates
  • The NAP inconsistency (three different phone numbers across directories) suppresses Google's confidence in the listing and limits the firm's organic map pack visibility — a fix that costs nothing but can recover meaningful ranking position
  • The geo-targeting page network is built but not optimized — without fresh content and authoritative backlinks, these pages generate minimal ranking value and represent unrealized SEO infrastructure
Every point of improvement in website conversion means more clients signed from the same marketing spend — and getting back to $13M starts with making sure every ad dollar produces the maximum possible return.
