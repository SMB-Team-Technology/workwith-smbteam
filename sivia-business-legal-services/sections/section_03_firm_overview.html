Here Is Where Sivia Business & Legal Services Stands Today

Todd Sivia founded Sivia Business & Legal Services in 2006 with a vision that went beyond a typical law practice — and two decades later, the firm operates from seven offices across Illinois and Missouri, serving clients in business law, estate planning, elder law, personal injury, and more. But with a 7-location footprint, no observed paid digital advertising, a blog dormant for nine months, and far fewer online reviews than dominant local competitors, the firm's visibility does not yet match the scope of what Todd has built.

Stage 6 is where the firm generates new clients without Todd personally driving every relationship — and where the 20-year investment he has made in building a multi-location practice finally delivers the freedom and scale it was always capable of.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • Multi-location presence across 7 offices in IL and MO — but no observed paid digital channels driving consistent new client flow
  • Blog has been dormant for 9+ months, and reviews on directory platforms are significantly below top local competitors
  • Todd is still the face and primary rainmaker for the firm — business development relies on his personal reputation and referral relationships
  • The 7-location footprint is a genuine competitive differentiator that is not yet matched by online visibility or a systematic lead generation engine

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between where you are and where you want to be is not a question of your reputation or your firm's capacity — it is a question of whether the systems exist to convert that reputation into a predictable, scalable flow of new clients that does not depend on you.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Sivia Business & Legal Services
Sivia Business & Legal Services is operating at Stage 4 — a firm that has done the hard work of building a team and a multi-location infrastructure, but has not yet built the digital marketing systems that would let that infrastructure generate clients independently of Todd's personal involvement. The single most urgent gap is lead generation: with no observed paid advertising presence, a dormant content program, and a fraction of the online reviews held by the market's top competitors, the firm's new client pipeline is largely dependent on referrals and organic discovery — neither of which scales predictably. Until a systematic digital lead generation engine is in place, the firm will continue to grow at the pace of Todd's personal reach rather than the pace of its actual potential.
