Here Is Where JD Law Group Stands Today

JD Law Group is a two-attorney criminal defense and personal injury firm in Roswell, GA that generated $2.5 million in 2025 — but is now in the middle of an 88% case volume collapse caused by a three-domain SEO crisis that has made the firm effectively invisible on Google. The firm's PI-focused website (roswelllegal.com) has never been indexed by Google, the old ChancoSchiffer.com site remains live and actively competing for the same keywords, and the firm does not appear in any local search packs for either criminal defense or personal injury in Roswell. The single most important finding: the systems that built a $2.5M firm no longer exist — and competitors like The Ford Law Firm and Hammers Car Accident & PI Lawyers are pulling further ahead every month the firm remains invisible.

Stage 6 is where the firm runs itself — where Doug gets to spend his time solving the legal puzzles he loves, not managing the business chaos that is consuming him right now.

Overall Growth Stage Assessment
JD Law Group
JD Law Group is operating at Stage 4 — a firm that built significant revenue through a combination of organic search and legal skill, but is now in crisis because the one lead generation system it depended on has been destroyed by a three-domain SEO conflict. The most urgent limiting area is lead generation: with zero paid advertising, zero local pack visibility, and a PI-focused website that Google has never indexed, the firm is generating a fraction of its 2025 case volume. Until the triple-website problem is resolved and a paid marketing engine is rebuilt from the ground up, the $2.5M revenue base Doug worked to build will continue to erode — and the freedom to focus on the actual practice of law will remain out of reach.
