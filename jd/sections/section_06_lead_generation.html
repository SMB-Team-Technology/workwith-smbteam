Lead Generation

JD Law Group built $2.5M in 2025 almost entirely on organic Google search — but that engine has collapsed due to a triple-website crisis that has split domain authority and left the firm invisible in every search that matters. With no paid advertising running, no local pack visibility, and a PI-focused website that has never been indexed by Google, the firm is operating without any functional lead generation system.

Website Audit

"Competitors like The Ford Law Firm rank in Google AI's 'best attorney' answers due to high-quality, in-depth website content, which JD Law Group lacks."
The Problem
  • roswelllegal.com — the firm's PI-focused brand — has zero indexed pages on Google; any PI search in Roswell returns no benefit from this domain whatsoever
  • chancoschiffer.com (old firm name) is still live and fully indexed with 10+ pages, same address, and same phone number — competing directly against jdgroup.law for criminal defense and PI keywords
  • PageSpeed scores: Pending manual verification at pagespeed.web.dev — the site's zero-index status and three-domain authority split strongly suggest technical performance issues
  • Website content is thin across all three domains — no practice area depth pages, no geo-targeted landing pages for Alpharetta, Sandy Springs, or neighboring markets, and no content competing for Google AI citation
  • Contact form on roswelllegal.com is not above the fold — primary contact is phone only, with no after-hours capture mechanism
Blind Spot: Doug's business email (doug@csfirm.com) still uses the old ChancoSchiffer domain. Google reads this as confirmation that the rebrand is incomplete — further reinforcing the authority split between jdgroup.law and chancoschiffer.com. Until the old site is redirected and the email domain is updated, Google will continue treating these as competing entities rather than one authoritative firm.
The Positive
  • jdgroup.law is indexed by Google with multiple pages including recent articles (2025–2026 content) — this domain has existing crawl authority that can be rebuilt upon
  • roswelllegal.com has a modern, mobile-responsive design with a clear PI headline and phone number above the fold — the structure is sound; the problem is technical, not visual
The Gap
  • Every PI prospect who searches Google finds nothing from the firm's PI-branded site — competitors like Hammers and Van Sant Law capture every one of those clicks
  • The content depth gap means JD Law Group does not appear in Google AI's "best attorney" responses — a visibility channel The Ford Law Firm has already claimed for criminal defense in Roswell
  • Without a single authoritative domain, any investment in SEO or paid ads is diluted across three competing properties instead of compounding in one place
Fixing the website foundation is what allows Doug to stop watching leads go to competitors and start seeing the firm's reputation translate into the case flow that lets him focus on the legal work.

This is one of the firm's most powerful credibility assets — and it is currently being attributed to a brand that no longer exists. Hammers has location-specific pages for every market they serve — giving them a geo-authority advantage that grows with each passing month.
Dominating local SEO in Roswell means Doug stops competing for the same case he already won once — and starts receiving a predictable flow of new clients without personally managing every lead source.
