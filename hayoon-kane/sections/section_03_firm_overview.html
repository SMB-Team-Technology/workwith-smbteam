Here Is Where Hayoon Kane Law Firm Stands Today

Hayoon Kane Law Firm is a 5-person immigration practice based in Las Vegas, Nevada, specializing in T-Visa cases for human trafficking victims and serving the Korean-speaking immigrant community — generating approximately $700K annually and operating as a Stage 4 Small Business Manager. The firm has built a genuine competitive moat in the Korean-language immigration niche, with 159 five-star Google reviews and a dominant organic presence for Korean immigration searches in Las Vegas. The single most critical finding is that 100% of the firm's paid lead generation depends on one channel — Korean-language Meta ads at $9,000 per month — while at least four named competitors are actively running Google Ads for immigration searches, and an Arizona-based firm is advertising directly into Las Vegas for the firm's highest-value case type.

Stage 6 is the stage where Hayoon can step back from every consultation, trust her team to run the practice, and have the income to purchase the home in San Francisco she has been working toward — and the capacity to serve the Korean immigrant community at the scale she knows is possible.

Where You Need To Be

The distance between $700K on Meta ads and $1.5M with a full multi-channel system is not as large as it feels — but it requires two things happening in parallel: more high-intent leads coming in, and a Consultation Specialist in place so those leads do not wait for Hayoon.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Hayoon Kane Law Firm
Hayoon Kane Law Firm is operating at Stage 4 — a Small Business Manager who has built one functional lead generation system and assembled a small team, but whose growth is constrained by a single-channel lead pipeline and an owner who is personally required for every consultation. The most urgent gap is not on the marketing side — the Korean-language niche and Meta ads are producing leads. The gap is that those leads are low-intent social scrollers, while the highest-intent searchers (people actively looking for T-Visa lawyers and Korean immigration attorneys on Google) are being captured by competitors every day. Doubling revenue to reach $1.2–$1.5M requires adding Google Ads and LSA to the existing Meta foundation — and adding an internal Consultation Specialist so the lead volume the new channels generate can actually convert.
