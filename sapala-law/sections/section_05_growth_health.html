Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • Zero paid marketing or formal lead generation system in five months of operation — every client relationship today started as a referral
  • No Google Ads, Local Service Ads, or Meta Ads activity observed for the firm, or for either of its two named local competitors
  • Growth so far has come entirely from Brandon's personal network — a channel that caps out at the size of his own relationships
Every hour spent chasing the next referral is an hour not spent building the systems that let you step back into the rainmaker role.

Intake

  • Every prospective client conversation — including the one that led to this engagement — currently runs through Brandon personally
  • No intake team, script, or process exists to qualify or follow up on inbound interest
  • Without a process, any spike in inbound interest becomes another demand on Brandon's own time, not the team's
Fixing intake means converting more of the reputation you've already built into signed engagements — without adding another hour to your week.

Self-Managing Team

  • Brandon is the firm's sole attorney-operator — every deal, client relationship, and dollar collected runs through him alone
  • No accountability structure or delegated ownership exists five months into the firm's life
  • The firm cannot yet absorb a slow stretch, a health issue, or a vacation without growth stalling
Until this changes, you cannot step into the rainmaker role you described — you'll stay the whole operation.

Profit

  • Monthly collections have grown quickly — from $28K in April to $43K in June, with June billings topping $50K
  • No margin, overhead, or profit-percentage figures were shared — revenue is being tracked, but profit is not yet being planned
  • With no coaching relationship or financial plan in place, a growing top line doesn't automatically mean growing take-home pay
Real freedom isn't just more revenue — it's knowing exactly what you keep, so the move from operator to owner is planned, not hoped for.
