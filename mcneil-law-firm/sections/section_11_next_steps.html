After-Hours Lead Capture Process
Intake Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
Family law prospects frequently research and reach out during evenings and weekends — often at emotionally difficult moments when the need to act is highest. With no after-hours coverage and no automated capture mechanism, those prospects are reaching voicemail and calling the next firm on the list before the office opens.
