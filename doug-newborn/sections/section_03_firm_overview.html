Here Is Where Doug Newborn Law Firm, PLLC Stands Today

Doug Newborn Law Firm, PLLC is a $5M two-practice firm in Tucson, Arizona, serving clients in Probate, Trusts, and Estates through a dedicated non-litigation pod, and Personal Injury through Doug and his wife alongside four litigation attorneys and 25 total staff. Despite 850+ five-star Google reviews, a veteran-founded brand story, and an active SMB Team Platinum digital campaign for Probate and Estate Planning, the firm's primary growth constraint is internal — Doug is the escalation point for every operational fire, critical technology projects have stalled for over a year without an owner to drive them, and the PI practice runs on bus wraps and organic posts while competitors spend heavily on every digital PI channel in Tucson.

Stage 6 is not about more revenue — it is about building a firm where every project gets executed, every attorney is accountable, and Doug spends his time on the work he chooses, not on the fires he has to fight.

Where You Are Today

Stage 5: Law Firm CEO
  • SMB Team Platinum campaign active for Probate/EP — but PI has no paid digital presence while competitors dominate every Tucson injury search online
  • 25-person team with defined practice pods, but no KPIs, billable hour targets, or utilization tracking for any of the 7 attorneys
  • A $8–12K Clio Draft project paid and dormant for over a year — no one owns implementation, so it stalls each time Doug's attention moves elsewhere
  • Revenue improved to $5M through stricter AR enforcement, but the firm still lacks a weekly P&L dashboard to manage margins proactively

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between Stage 5 and Stage 6 is not about revenue — it is about building the operational infrastructure that runs the firm when Doug is not in the room, and that is exactly what this roadmap addresses.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Doug Newborn Law Firm, PLLC
Doug Newborn Law Firm operates at Stage 5 — it has the infrastructure of a Law Firm CEO with multiple marketing systems running, a defined team structure across two practice pods, and an active external coaching relationship through Atticus. But it is missing the operational execution layer that would let the owner fully step back: no KPIs, no utilization metrics, and no dedicated COO-level executor means that every strategic initiative — from Clio automation to PI digital expansion — stalls at Doug's bandwidth ceiling. The single most urgent gap is not lead generation (the Platinum campaign is working for Probate) but operational accountability infrastructure — the difference between a firm that grows and one that lets its owner have his time back.
