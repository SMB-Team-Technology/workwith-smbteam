Website Audit

The Problem
  • Contact form is buried at the bottom of the homepage — there is no above-fold form or sticky contact mechanism, meaning mobile visitors who do not scroll past the first screen never encounter a submission option
  • PageSpeed scores: Not retrieved during research (API rate-limited) — manual pull required at pagespeed.web.dev using dougnewbornlawfirm.com before the sales call
  • No geo-targeted landing pages for any surrounding community (Marana, Oro Valley, Sahuarita, Green Valley, Sierra Vista, Vail) despite the firm serving all of Pima County and Southern Arizona
  • NAP inconsistency: two different phone numbers — (520) 355-1161 on the homepage header vs. (520) 585-5525 on BBB and FindLaw — and two different addresses appearing across directory listings, which actively suppresses local search rankings
Blind Spot: The buried contact form is an intake funnel hole that compounds with every marketing dollar spent — the majority of mobile visitors never scroll to the bottom of a homepage, which means a significant portion of after-hours PPC traffic is landing, finding no immediate contact option, and leaving to call a competitor who has a sticky header form visible above the fold.
The Positive
  • Active blog with regular expert content — most recent post June 8, 2026 ("Trust Administration vs. Probate: Which is Faster in Arizona?") demonstrates topical authority and helps SEO rankings
  • Strong unique brand story visible above the fold: Air Force JAG service, Navy submarine officer background, and 850+ five-star review badge — all three are trust signals competitors cannot replicate
  • Mobile-responsive design with a clear "Schedule a Free Consultation" CTA button and attorney video section above the fold
The Gap
  • Every visitor who lands and cannot immediately find a form is a missed intake opportunity — and that gap compounds daily across 850+ reviews worth of online credibility that is already drawing traffic
  • No geo-targeted pages means the firm is not capturing any search volume from the Marana, Oro Valley, Sahuarita, or Green Valley growth corridors, where estate planning and PI searches are increasing as those communities expand
  • NAP inconsistency creates a conflicting citation signal that can cause Google to downrank or suppress local listings — a direct threat to the Platinum campaign's organic amplification
Fixing the website means more of the marketing budget Doug is already spending converts into signed clients — without changing a single ad.
