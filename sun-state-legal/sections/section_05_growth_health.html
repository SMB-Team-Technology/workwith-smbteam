Lead Generation

Intake

  • 2–3 potential client voicemails arrive daily; 4–5 per week merit follow-up, but go unanswered due to bandwidth
  • No triage system, no intake script, no tracking — qualified leads are screened by voicemail transcription and then left in limbo

Every unanswered voicemail is a potential case that went to a competitor — and fixing this costs nothing compared to what those cases are worth.

Self-Managing Team

  • Nate is the entire firm — no full-time staff, no defined roles, no accountability structure
  • Contract paralegal and associate are used sporadically; neither has a defined scope, schedule, or reporting structure
  • There is no version of this firm that runs without Nate — every case, every client, every decision flows through him personally
The legacy and freedom you described — a firm your son could one day inherit — cannot exist until the firm is capable of operating without you at the center of it.

Profit

  • Revenue is growing (~$450k to ~$500k) but there are no KPIs, no matter-level tracking, and no financial visibility beyond top-line revenue
  • Case value is highly variable and untracked — no data on which case types produce the highest return on time invested
  • No formal profit plan exists; Nate operates to meet a personal income floor, not toward a defined financial goal
Growing revenue without knowing where profit lives means you could hit $700k and still feel like you are working for a salary — not building wealth.
