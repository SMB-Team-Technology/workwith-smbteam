Here Is Where Sun State Legal PLLC Stands Today

Nate Hill is a 15-year employment law veteran who left a long-tenured partner role in late 2024 to launch Sun State Legal PLLC — a solo practice serving both employers and employees across the Phoenix metro on workplace discrimination, wrongful termination, wage disputes, and employment agreements. He is generating approximately $500,000 in annual revenue entirely through personal referrals, with no marketing system, no team structure, and no intake process — a firm built entirely on his name, his relationships, and his time. The single most important finding from this audit is that Nate has already demonstrated he can build a highly valuable practice from scratch; the only thing missing is the system to make it run without him.

Stage 6 is the stage where the firm produces revenue and freedom without requiring your constant presence — and building a legacy asset you could one day pass to your son starts exactly there.

Overall Growth Stage Assessment
