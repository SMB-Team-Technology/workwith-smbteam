Set Up an After-Hours Intake Response System
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Employment matters are often urgent — a wrongful termination victim who calls at 7pm and gets voicemail will have called two more attorneys before 9am the next morning. The firm that responds first gets the case; the firm that responds second gets an apology.
The Opportunity
A simple after-hours response system — whether an answering service, an automated acknowledgment with a callback scheduling link, or a text-first protocol — can capture leads that currently go cold between 5pm and 9am without requiring Nate to personally answer the phone.
