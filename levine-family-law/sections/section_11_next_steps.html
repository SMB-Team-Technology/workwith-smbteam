Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
The website "About" page lists 500 E Broward Blvd, Suite 900, while all directory listings — Avvo, Yelp, and the Google Business Profile — show 6550 N Federal Hwy, Suite 220. This Name-Address-Phone inconsistency directly suppresses GBP ranking because Google cannot confidently display a business with conflicting address signals across authoritative citation sources.
The Opportunity
Correcting the website's address to match the GBP address (6550 N Federal Hwy, Suite 220) and auditing all major directories for consistency is a one-time technical fix that removes a ceiling on GBP ranking. Combined with the review-generation program, this fix can produce measurable 3-pack ranking improvements within 60 days.
