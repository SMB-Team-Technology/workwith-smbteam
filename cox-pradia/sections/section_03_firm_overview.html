Here Is Where The Cox Pradia Law Firm Stands Today

The Cox Pradia Law Firm is a 10-person personal injury practice in Houston, Texas, built by partners Jonathan Cox and Troy Pradia, who together bring more than 40 years of combined legal experience handling car accidents, truck accidents, traumatic brain injuries, wrongful death, and a full range of serious injury cases. The firm has a structured operational team — including two intake coordinators, a claims manager, a senior case manager, and an office manager — showing real capacity for growth. The most critical finding: despite this established team and a recognizable Houston brand, the firm does not appear in the local 3-pack for any of its primary search terms and does not appear to be running any paid advertising, leaving it invisible to the high-intent searchers that competitors are capturing every day.

Stage 6 is where Cox and Pradia get to focus on the complex, high-value cases they built this firm to win — while a system generates leads, a trained team closes them, and the numbers finally reflect the impact they have been making for decades.

Overall Growth Stage Assessment
The Cox Pradia Law Firm
The Cox Pradia Law Firm is operating at Stage 4 — a capable, structured practice with a defined team, an established brand, and a proven track record in Houston's personal injury market. The single most urgent gap limiting their growth is the absence of a paid lead generation system: with no observed presence in Google Ads, Local Service Ads, or confirmed Meta campaigns, the firm is leaving every high-intent searcher to be captured by competitors spending hundreds of thousands of dollars per month. Until a predictable lead generation engine is built, the team and the talent inside this firm cannot reach their full potential — and neither can the revenue those talents deserve.
