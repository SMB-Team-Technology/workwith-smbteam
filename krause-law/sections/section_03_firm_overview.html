Here Is Where Krause Law, PLLC Stands Today

Benjamin Krause is a disabled Air Force veteran and VA benefits attorney who has spent more than a decade building one of the most trusted and widely read veterans advocacy platforms in the country — DisabledVeterans.org — while simultaneously practicing law through Krause Law, PLLC in Minneapolis. The firm handles VA and DoD benefit appeals exclusively, with a tight focus on cases where the VA has already said no at least once. The single most important finding from this audit: Benjamin has built extraordinary organic authority and a loyal audience of veterans that most competitors could never replicate — but he has no paid lead generation system to capture the demand he is already generating.

Stage 6 is where Benjamin can step fully into the role he was built for — the most recognized veterans attorney in America, with a law firm that runs without him — and that is exactly where we are taking him.
