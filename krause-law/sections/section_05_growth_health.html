Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • Zero paid advertising — no Google Ads, no LSA, no Meta campaigns — leaving the entire paid search landscape to national competitors
  • DisabledVeterans.org and YouTube channel generate consistent organic traffic but lack a conversion funnel routing viewers directly into the consultation pipeline
  • CCK Law and Berry Law are running aggressive multi-channel paid campaigns nationally, capturing veterans at the exact moment they are ready to hire an attorney
Without a predictable paid lead generation engine, thousands of veterans who need Benjamin's help are being captured by national firms before they ever find Krause Law.

Intake

  • Free case evaluation form exists on the website but no AI-powered follow-up sequence confirmed — leads may wait hours for a response in a national, multi-time-zone practice
  • No dedicated intake specialist confirmed — national practice requires 24/7 intake coverage that a small team cannot provide without AI automation
  • Intake workflow not yet optimized for the specific emotional needs of veterans who have already been denied once and are re-entering the fight
A veteran who inquires and doesn't hear back quickly will call someone else — and at national scale, slow intake means cases lost that could have been won.

Self-Managing Team

  • Director of Operations in place — above average organizational infrastructure for a firm at this revenue stage
  • Benjamin remains the firm's primary brand, content creator, and lead attorney — his personal capacity is still the ceiling on growth
  • AI workforce tools are being explored but not yet systematically deployed — significant efficiency gains available through proper AI implementation
Until content creation, client communication, and case processing are handled by AI-powered systems and a self-managing team, Benjamin's hours will remain the ceiling on how many veterans he can serve.

Profit

  • Cost per acquisition not systematically tracked against marketing channels — organic traffic brings clients but without measurement, optimization is guesswork
  • No confirmed financial reporting framework for tracking case profitability by type, acquisition source, or timeline
  • As paid advertising comes online, ROI tracking infrastructure needs to be in place first — without it, ad spend decisions are made blind
Scaling to help thousands of veterans requires knowing exactly what each case costs to acquire and deliver — that clarity is what turns growth into profit.
