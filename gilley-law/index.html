Stage 6 is the model Chris left his previous firm to eventually build — a practice that generates cases, serves clients, and grows without requiring him to be the last one working every night.

The Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model

We Help Law Firm Owners Get More Profit, More Personal Freedom, And Outstanding Results For Their Clients.

Most law firms try to grow by chasing more leads. But leads alone are not the problem — and leads alone are not the answer. Here is what actually works.

Lead Generation

Leads are to a law firm what gasoline is to a car. Without them, you go nowhere. But a steady, predictable flow of leads changes everything — you get to choose your cases instead of taking whatever comes in.

Intake

But all the leads in the world are worthless without a system to turn them into paying clients. Most firms are losing cases they already paid for simply because their follow-up is slow or inconsistent.

Self-Managing Team

Once clients are coming in, you need a team — otherwise you end up working 90-hour weeks and results suffer. A self-managing team is what gives you the freedom to step back.

Profit Plan

And once the team is in place, most owners look at their bank account and ask: where did all the money go? That is what a profit plan solves — clarity on what you earn, what you keep, and how to grow both.

All four have to work together. If one is missing, you cannot have more profit, more freedom, and outstanding results for your clients at the same time.

Intake Assessment

Gilley Law launched with Chris as the only person handling every intake inquiry — every call, every form submission, and every follow-up depends entirely on his personal availability, and as LSA volume grows that bottleneck will only get more expensive. Fixing intake means converting more of the leads the firm is already generating — without spending another dollar on marketing.

Status: RED — Critical Priority
The Problem
  • Chris personally handles every intake call — when he is in court, on a case, or otherwise unavailable, there is no one to answer and no system to respond

  • No defined follow-up sequence for leads who contact the firm but do not immediately sign — prospects who need time to decide have no reason to choose Gilley Law over a competitor who follows up faster

  • Free 60-minute consultation offer is prominently displayed, lowering the barrier for first contact and increasing the likelihood that leads take action
The Gap
  • Every missed call represents $2,000–$2,500 in potential case revenue that was already earned through paid advertising and then surrendered to a competitor who answered the phone
  • Without intake tracking, there is no visibility into the conversion rate of LSA leads — the firm cannot know how many leads are being won versus lost, or why
  • As paid advertising scales, the intake bottleneck will prevent the marketing investment from reaching its full return — more leads without a better intake process just means more lost leads
