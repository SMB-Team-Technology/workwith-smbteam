Here Is Where Gilley Law Stands Today

Chris Gilley launched Gilley Law in Anderson, Indiana in May 2026 — a solo criminal defense and family law practice built on 20 years of legal experience, 8 years as a criminal prosecutor, and 100+ jury trials. In just 2.5 weeks the firm generated $60,000 in revenue and began generating cases through Google Local Service Ads — but the most critical finding is this: the entire operation runs through Chris personally, and the website that exists today is a landing page with no organic search capability, no intake team, and no system that works when he is not in the room.

Stage 6 is the model Chris left his previous firm to eventually build — a practice that generates cases, serves clients, and grows without requiring him to be the last one working every night.

Where You Are Today

Stage 3: Solo Practitioner
  • Solo attorney handling all casework, intake, and business operations personally
  • LSAs generating early traction at $6,000/month, but website is a landing page with no organic search capability
  • All revenue depends entirely on Chris's personal availability — there is no one to delegate to
  • Word-of-mouth drove $60,000 in the first 2.5 weeks — no system exists to replicate that predictably

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between where Gilley Law is today and the firm Chris left his previous job to build is not as wide as it feels — but closing it requires the right systems, and that is exactly what this roadmap lays out.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Gilley Law
