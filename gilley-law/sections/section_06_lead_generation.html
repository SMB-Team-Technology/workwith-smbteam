"A basic landing page, not built for SEO."
The Problem

  • PageSpeed scores: Mobile and Desktop scores could not be retrieved automatically — manual verification required at pagespeed.web.dev/?url=https://gilley.law before the audit is presented

  • No geo-targeting pages for surrounding markets (Muncie, Noblesville, Fishers, Indianapolis) despite the firm explicitly serving a 5-county region
Blind Spot: The current website cannot rank for a single keyword searched by someone looking for a criminal defense or family law attorney in Anderson — every visitor today is either clicking a paid ad or searching specifically for Gilley Law by name. Organic search does not exist for this firm yet.
The Positive
  • Strong attorney bio above the fold — 20 years of experience, 8 years as a criminal prosecutor, and 100+ jury trials establish credibility immediately
  • Clear "Request a Consultation" CTA appears twice above the fold with anchor links that drive visitors directly to the contact form
The Gap
  • Without SEO content, Gilley Law cannot capture organic search traffic — the vast majority of legal service searches never reach paid results, and 100% of that traffic currently goes to competitors
  • No city-specific landing pages means the 5-county service area (Madison, Delaware, Hamilton, Hancock, and Henry counties) is entirely unaddressed in search
  • Every month without an SEO-optimized site is a month Zaki Ali Trial Lawyers, Daniel K. Whitehead, and Adler Attorneys extend their organic advantage further
A new website built for SEO is not a cost — it is the infrastructure that generates leads while Chris is in the courtroom, not at his desk.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • Gilley Law does not appear in the local 3-pack for any criminal defense or family law keyword in Anderson, Indiana — confirmed across six separate searches including "criminal defense attorney Anderson Indiana," "DUI attorney Anderson," "divorce attorney Anderson IN," and "child custody attorney Anderson Indiana"

  • GBP shows approximately 20 reviews as the new Gilley Law entity — the dominant competitor, Zaki Ali Trial Lawyers, holds 462 reviews at 4.9★, creating a trust gap that will take a systematic review strategy to close
Blind Spot: The residual online presence of "Gilley, Dandurand & Summerfield Law Group LLP" in Anderson-area searches creates active brand confusion — potential clients searching for Chris may find the old firm first and assume he is still associated with it, or that he is no longer practicing independently.
The Positive
  • Starting a new firm GBP with 39 reviews at 4.8 stars — carried over from previous roles — is a significantly stronger baseline than most new practices launch with, and it is already generating LSA activity
The Gap
  • Each directory inconsistency is a trust signal penalty to Google's local algorithm — fixing all seven NAP errors is the fastest, lowest-cost lever to improve 3-pack ranking potential
  • Every month without a local SEO content strategy is a month competitors extend their 3-pack dominance — organic 3-pack placement drives the highest-intent, lowest-cost-per-lead traffic available to a local law firm
Zaki Ali Trial Lawyers — 462 Google reviews at 4.9★, appearing at the top of the local 3-pack for every major criminal defense keyword in Anderson. This is the benchmark to close toward — and the gap widens every month without a systematic review and local SEO strategy.
Owning the local 3-pack means new clients find Gilley Law first — without Chris making a single call or attending a single event.
