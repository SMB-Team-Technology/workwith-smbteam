Here Is Where The Law Offices of David P. Shapiro Stands Today

David P. Shapiro has spent 16 years building one of San Diego's most decorated criminal defense practices — two locations, seven team members, 12 consecutive Super Lawyers designations, an AVVO 10.0 rating, and radio presence on the Padres broadcasts. The firm has built extraordinary credibility — but lacks the scalable system to translate that credibility into a predictable, measurable flow of new clients.

Stage 6 is where a firm like this one stops being built on the owner's personal reputation alone and starts running as a market-dominating institution — and that is exactly where David P. Shapiro belongs.
