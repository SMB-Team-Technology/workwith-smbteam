  • PageSpeed scores: Could not be determined — the TLS certificate error blocks the PageSpeed API from accessing the site. Given the site's apparent age, AOL email contact, and broken TLS cert, mobile performance is expected to be poor. A manual test at pagespeed.web.dev is required before the proposal call.
  • Website design and infrastructure appear dated — an AOL contact email (dshapirolaw@aol.com), broken HTTPS, and the inability to confirm basic features like mobile responsiveness or a contact form suggest the site has not been meaningfully updated in years
  • No confirmed contact form, no above-the-fold CTA, and no after-hours intake path — digital leads arriving at any hour have no way to submit an inquiry and expect a response
Blind Spot: The TLS certificate issue is not just a technical annoyance — it is an active conversion killer affecting every single prospect who receives this firm's web address from a referral source, a directory listing, or a Google search result and tries to visit the site. Some browsers block HTTPS-broken sites entirely. Every warm referral that bounces from this warning is a case that cost nothing to earn and everything to lose. This issue costs under $100/year to fix.
The Positive
  • The firm has a confirmed Google-indexed web presence with attorney profile pages and a blog — foundational SEO infrastructure exists and could be significantly improved with a modern rebuild
  • David B. Shapiro has a notable professional biography — former Maryland House of Delegates member, argued a case before the U.S. Supreme Court, and 37+ years of practice — credentials that a rebuilt, well-designed website could leverage powerfully
The Gap
  • Every referred prospect who tries to visit the website encounters a security warning — the most credible referral in the world can be undone by a browser warning that looks like the firm is compromised
  • Without mobile optimization, fast load times, and a clear above-the-fold conversion path, any traffic driven by future paid advertising will convert at a fraction of its potential
  • Competitors with modern websites and clear conversion funnels are capturing digital prospects that this firm earned through reputation but cannot collect due to technical failure
A working website is the foundation every other marketing investment depends on — without it, any money spent on ads or SEO is driving traffic through a locked door.
