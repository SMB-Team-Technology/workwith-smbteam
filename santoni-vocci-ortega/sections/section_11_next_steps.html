Quick Wins for Santoni, Vocci & Ortega LLC

Launch Google Local Service Ads
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
LSAs appear above paid ads, above the map pack, and above all organic results on Google — and the firm pays only when a verified prospect calls directly. With 102 Google reviews already on file, SVO qualifies for enrollment now and is leaving the most prominent position on the page completely empty.
Who Is Doing It Better
Leppler Injury Law — 407 Google reviews at 5.0 stars positions them as a dominant LSA candidate, capturing the top-of-page position for PI searches before organic results even begin.
The Opportunity
Enrolling in LSA for personal injury and consumer rights puts the firm's name, phone number, and review rating at the very top of every relevant Google search — a position SVO currently does not hold on any platform, for any practice area.
Fix NAP Inconsistency Across Directories
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
Two different addresses appear across directories — the Lutherville-Timonium address on Yelp, FindLaw, and Birdeye; the Towson address on BBB and Martindale. Google uses NAP consistency as a local ranking signal, and when two addresses compete for the same entity, both profiles lose authority.
The Opportunity
Correcting NAP across all directories is a one-time audit that improves organic local rankings for both office locations at no ongoing cost — directly increasing visibility for personal injury and consumer rights searches without a single dollar of ad spend.
Launch Paid Search for Consumer Rights and Lemon Law
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
Consumer rights, lemon law, FDCPA, and debt collection violation terms have lower keyword competition and lower cost-per-lead than personal injury — making them the most cost-effective entry point for paid search for a firm with SVO's specific practice area depth.
Who Is Doing It Better
National Lemon Law Center — a national advertiser actively bidding on lemon law and auto fraud terms in the Baltimore market, capturing leads that SVO's local expertise is better positioned to convert.
The Opportunity
The firm can establish first-mover paid dominance in consumer rights and lemon law search terms before local competitors activate — capturing a niche where SVO's documented credentials give it a conversion advantage over national generalist advertisers.
Implement After-Hours Lead Response Protocol
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Most personal injury and consumer rights prospects contact more than one firm before signing. Speed of response is the primary driver of who gets the case — and any inquiry received outside business hours with no immediate acknowledgment is a lead that signs with a competitor before the firm even knows they called.
The Opportunity
An automated acknowledgment for after-hours form submissions — including next steps and a clear promise to call within one business hour — increases conversion on the organic leads the firm already generates without any additional marketing spend.
Build a Case Acquisition Cost Dashboard
Profit Quick Win
Why It Matters
Without tracking what it costs to generate and sign each case by practice area, marketing investment decisions are made without ROI data — making it impossible to know which channels are profitable, which practice areas generate the best return per partner hour, and when to scale versus when to stop.
The Opportunity
Establishing a simple case acquisition cost framework before scaling ad spend means every marketing decision from day one is measurable — the firm can see which campaigns pay for themselves and which do not, rather than discovering that months after the spend.
Build City-Specific Landing Pages for the Baltimore Metro Area
Lead Gen Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
The firm serves Baltimore City, Towson, Lutherville-Timonium, and surrounding communities but has only two confirmed geo-targeted pages — meaning it captures almost none of the location-modified search volume from the broader market it serves every day.
Who Is Doing It Better
Portner & Shure, P.A. — multi-location firm with documented geo-targeted SEO strategy, including dedicated Towson landing pages that capture location-specific search traffic SVO currently loses.
The Opportunity
Each new city-specific landing page for Baltimore, Pikesville, Cockeysville, Hunt Valley, and Catonsville captures a new pool of geo-targeted organic traffic — and gives paid campaigns a high-relevance destination that increases Quality Score and reduces cost-per-click.
Define and Fill a Dedicated Intake Coordinator Role
Team Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
As paid advertising brings in more leads, the absence of a dedicated intake person creates a bottleneck where partner time is consumed by initial consultations and follow-up calls that a trained team member could handle — capping the firm's conversion rate at what three busy partners can personally manage.
The Opportunity
A dedicated intake coordinator — with a defined script, follow-up sequence, and conversion KPIs — protects the return on every dollar of ad spend by ensuring no lead falls through the cracks, regardless of what the partners are working on at the time a call comes in.
Accelerate Google Review Acquisition
Lead Gen Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
The 305-review gap between SVO (102 reviews) and Leppler Injury Law (407 reviews at 5.0 stars) is the primary structural reason the firm does not appear in the PI local 3-pack — where the highest-intent searches produce clicks that close at dramatically higher rates than any other channel.
Who Is Doing It Better
Leppler Injury Law — 407 reviews at a perfect 5.0 rating gives them structural dominance in the local 3-pack for personal injury searches that SVO cannot currently challenge without closing the review gap.
The Opportunity
A structured post-case review request process — deployed consistently after every settled PI and consumer rights case — can generate 3–5 new reviews per month. At that pace, the firm closes the gap significantly within 18–24 months while also improving its standing for LSA and organic rankings.
