Intake

  • 40–73% no-show rate over four consecutive months — for every 10 consultations scheduled, as few as 3 actually occur

Self-Managing Team

  • Team of five with no accountability structure, no KPIs, and no defined management layer — David is the de facto operations manager and sole attorney simultaneously

A self-managing team is what lets David practice law instead of managing people — and it is the prerequisite for reaching the $2M revenue goal before 2027.

Profit

  • $100,000+ in outstanding AR with approximately $50,000 collectible — no systematic collections workflow or retainer replenishment process is in place
  • Owner is underpaying himself despite growing revenue — cash cushion is drained, creating financial stress that affects hiring and investment decisions

Fixing profit visibility is how David stops discovering what he made at the end of the quarter and starts deciding — and finally pays himself what this firm has earned.
