Here Is Where Law Offices of David Bliven Stands Today

David Bliven has built a thriving family law practice in Westchester County and the Bronx, handling divorce, child custody, child support, and related family matters across two office locations with a team of five — and is already running a full-service marketing program through SMB Team. Stage 6 is the stage where the firm runs without David as the firefighter — where intake systems convert consultations, the team operates against clear KPIs, and he finally pays himself what this firm has earned. That is exactly where this roadmap is taking him.
