Consultation Reminder & Confirmation Sequence
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
A 40–73% no-show rate over four consecutive months is almost always a follow-up gap. When prospects schedule a consultation and receive no automated confirmation or reminder in the 24–48 hours before, they forget — and book with whoever reaches them next. Clio Grow supports automated reminder sequences natively; if this is not configured, it is a same-week fix.
The Opportunity
A properly configured reminder sequence — 48 hours before, 24 hours before, and a morning-of confirmation — could recover 20–30% of current no-shows within 30 days. At a $25,000 average case value, each recovered consultation is worth thousands in revenue that is already being lost.
Intake KPI Dashboard
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Without measurable targets for show rate, response time, and lead-to-client conversion, the intake team has no feedback loop — and no way to detect when performance is declining. The September-to-December trend shows a steady drop from 44% to 27% show rate with no apparent intervention. You cannot fix what you are not tracking.
The Opportunity
Establishing three core intake KPIs — weekly show rate target, response time SLA, and monthly conversion rate — gives David visibility into intake performance without personally managing every interaction. When a metric drops below target, the system flags it before a month of lost revenue passes.
Paralegal Utilization System
Team Quick Win
Why It Matters
Two senior paralegals billing only ~10 hours per week at $300/hr each are generating approximately $26,000/month in billings against combined salaries of over $200,000/year. At current utilization rates, they are cost centers — not revenue drivers. This is the single largest immediately recoverable profit gap in the firm.
The Opportunity
