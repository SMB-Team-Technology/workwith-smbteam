Here Is Where Mandall Immigration Law Stands Today

Mandall Immigration Law is a 22-month-old Canadian immigration firm founded by Austin Mandall Rooplall, operating out of Toronto and Ottawa and already running at a roughly $2M annual revenue pace — built almost entirely on one Google Ads account plus organic search. But the firm is still operating as a Small Business Manager: Austin personally handles payment collection and file opening, sales are tracked by hand in Apple Notes, and the one paid channel driving growth swings wildly week to week ($37K one week, $111K the next) with zero performance reporting from the agency running it.

Stage 6 is where Austin trades payment collection and file-opening for the freedom to travel and focus on strategy — and where the firm keeps growing without needing him in the room.

Where You Are Today

STAGE 4: SMALL BUSINESS MANAGER
  • Google Ads and organic search generate roughly 600 leads a month and a ~$2M revenue run rate in under two years — but results swing wildly week to week with no performance reporting from the agency running the account
  • Austin personally handles payment collection and file opening — low-value admin work that competes directly with the time he needs for strategy and growth
  • Sales are tracked manually in Apple Notes, and files take 75–90 days to complete versus an ideal 2–3 weeks, delaying the second retainer payment and creating a real cash flow drag
  • The firm has geo-targeted landing pages and a 5-star reputation, but has not yet built the review volume, diversified ad channels, or reporting infrastructure that its established Toronto competitors have had years to compound

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
Closing the gap between where Mandall stands today and the $100M/year vision Austin has for the firm starts with fixing the two things quietly capping growth right now: an unreliable paid lead engine and an owner still doing the job of an admin assistant.

The Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model

We Help Law Firm Owners Get More Profit, More Personal Freedom, And Outstanding Results For Their Clients.

Most law firms try to grow by chasing more leads. But leads alone are not the problem — and leads alone are not the answer. Here is what actually works.

Lead Generation

Leads are to a law firm what gasoline is to a car. Without them, you go nowhere. But a steady, predictable flow of leads changes everything — you get to choose your cases instead of taking whatever comes in.

Intake

But all the leads in the world are worthless without a system to turn them into paying clients. Most firms are losing cases they already paid for simply because their follow-up is slow or inconsistent.

Self-Managing Team

Once clients are coming in, you need a team — otherwise you end up working 90-hour weeks and results suffer. A self-managing team is what gives you the freedom to step back.

Profit Plan

And once the team is in place, most owners look at their bank account and ask: where did all the money go? That is what a profit plan solves — clarity on what you earn, what you keep, and how to grow both.

All four have to work together. If one is missing, you cannot have more profit, more freedom, and outstanding results for your clients at the same time.
