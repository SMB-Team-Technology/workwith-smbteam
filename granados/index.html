Here Is Where Granados Law Group Stands Today

Granados Law Group, PLLC is a Cary, NC criminal defense and DWI firm founded by Lindsey Granados, who rebuilt the practice after losing her founding partner when he was elected to Congress in 2023. With a team of six, $750,000 in annual revenue, and active Google Ads and LSA campaigns in the Wake County market, the firm has recovered — but Lindsey works 11–12 hours per day and has no system that allows the firm to run without her.

Stage 6 is the stage where you take your two-week vacation and come back to find the firm ran itself — where your income is no longer tied to how many hours you personally put in, and where college and retirement are funded by a business, not by your calendar.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • Google Ads and LSAs are running but pause at night — missing the high-value DWI arrests and criminal emergencies that happen on Friday and Saturday evenings
  • Lindsey handles all high-value felony intake personally, making her the bottleneck for the firm's most important cases
  • No after-hours answering service — evening and weekend calls go unanswered at the exact hours when clients need help most
  • No financial forecasting or revenue planning — the full extent of financial visibility is "we're in the black"

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between where Granados Law Group stands today and where it needs to be is real — but it is entirely closeable with a structured plan, and that is exactly what this report lays out.

The Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model

We Help Law Firm Owners Get More Profit, More Personal Freedom, And Outstanding Results For Their Clients.

Most law firms try to grow by chasing more leads. But leads alone are not the problem — and leads alone are not the answer. Here is what actually works.

Lead Generation

Leads are to a law firm what gasoline is to a car. Without them, you go nowhere. But a steady, predictable flow of leads changes everything — you get to choose your cases instead of taking whatever comes in.

Intake

But all the leads in the world are worthless without a system to turn them into paying clients. Most firms are losing cases they already paid for simply because their follow-up is slow or inconsistent.

Self-Managing Team

Once clients are coming in, you need a team — otherwise you end up working 90-hour weeks and results suffer. A self-managing team is what gives you the freedom to step back.

Profit Plan

And once the team is in place, most owners look at their bank account and ask: where did all the money go? That is what a profit plan solves — clarity on what you earn, what you keep, and how to grow both.

All four have to work together. If one is missing, you cannot have more profit, more freedom, and outstanding results for your clients at the same time.
