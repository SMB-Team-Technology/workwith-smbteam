Here Is Where Polchinski & Smith LLP Stands Today

Polchinski & Smith LLP is an Oklahoma City personal injury firm built by partners Ryan Polchinski, Andrew Polchinski, and Houston Smith, handling car accidents, truck accidents, workers' compensation, and other injury cases out of a single office. In roughly a year the firm has grown from $200K to a $1M+ revenue pace — but the partners still personally handle every intake call and every settlement themselves. That single bottleneck, not a shortage of demand, is the biggest thing standing between this firm and its next stage of growth.

Stage 6 is the stage where the partners stop being the firm's "Chief Everything Officers" and start running a business that runs without them — and it is exactly where this roadmap is built to take you.

Where You Are Today

STAGE 4: SMALL BUSINESS MANAGER
  • Revenue has scaled from $200K to a $1M+ pace, but the systems have not caught up to the growth
  • The three partners personally handle all intake and every settlement — no delegation exists
  • The website ranks organically for core personal injury and car accident searches, but has zero visibility for truck accidents or workers' compensation — the practice areas the firm markets but does not rank for
  • No paid advertising of any kind was found running — Google Ads, Local Service Ads, or Meta

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
You have already proven the demand is there — the firm 5x'd its revenue in a year. The gap between where you are and where you want to be is not a demand problem, it is a systems and delegation problem, and that is exactly what this plan solves.

The Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model

We Help Law Firm Owners Get More Profit, More Personal Freedom, And Outstanding Results For Their Clients.

Most law firms try to grow by chasing more leads. But leads alone are not the problem — and leads alone are not the answer. Here is what actually works.

Lead Generation

Leads are to a law firm what gasoline is to a car. Without them, you go nowhere. But a steady, predictable flow of leads changes everything — you get to choose your cases instead of taking whatever comes in.

Intake

But all the leads in the world are worthless without a system to turn them into paying clients. Most firms are losing cases they already paid for simply because their follow-up is slow or inconsistent.

Self-Managing Team

Once clients are coming in, you need a team — otherwise you end up working 90-hour weeks and results suffer. A self-managing team is what gives you the freedom to step back.

Profit Plan

And once the team is in place, most owners look at their bank account and ask: where did all the money go? That is what a profit plan solves — clarity on what you earn, what you keep, and how to grow both.

All four have to work together. If one is missing, you cannot have more profit, more freedom, and outstanding results for your clients at the same time.
