Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • Ranks organically for core personal injury and car accident searches, but has zero visibility for truck accident or workers' compensation searches — two of the firm's own practice areas
  • No paid advertising observed on Google Ads, Local Service Ads, or Meta — every paid channel is currently open ground for competitors
  • 47 Google reviews at 4.8 stars is a strong foundation, but far behind the leading named competitors in this market
Closing the visibility gap in truck accidents and workers' comp means capturing revenue you are already licensed to earn.

Intake

  • All intake calls and every settlement run through the three partners personally — no delegation exists
  • The partners describe themselves as the firm's "Chief Everything Officers," which they say is unsustainable
  • This bottleneck is already stalling case progression today, before a single new marketing lead is added
Every hour the partners spend on intake is an hour they are not getting back — and freeing that time is the entire point of this plan.

Self-Managing Team

  • 7 total staff including the partners — a real team exists, but the partners still personally manage all intake and settlements
  • No dedicated ops, marketing, or intake lead was identified — the partners remain the operational center of the firm
  • The stated 12-month goal of hiring another attorney and automating procedures has not yet started
Until someone besides the partners can run intake and settlements, adding another attorney creates more work to manage — not more free time.

Profit

  • Revenue has grown roughly 5x in a year — from $200K to a $1M+ pace — a strong signal of demand
  • No profit, margin, or acquisition-cost visibility was discussed on the call
  • Rapid revenue growth without financial systems in place is often where profit gets lost, not found
Growth without a profit plan means bigger revenue on the same number of hours — not the freedom you are actually working toward.
