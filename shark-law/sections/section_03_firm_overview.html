Here Is Where Shark Law Stands Today

Shane Herzner has spent 22 years building one of Cincinnati's most trial-active DUI and OVI defense practices — backing a brand (The Shark) that clients remember, a track record that speaks for itself (5 jury trials and 4 wins in 2026 alone), and a firm pacing toward $800K this year with a clear goal of breaking $1M. The single most important finding from this audit is that a dominant competitor is capturing approximately 96% of the Google Maps searches that Shark Law should own — and while Shane's results are exceptional, almost no one searching for a DUI attorney in Cincinnati is being given the chance to find that out.

Stage 6 is where you stop doing every intake, stop taking every case, and start running a firm that wins by design — which is exactly what this plan is built to get you to.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • Referrals and LSA are the primary consistent lead sources — no predictable multi-channel pipeline
  • Shane personally handles every intake call and every consultation close — the firm's revenue is tied to his time
  • Shark Law appears in roughly 11% of Google Maps searches for "DUI attorney Cincinnati" while the dominant competitor appears in ~96%
  • Revenue is growing ($700K → $800K pacing) but no clear visibility into cost per lead, cost per signed case, or which channels drive the best margins

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between where you are and where you want to be is not about your results — your results are already exceptional. It is about the systems that make those results visible, replicable, and independent of you being the one to answer every call.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Shark Law
Shane is operating at Stage 4 — a Small Business Manager who has built a real team, a real brand, and a real track record, but whose growth is still bottlenecked by two critical gaps: lead generation that depends too heavily on referrals and a single competitor dominating local search, and an intake process where Shane himself is the closer on every case. The single most urgent limiting factor is local search invisibility — Suhre & Associates has nearly four times as many Google reviews and appears in the local map pack for searches that should belong to Shark Law. Until that changes, Shane will keep winning the cases he gets but never get the volume of cases that match his capacity to win.
