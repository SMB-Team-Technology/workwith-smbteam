Lead Generation

Gunn & Gunn P.C. has built nearly 90 years of combined legal reputation in Salem — but that reputation is not visible where today's criminal defense clients are looking. The firm has an existing marketing provider that generates some leads, but there are critical gaps in paid digital advertising, local search visibility, and review velocity that are actively costing the firm new clients every month.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • The website (gunnlawfirm.com) blocks search engine crawlers and automated tools — a technical configuration that limits how well Google can index and rank the site's content
  • Blog last updated March 2025 — over 15 months without fresh content signals an inactive site to both Google and prospective clients who visit looking for recent activity
  • The firm operates at least four separate web properties (gunnlawfirm.com, salemdui.com, gunndefense.com, salemcriminalattorney.com) — without a unified strategy, these micro-sites can split authority and create NAP inconsistencies that hurt local rankings
  • No visible contact form above the fold or live chat capability observed — prospects who arrive after-hours or on weekends have no immediate way to connect
Blind Spot: The multiple micro-sites Gunn & Gunn has built over the years (salemdui.com, gunndefense.com, salemcriminalattorney.com) may feel like a marketing advantage — but without a coordinated SEO strategy linking them, they are more likely diluting the firm's authority than multiplying it. A competitor with one focused, well-optimized site will often outrank three unfocused ones.
The Positive
  • Dedicated practice area pages exist for criminal defense, DUI/DUII, and bankruptcy — this is a strong foundation for SEO when properly optimized
  • Separate attorney bio pages for all three attorneys demonstrate credibility and personal connection that many competitor sites lack
The Gap
  • A website that blocks crawlers and has 15 months of stale content cannot rank competitively — every month without optimization is a month more leads go to Gilroy Napoli Short or Reynolds Defense instead
  • The absence of above-fold conversion tools (chat, immediate call-to-action form) means site visitors who are ready to hire right now are leaving without connecting
  • Criminal defense clients often make contact decisions within minutes of their arrest or charge — a website that cannot be found or does not capture urgently is invisible at the moment it matters most
A high-converting, fully optimized website is how Gunn & Gunn's 40-year reputation translates into the revenue that lets Marcus triple his income and stop being the firm's only salesperson.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • Gunn & Gunn has 69 reviews at 4.5 stars — while Gilroy Napoli Short has 191 reviews and Reynolds Defense Firm has 189 Google reviews. In local pack ranking, review count is one of the most heavily weighted factors, and this gap is costing the firm visibility
  • The firm does not consistently appear in the top 3-pack for high-intent searches like "criminal defense attorney Salem Oregon" or "DUI attorney Salem" — the spots that drive the most organic client calls are held by competitors
  • Multiple micro-sites (salemdui.com, gunndefense.com, salemcriminalattorney.com) create the risk of NAP inconsistencies across directories, which suppresses local rankings
Blind Spot: Gunn & Gunn has been in Salem for over 40 years — which means there may be dozens of outdated directory listings with old addresses, old phone numbers, or inconsistent firm name formats across Avvo, Yelp, Justia, and FindLaw. Every inconsistency is a signal to Google that the firm's data is unreliable, and that suppresses rankings directly.
The Positive
  • 69 reviews at 4.5 stars is a real foundation — the firm is not starting from zero. With a systematic review generation campaign, closing the gap on competitors is achievable within 6–12 months
The Gap
  • A 122-review gap vs. Gilroy Napoli Short means the firm is effectively invisible in the local pack for the searches that criminal defense clients run immediately after being charged
  • Without regular Google Business Profile posts, photos, and Q&A activity, the firm signals to Google that it is less active and relevant than competitors who maintain their profiles weekly
Gilroy Napoli Short Law Group — 191 reviews at 4.7 stars across five offices, with a dedicated Salem criminal defense and DUI landing page. They have built a regional brand that captures Salem clients even though their headquarters is in Portland, using exactly the kind of multi-location SEO strategy that Gunn & Gunn's deep local roots could replicate — and exceed — with the right system in place.
Closing the review gap and claiming the local pack means the firm's phone rings consistently — which is the foundation Marcus needs to hire the next attorney and step back from the day-to-day.

The Opportunity

Local Service Ads

LSA Presence
  • Gunn & Gunn does not appear to be running Local Service Ads for criminal defense or DUI searches in Salem — this is a significant visibility gap at the very top of Google search results
  • The firm is not observed to be Google Screened, which is required to appear in LSA and adds a verified trust signal that converts browsers into callers at a higher rate
Competitor LSA Landscape
  • Reynolds Defense Firm — appears in local pack and paid results for DUI searches in Salem; likely LSA-eligible given their review count and Google Screened status
  • Gilroy Napoli Short — statewide presence and review volume positions them for LSA dominance in every Oregon market they serve, including Salem
The Opportunity
  • LSAs appear above everything else on Google — above paid ads, above the map pack, above all organic results — and you only pay when a real prospect calls you directly
  • With 69 reviews already on Google, Gunn & Gunn has the review foundation needed to apply for Google Screened status and enter the LSA program — this is a near-term win that could deliver calls within weeks of launching
  • Criminal defense and DUI are high-urgency practice areas — clients searching at 10pm after an arrest are clicking the very first result they see, which is LSA. Being absent from that position means losing the most motivated leads in the market
