Lead Generation

  • Google Ads were paused six months ago and have not been reactivated, cutting off what was previously the firm's most direct lead channel

Every month without an active lead system is a month further from the stable, predictable revenue Larry needs before he can expand.

Intake

  • Larry personally evaluates every incoming case himself, with no dedicated intake staff to help triage volume
  • The pre-intake evaluation process was named directly on the call as the firm's single biggest operational bottleneck
  • Lead flow is already described as "inconsistent" — and with no intake system in place, any new leads generated risk sitting un-triaged
The workflow automation Larry wants to build starts here — with a pre-intake process that runs without consuming his personal time.

Self-Managing Team

  • No additional attorneys, paralegals, or intake staff were discussed on the call — Larry is effectively a firm of one
  • Every case evaluation, from first contact through the complex med-mal and abuse cases the firm specializes in, currently runs through Larry personally
  • The stated plan is to fix workflows and technology before hiring — meaning today's bottlenecks would only multiply across new hires without a system in place first
Until a self-managing structure exists, adding headcount will not give Larry the time back he is looking for — it will just spread today's bottleneck across more people.

Profit

  • Annual revenue is $1M+, but cash flow is significantly volatile due to the long resolution timelines on complex, high-value cases
  • No profitability figures by practice area were discussed on the call
  • Average case value was described only in range terms ($400k+) rather than as a tracked, predictable number
Stabilizing revenue — the outcome Larry named directly on the call — starts with knowing these numbers as clearly as he knows his case outcomes.
