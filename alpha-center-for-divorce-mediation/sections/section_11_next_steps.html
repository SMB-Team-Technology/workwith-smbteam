Launch a Systematic Review Generation Campaign
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
Alpha Center holds 39 Google reviews at 4.3 stars — the highest raw count among dedicated mediation firms in Doylestown, but a lower star rating than every major competitor. More critically, the most recent visible review is approximately 4 years old. Google's local ranking algorithm heavily weights recency; a firm with older reviews can be outranked by a competitor with fewer but more recent ones.
Who Is Doing It Better
Rubin, Glickman, Steinberg & Gifford P.C. — 69 reviews at 4.8 stars with a review confirmed April 14, 2026. Their review velocity is active and current; Alpha Center's has gone quiet for approximately four years.
The Opportunity
A systematic post-mediation review request process — a simple follow-up message to clients within 48 hours of case completion — could generate 5–10 new five-star reviews per month across all offices. Closing the gap from 4.3 to 4.8+ stars and maintaining active review velocity would meaningfully improve map pack rankings for "divorce mediation near me" searches across Eastern Pennsylvania.
Embed Contact Form on Homepage
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
The firm's contact form lives on a separate /contact-us/ page — every CTA button click sends a motivated prospect to a second page before they can submit their information. In a market where the prospect is often emotionally driven and time-sensitive, every extra click is a drop-off risk. The firm is paying to drive traffic that then hits unnecessary friction at the moment of conversion.
The Opportunity
Embedding a short contact form directly on the homepage — above the fold or immediately below the primary CTA — is a 1–2 day implementation that could meaningfully improve the conversion rate of existing PPC and organic traffic without spending an additional dollar on advertising. Even a 10% improvement in homepage conversion could mean 5–10 additional consultation requests per month.
Implement After-Hours Intake Coverage
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Divorce inquiries are emotionally triggered — they happen in evenings and on weekends, when a difficult conversation or a decision finally gets made. Without after-hours intake coverage across four or more offices, the most motivated prospects are the ones most likely to call a competitor before morning. First responder wins the case. No confirmed after-hours system means the firm's existing ad spend is generating after-hours leads that go unanswered.
The Opportunity
An after-hours answering service or intake system that captures lead information and sends an immediate acknowledgment — even a simple auto-response — ensures no motivated prospect goes cold overnight. For a firm with a multi-office footprint and an active paid search campaign, this is one of the highest-ROI intake improvements available because it converts leads already being generated.

Fix Martindale NAP Discrepancy
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
Martindale lists the firm's address as "55 N Main Street Suite A" — the correct address is "155 N Main Street Suite A." NAP inconsistency (Name, Address, Phone) across directories is a known suppressor of Google Business Profile rankings. This single discrepancy is actively working against the firm's local SEO across all markets where Martindale data is referenced.
The Opportunity
Correcting the Martindale listing is a one-time fix with no cost that removes an active local SEO liability. Combined with a full NAP audit across all directories, this ensures the firm's digital footprint is consistent — a prerequisite for maximizing map pack performance across all four office locations.
Build Cross-Office Accountability Structure
Team Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
A team of 14+ professionals across four geographic locations without documented KPIs and accountability systems means the firm's performance and quality depend on individual practitioners rather than repeatable processes. The owner cannot step back from a firm that requires her to be the coordination layer across four offices.
The Opportunity
Defining role ownership, case acceptance criteria, and performance metrics per office creates the operational infrastructure that allows the firm to grow — adding cases, offices, and staff — without requiring proportionately more of Keila's time. This is the structural prerequisite for the freedom the organization was built to create.
Implement Cost-Per-Case Tracking by Service Type
Profit Quick Win
Why It Matters
The firm offers multiple service lines — divorce mediation, parenting mediation, financial counseling, and tax planning — that almost certainly carry different profit margins. Without tracking cost and revenue per case type, the firm cannot determine which services are most profitable, which offices are generating the most value, or what the true ROI of the existing marketing investment actually is.
The Opportunity
Case-level profitability data enables every strategic decision the firm needs to make — which services to prioritize in marketing, which offices to expand, and how to allocate the team's professional hours for maximum impact. This is the data foundation that transforms a thriving practice into a strategically managed firm with a clear profit plan.
