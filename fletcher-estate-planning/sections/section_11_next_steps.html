Consolidate Dual Domains to a Single Website
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
Running two separate domains — fletcherestateplanning.com and jimfletcherlaw.com — splits Google's link authority between two sites, reducing the ranking potential of both. Google may not know which site to rank, and inbound links that should reinforce one strong domain are divided across two weaker ones.
The Opportunity
Redirecting jimfletcherlaw.com to fletcherestateplanning.com consolidates all link equity into a single authoritative domain. This is one of the highest-leverage SEO moves available — and it requires no new content, no advertising spend, and no design work.

Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
LSAs appear above paid search ads, above the map pack, and above all organic results — and you only pay when a real prospect calls directly. Fletcher Estate Planning is not enrolled, which means competitors may occupy all three top-of-page positions while Jim's listing appears below the fold.
Who Is Doing It Better
Perigon Legal Services — their multi-office scale and active digital marketing infrastructure make LSA enrollment likely, giving them a top-of-page position that captures calls before prospects ever reach the map pack or organic results where Jim currently ranks.

Introduce a Consultation Fee to Eliminate No-Shows
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
No-shows block consultation slots that Jim cannot recapture — at $3,000 per client and a 79% close rate, a single missed consultation represents approximately $2,370 in expected revenue that evaporates. When paid advertising is running and lead volume increases, this problem scales directly with ad spend.

Jim is already considering a $99 consultation fee — implementing it immediately filters for serious prospects, reduces no-shows, and signals professionalism to high-value estate planning clients who expect to pay for a senior attorney's time. Build After-Hours Lead Capture and Follow-Up Protocol
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Estate planning decisions are often made in the evening — after a parent's health scare, after a financial planning conversation, after a friend's estate went through probate. Prospects who cannot reach someone after 5pm routinely call the next firm on their list. Without after-hours capture, paid advertising will generate leads that never make it to Jim.
The Opportunity
A structured after-hours system — whether a virtual receptionist, an automated text acknowledgment, or a calendar booking link — captures the prospect's contact information and books the consultation before they move on. Combined with a follow-up email sequence, this converts leads that are currently being silently lost.
