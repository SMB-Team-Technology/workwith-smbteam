Website Audit

The Problem
  • The site runs on the same Wix template with no structural redesign since at least the January 2019 Wayback Machine snapshot — 7+ years on the same platform
  • PageSpeed scores: Mobile 56/100 — Desktop 99/100
  • The "CONTACT US" button sits below the practice-areas section — no clear call to action above the fold
  • The "News" section and "Cases of Interest" page are effectively undated, with no evidence of recent, active maintenance
The Positive
  • The site is mobile responsive, with dedicated attorney bio pages for all 8 attorneys, including founding partners Meryl L. Wenig and Jeffrey L. Saltiel
  • Desktop performance is excellent at 99/100
The Gap
  • A 56/100 mobile score means a meaningful share of prospective clients researching landlord or foreclosure issues on their phones face a slower, clunkier experience than competitors offer
  • An undated blog and case-summary section signals inactivity to anyone comparing firms online
None of this changes what Jeffrey actually asked for — but it is worth knowing this exists as the firm's reputation continues to carry the load the website does not.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • The firm's Google Business Profile shows 39 Google reviews (per Birdeye's aggregator breakdown) and a 3.4-star blended rating across 43 total reviews
  • FindLaw, Martindale, BCGSearch, and LawCrossing all still list a stale Brooklyn address (26 Court Street, Suite 1200) even though the firm's current offices are in Manhattan and Staten Island
  • No dedicated geo-targeting landing pages exist for surrounding boroughs despite the firm having handled Brooklyn-based matters historically
Blind Spot: A stale address propagating across four high-authority legal directories can quietly confuse both prospective clients and Google's own local ranking signals — even though it has nothing to do with the actual work happening inside the firm.
The Positive
  • 39 Google reviews and a 25+ year reputation, with Meryl L. Wenig holding Avvo Top Contributor and Client's Choice awards and Jeffrey L. Saltiel a Super Lawyer since 2018, are real, earned credibility markers
The Gap
  • Rosenberg & Estis (57 reviews, 4.6 stars) and Belkin Burden Goldman (52-53 reviews, 4.7 stars) both edge out Wenig Saltiel's review volume and rating in the same practice areas and same borough
Rosenberg & Estis, P.C. — holds more total reviews and a higher star rating in the same Manhattan landlord-representation and real estate litigation space.
This is useful context for the firm's broader market position — but fixing it is not what Jeffrey asked SMB Team to help with.
