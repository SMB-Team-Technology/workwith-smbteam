Lead Generation

Jensen & McComas has grown to $1.2M in revenue with almost zero formal marketing — new clients arrive through partner outreach and conference networking, not through any repeatable digital system. That has worked for 16 months, but it does not scale to a $3M target on its own.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Both hero-section "Learn More" buttons route to the About page — there is no consultation-request or contact CTA above the fold
  • PageSpeed mobile and desktop scores were not obtained this session — the public PageSpeed API returned HTTP 429 (quota exceeded) on three separate attempts; a live manual check is recommended before the proposal call
  • No contact form is visible on the homepage itself — it is buried on a separate page
  • No geo-targeted landing pages exist for surrounding growth markets such as Columbia, Annapolis, or Bel Air
Blind Spot: With no above-the-fold call to action and no visible contact form, most site visitors today — including people referred directly by a partner — have no obvious next step except to go back to Google.
The Positive
  • Full "Our People" section with photos and individual bio pages for all 8 attorneys
  • Site is mobile responsive, and an active News/blog section is regularly updated
The Gap
  • Referral traffic that reaches the site today has nowhere clear to convert once it arrives
  • Growth-market interest from surrounding cities goes uncaptured entirely with no dedicated landing pages
  • Every visitor who leaves without a clear next step is a lead the firm already earned but never converted
A website built to convert would turn every dollar of reputation you have built through partners and conferences into an actual pipeline — not just a digital business card.
