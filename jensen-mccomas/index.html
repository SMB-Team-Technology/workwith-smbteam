Here Is Where Jensen & McComas, LLC Stands Today

Jensen & McComas, LLC is a 16-month-old corporate and trusts & estates firm in Towson, MD, built by two partners who split off from an established regional firm in March 2025. In just over a year the firm has grown to 16 staff and roughly $1.2M in annual revenue almost entirely through partner outreach and referrals — but the same absence of a marketing engine and a leaky intake process that let the firm get this far without spending a dollar on ads is exactly what stands between it and the stated goal of reaching $3M within the year.

Stage 6 is where Jensen & McComas adds the five new attorneys and staff already planned, reaches $3M in revenue, and does it without the wheels coming off — building the firm Mark and Kristine set out to create when they left their prior firm, not just a bigger, more strained version of what exists today.

Where You Are Today

STAGE 4: SMALL BUSINESS MANAGER
  • New business comes almost entirely from partner outreach — Dealmakers conferences and the Chambers 100 network — not from any repeatable marketing system
  • Google Business Profile is unoptimized with zero reviews, and no formal marketing has ever run on Google, Meta, or Local Service Ads
  • Admin staff are already supporting multiple attorneys each at max capacity, and intake has no defined, delegated process
  • Kristine is personally building the firm's future intake system while also running the practice day to day

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
Closing that gap starts with the foundational systems you already identified on the call — a real intake process and an operational structure that lets you hire five people without breaking what is already stretched thin — before a single dollar goes to marketing.

The Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model

We Help Law Firm Owners Get More Profit, More Personal Freedom, And Outstanding Results For Their Clients.

Most law firms try to grow by chasing more leads. But leads alone are not the problem — and leads alone are not the answer. Here is what actually works.

Lead Generation

Leads are to a law firm what gasoline is to a car. Without them, you go nowhere. But a steady, predictable flow of leads changes everything — you get to choose your cases instead of taking whatever comes in.

Intake

But all the leads in the world are worthless without a system to turn them into paying clients. Most firms are losing cases they already paid for simply because their follow-up is slow or inconsistent.

Self-Managing Team

Once clients are coming in, you need a team — otherwise you end up working 90-hour weeks and results suffer. A self-managing team is what gives you the freedom to step back.

Profit Plan

And once the team is in place, most owners look at their bank account and ask: where did all the money go? That is what a profit plan solves — clarity on what you earn, what you keep, and how to grow both.

All four have to work together. If one is missing, you cannot have more profit, more freedom, and outstanding results for your clients at the same time.
