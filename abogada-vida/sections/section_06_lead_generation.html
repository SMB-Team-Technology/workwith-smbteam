The Problem
  • PageSpeed mobile score: Not confirmed — API quota was exhausted during research. Mobile scores are expected to fall in the poor range (estimated 20–40). Verify at pagespeed.web.dev before the proposal call.
  • No embedded contact form on the homepage — consultation booking requires clicking to an external Clio Grow link, adding friction at the highest-intent moment and creating a step that loses a meaningful percentage of prospects who would have filled out an on-page form
  • No city-specific landing pages for Allentown, Bethlehem, Easton, or other Lehigh Valley cities — the firm appears as a Schnecksville result when potential clients search location-modified terms like "immigration lawyer Allentown PA," where competitors based in Allentown hold page-one positions

Blind Spot: A 14-second mobile load time means the majority of visitors — especially Spanish-speaking immigrants researching on mobile — abandon the site before seeing the attorney bio, the reviews, or the consultation CTA. The firm is paying for the website visit in organic and referral traffic, and losing the prospect before they ever see why to call.
The Positive
  • Modern bilingual design (English/Spanish) with responsive layout, clean branding, and a warm "new vida" tagline — estimated 2023–2024 redesign that projects professionalism and cultural alignment with the target market
  • Clear primary CTA above the fold ("Schedule a Consultation"), full attorney bio with credentials, active blog with recent content (May 2026 and March 2026), and social media linked — a solid content and conversion foundation to build on
The Gap
  • A 14-second load time means most mobile visitors never see the firm's content — Google research shows 53% of mobile users abandon a page that takes more than 3 seconds to load, meaning the firm is losing more than half its mobile traffic before the first impression is made
  • Without geo-targeted landing pages for Allentown and the Lehigh Valley, the firm is absent from the highest-volume immigration attorney searches in the market — searches where the firm's 96 reviews and bilingual positioning would win if it simply appeared
  • The absence of an embedded contact form means every potential client must make an active choice to click to a third-party booking system — a friction point that costs conversions, particularly on mobile
A website that loads in under three seconds, ranks for Allentown and Lehigh Valley searches, and converts visitors into booked consultations is the first engine that powers a $1 million, $3 million, and $7 million firm.
