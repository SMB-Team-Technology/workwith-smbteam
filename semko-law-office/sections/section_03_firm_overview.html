Here Is Where Semko Law Office LLC Stands Today

Jeffrey Semko and Kasey Defoe operate Semko Law Office LLC out of Schererville, Indiana, handling criminal defense, DUI/OWI, traffic violations, expungement, and family law for clients throughout Lake County and Northwest Indiana. With 109 Google reviews and a 4.8-star rating, the firm has earned genuine credibility — but without paid advertising, a blog, or any observed digital marketing presence, it has no system for converting that credibility into a predictable flow of new clients. Meanwhile, Stracci Law Group holds 392 Google reviews and is actively running paid ads in the same market.

Stage 6 is where you stop being the sole engine of the firm and cases come to you on a schedule — so your income and your time are no longer dictated by who called you this week.

Where You Are Today

Stage 3: Solo Practitioner
  • No paid advertising observed — marketing appears to rely on referrals and organic search results that the firm does not actively manage

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The firm has earned real credibility — but credibility without systems means growth is capped by the referrals that come in this month, not by the quality of the work being done.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Semko Law Office LLC
Semko Law Office is operating at Stage 3 — a growing firm with genuine client satisfaction and a proven ability to win cases, but no scalable lead generation engine in a market that is growing more competitive by the month. The single most urgent gap is the complete absence of paid digital marketing: Stracci Law Group has 392 Google reviews, active Google Ads, geo-targeting pages across six counties, and media coverage — while Semko Law has none of those systems. Until a reliable marketing infrastructure is in place, the growth this firm has earned will remain dependent on who happens to refer a client this week.
