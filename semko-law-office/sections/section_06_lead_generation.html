Lead Generation

Semko Law Office LLC has built a 4.8-star reputation across 109 Google reviews without any observed paid advertising investment — a remarkable foundation that is being left entirely unmonetized while competitors in the same market run active campaigns across multiple channels. The gap between what this firm has earned and what it is capturing in new case flow is significant and growing.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Site estimated 5–8 years old with a dated WordPress aesthetic — the visual age of the site signals to first-time visitors that the firm may not be keeping pace with competitors who have modern, conversion-focused websites
  • PageSpeed scores: Not retrieved — manual verification required (the PageSpeed Insights tool returned loading states during all research attempts; based on the site's dated theme and build, scores are likely below the market average and should be checked before the audit is finalized)
  • Contact form buried on a separate /contact-us/ page — not visible above the fold; visitors who arrive ready to act must navigate away from the homepage before they can reach out
  • No video on the homepage — attorney credibility is not communicated visually, which is a meaningful conversion disadvantage against competitors who use video to build trust before a prospect ever calls
Blind Spot: The firm's homepage headline prominently targets traffic ticket and CDL cases, but the Yelp business category is listed as "Divorce & Family Law" — meaning different directories are sending inconsistent category signals about the firm's primary focus. This misalignment can suppress visibility for the criminal defense and DUI searches that represent the firm's core practice area.
The Positive
  • Website is mobile responsive — responsive navigation with hamburger menu confirmed, meeting the baseline requirement for mobile usability that Google now requires for ranking
  • "Former Lake County Prosecutor" is prominently stated on the site — a genuine trust differentiator that the vast majority of competitors in this market cannot claim
The Gap
  • The buried contact form means visitors who reach the site but cannot quickly find a way to submit an inquiry leave without converting — and in the criminal defense space, they call the next firm on their list within minutes
  • Without a blog or fresh content, the website cannot rank for the high-value long-tail searches — "first-time DUI Indiana what happens," "how long does expungement take Lake County," "what to do after OWI arrest" — that drive early-funnel leads at lower cost than paid ads
  • No geo-targeting landing pages means the firm only appears in Schererville searches — while Stracci Law Group appears across six counties because they have built dedicated pages for every city they serve
A rebuilt website that converts visitors into calls is not a design project — it is the foundation of a case pipeline that works while you are in trial.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • 109 Google reviews vs. Stracci Law Group's 392 — the review gap translates directly into lower local 3-pack rankings for every criminal defense, DUI, and expungement search across Lake County
  • No evidence of active Google Business Profile posts — GBP posting is a free local ranking signal that costs nothing and that competitors are using while this firm is not
  • Zero geo-targeting landing pages for the 11 cities the firm serves — Crown Point, Hammond, Merrillville, Gary, Dyer, Griffith, Highland, Munster, Hobart, and East Chicago are all listed as service areas on the website, but none have dedicated pages that Google can rank for local searches
Blind Spot: The Yelp listing categorizes the firm as "Divorce & Family Law" rather than "Criminal Defense Law" — a category mismatch that sends inconsistent signals to both users and search engines. A prospect searching for a criminal defense attorney who encounters a Yelp listing categorized as family law may question whether this is the right firm, reducing click-through and trust before a single word is read.
The Positive
  • 109 Google reviews at 4.8 stars is a genuinely strong foundation — well above the threshold needed to be competitive in Local Service Ads and sufficient to support a serious local SEO push with the right strategy in place
The Gap
  • Without active GBP posting and a structured review acquisition strategy, the firm will continue falling further behind competitors who are actively building their profiles — the review gap widens every month
  • The absence of city landing pages for Crown Point, Hammond, Merrillville, and Dyer means the firm is invisible in the largest cities it serves — and those are the markets with the highest search volume for criminal defense and DUI attorneys
Stracci Law Group — maintains geo-targeting pages for Lake, Porter, LaPorte, Newton, Jasper, and Starke counties, plus individual city pages across the entire Northwest Indiana region. They appear in local search results for searches the firm currently cannot rank for, capturing clients who are looking for exactly what Semko Law offers — just in a neighboring city.
Owning the local 3-pack across Lake County means cases come to you — without cold calls, without chasing referrals, and without showing up somewhere new every time the pipeline runs dry.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • Semko Law does not appear to be running Google search ads for criminal defense, DUI/OWI, or expungement searches in Lake County — the firm may not be configured to show for these terms, or may be getting outbid by competitors with larger budgets in this market
  • Every high-intent search for "DUI attorney Schererville," "criminal defense lawyer Crown Point," or "expungement attorney Northwest Indiana" is currently going to a competitor — these prospects have already decided they need an attorney and are ready to call
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Stracci Law Group — observed appearing at the top of DUI/OWI Crown Point Indiana search results in a paid position; actively investing in search ads to capture the high-intent criminal defense prospects who are ready to retain an attorney today
  • Note on market economics: Average cost-per-click for DUI attorneys in Indiana markets runs $50–$100+. At a retained case value of $3,000–$7,500 per client, even one additional case per month from ads produces a return that far exceeds the ad spend — the math strongly favors activation
The Opportunity
  • "Criminal defense attorney Schererville" / "DUI lawyer Schererville" — the highest-intent local searches in the firm's home market; prospects running these searches have already decided they need help and are comparing their options right now
  • "OWI attorney Lake County Indiana" / "expungement lawyer Northwest Indiana" — practice area plus geography combinations that produce qualified leads across the entire region the firm already serves
  • Competitor name bidding — targeting searches for Stracci Law Group and Shane O'Donnell captures prospects who are already researching criminal defense attorneys in the region and may not yet have committed to a firm
  • "CDL lawyer Indiana" / "traffic ticket attorney Lake County" — lower-competition terms where the firm's homepage already ranks organically; adding a paid position on the same search gives the firm both the ad and the organic result, doubling screen presence and eliminating a click to a competitor
Blind Spot: The firm's homepage already appears organically for traffic ticket and CDL-related searches because the headline directly targets those terms. Running Google Ads on those same keywords would place Semko Law in both the paid and organic positions simultaneously — giving the firm two spots on the page instead of one and significantly reducing the chance that a prospect clicks a competitor's ad instead.

Local Service Ads

  • With 109 Google reviews at 4.8 stars, the firm already has the review foundation that Google looks for when evaluating LSA eligibility — the infrastructure is in place; activation just has not happened
Competitor LSA Landscape
  • Stracci Law Group — with 392 Google reviews and a full marketing operation, positioned to hold LSA slots across criminal defense, DUI, and personal injury in the Northwest Indiana market
  • Law Offices of Shane O'Donnell — 364 total reviews and a 4.9-star rating; strong positioning for LSA slots in the Crown Point and Lake County market, capturing the top-of-page visibility that Semko Law is currently not competing for
The Opportunity

  • LSAs appear above everything else on Google — above paid ads, above the map pack, above all organic results — and you only pay when a real prospect calls you directly.
  • Google Screened status also functions as a trust badge that verifies the attorney's license and background — for a criminal defense firm, this government-backed verification is a conversion differentiator that no amount of organic SEO can replicate

Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Stracci Law Group — active Facebook page with 638+ likes and an established social media infrastructure; positioned to be running Meta retargeting and awareness campaigns for criminal defense and DUI leads throughout Northwest Indiana
  • Law Offices of Shane O'Donnell — active social presence confirmed across multiple platforms; social media visibility is part of their "Premier NWI" brand positioning and contributes to the awareness they build before a prospect is ready to call
The Opportunity
  • Retargeting — website visitors who did not convert are leaving with no follow-up; a Meta retargeting campaign showing Jeffrey Semko's face and credentials keeps the firm top of mind for prospects who are still deciding
  • Video ads featuring the attorney — a 60-second video of Jeffrey Semko explaining what to do after a DUI arrest or how expungement works in Indiana builds immediate trust before a prospect ever calls, and creates an emotional connection that static ads cannot
  • Educational content ads — "What to do if you're charged with a DUI in Indiana" style ads capture leads at the research stage, before they're ready to call but while they are actively considering their options and comparing firms
  • Attorney positioning ads — "Former Lake County Prosecutor now defending clients like you" ads would directly differentiate Semko Law from competitors and drive calls from prospects who specifically value prosecutorial experience on their side
