Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • No paid advertising observed — Google Ads, Local Service Ads, and Meta Ads are all absent while competitors run active campaigns
  • No blog or content strategy — the website has no fresh content to support organic rankings or early-funnel lead generation
  • 109 Google reviews vs. Stracci Law Group's 392 — the review gap directly suppresses local search rankings across all practice areas
Without a predictable lead flow, growth depends entirely on referrals — and you cannot step back from rainmaking until the firm can generate its own cases.

Intake

  • No live chat observed — after-hours criminal defense and DUI inquiries have no way to initiate contact without making a phone call during business hours
  • Contact form buried on a separate page — not visible to visitors who arrive on the homepage ready to act
  • No evidence of a defined follow-up sequence for web leads who submit a form but do not call immediately
Every lead that slips through intake is revenue the firm already generated — and time spent re-earning it is time that could have been spent elsewhere.

Self-Managing Team

  • Two attorneys on staff (Jeffrey Semko and Kasey Defoe) — but no observed dedicated intake, ops, or marketing coordinator
  • Broad practice area mix across criminal defense, DUI, expungement, family law, and personal injury handled by a 2-attorney team with no visible role specialization
  • No documented accountability structure — the firm is operationally dependent on both attorneys being available at all times
A 2-attorney firm without operational infrastructure means the owner is still the firm — and that limits both income and freedom regardless of how much revenue grows.

Profit

  • Revenue estimated at approximately $2M annually (public data) — a meaningful foundation, but without a marketing system, month-to-month case flow is unpredictable
  • No observed marketing investment means acquisition cost is not being tracked — the firm cannot know which sources or case types produce the best return
  • No evidence of cost-per-case visibility or financial benchmarking by practice area across the 10+ services offered
Knowing your numbers is not about accounting — it is about making decisions that compound. Without that clarity, the firm earns by accident and cannot plan intentional growth.
