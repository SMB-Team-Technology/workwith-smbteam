Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • Entire case pipeline depends on purchased leads at $2,000 per retainer — no owned marketing asset generating leads
  • No Google Business Profile, no directory listings, no organic presence — invisible in local search across both AZ and FL
  • No Google Ads, no LSAs, no Meta Ads — while Phillips Law Group and Lamber Goodnow dominate every paid channel in Phoenix
Without a predictable, owned lead engine, the firm cannot scale — and Mariano cannot step back from day-to-day case acquisition.

Intake

  • One hourly intake employee with no after-hours coverage — the website promises 24/7 service the firm cannot currently deliver
  • No defined intake process, no CRM, and no automated follow-up for leads who do not sign immediately
  • At $2,000 per purchased retainer, a single missed after-hours call is $2,000 in wasted spend plus a lost case
Every lead that falls through intake is money Mariano already paid for — and a case the firm cannot count toward its monthly target.

Self-Managing Team

  • Three-person support team is in place — intake, case manager, and paralegal — but no manager layer between the team and the owner
  • No documented workflows, accountability systems, or org structure to allow the team to operate independently
  • As case volume grows toward 20–25 per month, the current structure will require Mariano to be more involved, not less
A team without systems is a team that depends on the owner — and depending on the owner is the opposite of the automated firm Mariano wants to build.

Profit

  • The firm is pre-settlement with no collected fee revenue — all capital is currently funding lead purchases and operations
  • Spending $20,000–$25,000 per month on purchased leads with no visibility into cost-per-case, margin, or break-even point
  • No profit plan, no financial tracking framework — when settlements start arriving, there is no system to plan what to keep and what to reinvest
Revenue without a profit plan is just activity — and activity without financial clarity cannot fund the firm Mariano wants to build.
