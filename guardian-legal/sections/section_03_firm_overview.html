Here Is Where Guardian Legal PLLC Stands Today

Guardian Legal PLLC is a four-month-old personal injury firm licensed in Arizona and Florida, focusing on pre-litigation motor vehicle accident cases with 16 active files and its first settlement on the horizon. The firm is generating cases today entirely through purchased leads at $2,000 per signed retainer — a model that is consuming $20,000–$25,000 a month without building any owned marketing asset or online presence. The single most critical finding is this: Guardian Legal has zero Google visibility, no Google Business Profile, no directory listings, and no ads of any kind running in one of the most competitive personal injury markets in the country.

Stage 6 is where the firm runs on its own — where Mariano has built something automated, system-driven, and worth far more than the sum of its cases. That is exactly the destination this roadmap is designed to reach.
