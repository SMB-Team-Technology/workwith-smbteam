Establish Case-Level Profitability Tracking
Profit Quick Win
Why It Matters
PI cases vary dramatically in time-to-close, cost to work, and net margin — a car accident case and a catastrophic injury case can look similar on intake and produce very different outcomes. Without profitability data by case type, the firm cannot make informed decisions about where to invest its marketing budget or which referrals to prioritize.
The Opportunity
Setting up basic case-level profitability tracking allows marketing spend to be directed toward the case types that generate the most profit per dollar invested — not just the most volume — and gives the firm data to negotiate more effectively with referral sources and partner networks.
