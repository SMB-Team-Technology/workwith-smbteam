Here Is Where Lone Star Legal Stands Today

Lone Star Legal — the DBA of Kelly Law Firm, P.C. — is a Georgetown, Texas personal injury firm led by Managing Partner Robbye Kelly and Board Certified PI trial attorney L. Todd Kelly, backed by a nine-person team that includes a CEO, dedicated intake specialist, and senior paralegals. The firm has built real credibility over decades of practice, with Todd Kelly earning Super Lawyer recognition for more than ten consecutive years and a lifetime membership in the Multi-Million Dollar Advocates Forum. Despite those credentials, the firm's digital marketing footprint does not match what it has earned — it does not appear to be running paid ads on any channel, is not visible in the Google local 3-pack for its primary keywords, and faces rapidly growing competition from well-funded regional firms actively capturing Georgetown PI traffic.

Stage 6 is where a firm runs itself — where the systems, the team, and the marketing all work together without the owner in the middle of every decision — and that is exactly the destination this roadmap is designed to get Lone Star Legal to.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • No confirmed paid advertising on Google, LSA, or Meta — new cases depend primarily on referrals and organic presence
  • Not appearing in the Google local 3-pack for Georgetown personal injury searches despite a Georgetown address and a Board Certified PI attorney on staff
  • Competitors with 400–790+ reviews are dominating the search results and digital channels Lone Star Legal is not yet on
  • A nine-person team and dedicated CEO are in place — but without a predictable lead generation system, the team's capacity is underutilized

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The credentials, the team, and the reputation are already here — what is missing is the predictable lead generation system and the marketing infrastructure to make Lone Star Legal the dominant personal injury firm in Williamson County.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Lone Star Legal
Lone Star Legal is operating at Stage 4 — a Small Business Manager with an established team structure but a critical gap in digital lead generation. The firm has the credentials that most competitors can only aspire to — a Board Certified PI trial attorney, AV Preeminent rating, and 10+ years on the Super Lawyers list — but those credentials are invisible to the thousands of Georgetown-area accident victims searching Google every month. The most urgent limiting factor right now is the absence of a paid lead generation system: while firms like Angel Reyes & Associates and The Carlson Law Firm actively capture Georgetown PI traffic through Google Ads, LSAs, and local SEO, Lone Star Legal is not appearing in those channels — and every month without a system is a month those competitors extend their lead.
