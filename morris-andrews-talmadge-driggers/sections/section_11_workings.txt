AD SPEND — CONSERVATIVE AND AGGRESSIVE
===================================================================

Practice area used for channel minimums: MVA / Car Accidents (the firm's
core caseload — car, truck, motorcycle accidents).

CONSERVATIVE = sum of channel minimums (Google PPC $10,000 + Meta
Retargeting $1,500 + Meta Lead Gen $6,000) = $17,500/mo.
(Satisfies PI hard floor for High competitiveness, $10,000 min.)

AGGRESSIVE:
- 20% rule: Revenue GOAL $10,000,000 x 20% / 12 = $166,667/mo baseline.
  Geo multiplier: Dothan/Mobile AL = Tier 4 "most mid-size cities" (1.0x).
  No Spanish campaign. Minus Platinum mgmt fee ($15,997) = $150,670
  available ad spend under the 20% rule.
- Reverse math: $10M goal / 12 = $833,333/mo target revenue. Avg case
  value not stated in transcript — using disclosed default $7,500
  (midpoint of MVA $5K-$10K range, this firm's mix skews higher due to
  truck/construction/workplace claims). Target cases = 111/mo. Close rate
  not stated — default 15% -> ~741 leads/mo required. Blended CPL (Google
  Search 57% / Meta retargeting 9% / Meta cold 34% weighting, midpoints) ~
  $468, no cushion -> ~$346,900/mo required spend.
- Reverse math ($346,900) > 20% rule ($150,670) -> reverse math governs per
  rule, but BOTH exceed the Platinum tier cap ($150,000) AND the 35%-of-
  revenue total spend cap.
- 35% CAP CHECK (binding constraint): Monthly revenue ~$5M/12 = ~$416,667.
  35% = ~$145,833 max total SMB spend (mgmt fees + ad spend). Fixed mgmt
  fees = $19,294 (Platinum + FCOO Advisor) -> max ad spend for cap
  compliance = ~$126,539/mo.
- RECOMMENDATION: $125,000/mo aggressive ad spend — capped by the 35%
  total-spend rule, comfortably under the Platinum tier's own $150,000 cap.
  Reaching the full $10M goal purely through paid ads at a responsible
  spend level is not realistic in year one; hitting $10M+ will require
  continued referral-channel strength plus the FCOO-led operational
  scale-up in addition to paid growth. This nuance is reflected in the
  Phase 2/3 roadmap in section_11_next_steps.html, not stated as a
  guarantee anywhere in the client-facing copy.

CONSERVATIVE ROI PROJECTION ($17,500/mo):
- Blended CPL with 20% conservative cushion: ~$468 x 1.2 = ~$562
- Leads = $17,500 / $562 ~= 31/mo
- Cases = 31 x 15% close rate ~= 5/mo
- Revenue = 5 x $7,500 avg case value = $37,500/mo
- Return = $37,500 / $17,500 = 2.1x

AGGRESSIVE ROI PROJECTION ($125,000/mo):
- Blended CPL, no cushion: ~$468
- Leads = $125,000 / $468 ~= 267/mo
- Cases = 267 x 15% close rate ~= 40/mo
- Revenue = 40 x $7,500 avg case value = $300,000/mo
- Return = $300,000 / $125,000 = 2.4x

All figures disclosed to the client as estimates using an assumed 15%
close rate and $7,500 average case value (neither stated in transcript).
