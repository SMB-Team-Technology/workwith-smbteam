Lead Generation

Morris, Andrews, Talmadge & Driggers has built a 25+ year reputation in Dothan and Mobile almost entirely on referrals — with no tracked paid marketing, a broken primary domain, and a Dothan Google Business Profile that is invisible in local search. Meanwhile, named regional competitors are actively dominating the same online channels the firm has never tapped.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • The firm's own primary domain, mcatlaw.com, returns an error page ("Error. Page cannot be displayed") — anyone who types or clicks the firm's actual name loses the lead before ever reaching the site
  • PageSpeed scores could not be captured this session — Google's PageSpeed Insights API returned a hard daily quota error on repeated attempts; a manual pagespeed.web.dev check on injurylawamerica.com is recommended before finalizing scope
  • Contact form is buried mid-page, after the "Why Hire Us" section — not visible above the fold
  • No dedicated city or location landing pages beyond the two main offices, despite the firm's stated ambition to target a wide "Black Belt" region
Blind Spot: You may not realize that your own firm's actual domain name — mcatlaw.com — is broken for anyone who finds you by name, whether from a referral, a business card, or a direct search for your firm. That is a lost-lead risk hiding in plain sight.
The Positive
  • Site is mobile responsive with a working navigation, and all five attorneys have individual bio pages
  • The blog is actively maintained, with posts as recent as June/July
The Gap
  • Every referral or word-of-mouth prospect who tries the firm's real domain name hits a dead end instead of a call to action
  • A buried contact form means visitors have to work to reach the firm instead of being invited to
  • Without current PageSpeed data, mobile load speed — a top predictor of bounce rate — is an unverified risk
A website that turns visitors into calls is one of the fastest ways to start proving the ROI the partners need to unlock the next stage of growth.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • Main Google Business Profile shows just 3 reviews at 4.3 stars — a fraction of what every named competitor in Dothan and Mobile carries
  • The Dothan office GBP is unverified by Google, which blocks that location from local map visibility entirely
  • The firm does not appear to be showing in the local 3-pack for "car accident lawyer Dothan AL," "personal injury lawyer Dothan AL," "car accident lawyer Mobile AL," or "personal injury lawyer Mobile AL"
Blind Spot: The old firm name, "Morris, Cary, Andrews, Talmadge & Driggers, LLC," is still live on Avvo and at least one FindLaw profile — inconsistent business listings like this actively suppress the local rankings the firm is trying to win.
The Positive
  • Martindale listings for both offices are accurate and consistent with the current site and firm name
The Gap
  • Three reviews and an unverified Dothan listing mean the firm is functionally invisible in the exact searches its target clients run every day
  • Competitors with 60-150+ reviews are capturing the trust-driven clicks MCAT should be winning with 25+ years in the market
Stokes Stemle, LLC — 119 reviews at 4.9 stars in Dothan, an established PI-only brand with a review moat the firm cannot currently compete with on trust signals.
Every month the Dothan GBP stays unverified is a month of local search visibility the firm is voluntarily giving to Stokes Stemle and The Cochran Firm.
