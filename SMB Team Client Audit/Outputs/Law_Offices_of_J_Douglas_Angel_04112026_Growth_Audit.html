The Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model
We help law firm owners get more profit, more personal freedom, and outstanding results for their clients.

SMB Team has worked with hundreds of law firms and built a proven framework for helping law firm owners get three things at once — more profit, more personal freedom, and outstanding results for their clients. The framework is called the Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model. It is built on four pillars that every law firm must master to grow.

  • Lead Generation — a steady source of new clients that does not depend on referrals. The firm controls where its next client comes from.
  • Intake — a system that turns leads into signed clients without the owner doing it themselves. Speed and follow-up win cases before they are even filed.
  • Self-Managing Team — a team that runs the firm without the owner in every decision. Until this exists, the owner cannot have more free time — no matter how much revenue grows.
  • Profit Plan — a clear picture of what comes in, what goes out, and what the owner keeps. Revenue is vanity. Profit is sanity.

When all four pillars work together, the result is a firm that grows on its own, runs without the owner doing everything, and produces real profit — giving the attorney the income, the freedom, and the impact they set out to create.
