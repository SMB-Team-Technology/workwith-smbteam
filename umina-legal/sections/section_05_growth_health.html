Lead Generation

Intake

  • Ryan personally handles every intake call — when he is unavailable, every lead waits or leaves

Self-Managing Team

  • No intake coordinator, paralegal, or case manager — Ryan is the bottleneck for every function
  • Prior scaling attempt led to burnout — growth without delegation will repeat the same pattern
The 30-hour workweek Ryan described requires a team that can carry the firm's daily operations — that team does not yet exist, and building it is not optional on the path to $2M.

Profit

  • Revenue is strong at $100K/month but bookkeeping is acknowledged as a weakness requiring professional oversight
  • Case management on spreadsheets provides no visibility into margins, cost-per-acquisition, or write-off rates
  • No stated ROI tracking on Google Ads — at $3,000/month in spend, signed case attribution is unknown
Without knowing what the firm actually keeps, Ryan cannot plan the freedom his revenue should already be funding — profit needs to be engineered, not discovered at tax time.
