Lead Generation

Intake

  • No dedicated intake team member — call handling and follow-up run through a lean 3-person team plus AI call-answering tools
  • No contact form found above the fold on the homepage; direct phone and email are the only visible path to reach the firm
  • No live chat in place, so after-hours website visitors have no immediate way to start a conversation
Every lead that slips through a gap in intake is time you already spent on marketing walking out the door.

Self-Managing Team

  • Just 3 people total — John, a remote paralegal, and an in-house legal secretary — with no dedicated ops, marketing, or intake role
  • AI tools (RingCentral call answering, GoHighLevel CRM) are covering ground a trained team member would normally own
  • No management layer exists between John and day-to-day casework
Until this changes, the practice cannot run without John in the room — no matter how much revenue doubles.

Profit

  • Revenue is growing fast — from $270K to a projected $500K in 2026 — but there is no visibility into overall margin, only channel-level marketing ROI
  • Marketing spend is tracked closely by channel (LSA profitable, Access Legal ~3x ROI, Facebook low ROI), but that discipline hasn't been extended to the full practice P&L
  • No profit plan exists to make sure the revenue growth turns into take-home income rather than expanding overhead
Doubling revenue without a profit plan means doubling the risk that the growth never reaches your pocket.
