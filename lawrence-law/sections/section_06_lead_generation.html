Lead Generation

Lawrence Law LLC has built a $3 million boutique practice on the strength of Kate Lawrence's professional network and rare NAMWOLF and WBENC certifications — but with no digital lead generation system in place, the firm's growth ceiling is set entirely by how many new relationships Kate can personally build. Every day that passes without paid search, Local Service Ads, or a functioning website contact channel is a day Lawrence Law's competitors capture the commercial clients that should be coming here instead.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Website contact page (/contact-us) returns a 404 error — visitors who click "Contact" hit a dead end with no inquiry form, no phone number fallback, and no way to submit their information
  • PageSpeed scores: Not retrieved during research (API rate-limited) — must be verified manually at pagespeed.web.dev before campaign launch to confirm mobile performance
  • No above-fold contact form, no live chat, and no video — all three of the highest-converting website elements for professional services are absent from the homepage
  • Blog content is dormant since January 2024 — no fresh content to signal ongoing firm activity or capture long-tail SEO traffic for construction law, employment law, or commercial real estate searches
Blind Spot: Every NAMWOLF referral, speaking engagement, and industry article Kate publishes sends interested parties to the website — where a broken contact page stops them cold. The firm may be losing warm, already-interested prospects at the moment of highest intent, without ever knowing they came.
The Positive
  • Modern, professional aesthetic with clean navigation and clear practice area structure — visual design and firm branding do not undermine trust for visitors who make it through
  • Full team page with attorney bios, Super Lawyers recognition for multiple attorneys, and seven client testimonials build strong credibility signals for warm referral visitors
The Gap
  • A business that finds Lawrence Law through NAMWOLF or a referral, visits the website, and clicks "Contact" hits a 404 error — that opportunity is permanently lost before the firm even knows they came
  • Without PageSpeed data, the site may have mobile loading speed issues that kill conversion before any visitor sees the firm's credentials — particularly critical if paid ads start driving mobile traffic
  • No video, no lead magnet, and no above-fold CTA mean the website functions as a digital brochure rather than a lead generation asset
A website that cannot receive inquiries is not a business asset — it is a liability, and fixing the contact page is the single highest-ROI action in this entire audit before any paid advertising dollar is spent.
