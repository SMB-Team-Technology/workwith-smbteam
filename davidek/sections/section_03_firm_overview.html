Here Is Where Davidek Law Firm Pllc Stands Today

Davidek Law Firm PLLC is a New Braunfels estate planning, asset protection, and probate practice built on 85% estate planning work, with a strong local reputation — a 4.9-star rating, roughly 156 aggregated reviews, and an actively-updated blog publishing multiple posts a week. But the firm is currently running at 3 of 5 attorney seats after a year-long hiring struggle, payroll costs have climbed above 50% of revenue against a 25% target, and paid marketing is fully outsourced to an agency the firm is actively trying to reduce its dependence on — all while targeting $4M in revenue and an Austin market launch by 2027.

Stage 6 is where a firm becomes a sellable, self-sustaining asset instead of a job — exactly the kind of enterprise value you are building toward with your CFO work and your $4M target.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • Staffed at 3 of 5 attorney seats after more than a year spent trying to hire two more attorneys
  • Payroll costs exceed 50% of revenue against a stated 25% target, pushing margins into the low single digits
  • Lead flow is strong enough to book appointments into late August, but paid-channel performance is managed entirely by an outside agency the firm can't fully see into
  • No dedicated digital presence exists yet for the planned January 2027 Austin market launch

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
Closing the gap between Stage 4 and Stage 6 means turning today's lead flow and payroll pressure into predictable, hands-off systems — the kind that make a firm worth building toward $4M, and worth selling.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Davidek Law Firm Pllc
