Fix NAP Inconsistencies Across Directories
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
Google cross-references Name/Address/Phone data across the web to determine local search authority. Martindale still lists the old address (3264 Simmons St), the Texas State Bar lists the current address (2600 South Loop West), and multiple entity name variations exist across directories — these inconsistencies actively suppress local search rankings.
The Opportunity
Correcting NAP consistency across Martindale, Avvo, Justia, Yelp, FindLaw, and other active directories is a one-time, low-cost action that removes a known barrier to 3-pack visibility — and strengthens the authority of every other local SEO investment made going forward.
Track Profitability by Practice Area
Profit Quick Win
Why It Matters
A mixed-practice firm with PI contingency, criminal defense flat fees, estate planning, and real estate can generate revenue that looks healthy while hiding the fact that some practice areas consume far more attorney time than the fees justify — without tracking, it is impossible to know where to focus.
The Opportunity
A simple case-type profitability analysis — revenue per matter type vs. hours invested — can immediately reveal where the firm should direct its marketing spend and intake effort to maximize income per hour worked, without adding a single new case.
