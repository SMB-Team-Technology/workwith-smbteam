Here Is Where Just Jurist Law Firm Stands Today

Pablo Gonzalez Zepeda has built Just Jurist Law Firm, PLLC into a profitable Miami HOA and condominium association practice serving approximately 40 associations — entirely without advertising, through inherited client books and years of word-of-mouth reputation. The firm runs profitably with a 3-person team, but Pablo is the nucleus of every board meeting, every client relationship, and every decision; the single most critical finding is that the firm is invisible online despite serving one of the most HOA-dense markets in the country, and that invisibility, combined with a team that cannot operate without Pablo, is the precise thing standing between where he is today and the autonomous firm he wants to own.

Stage 6 is the destination where the firm runs without Pablo — where evenings are his own, where board meetings happen without him, and where the practice he has built finally works for him instead of the other way around.

Where You Are Today

Stage 3: Solo Practitioner
  • All new clients come from inherited books and word-of-mouth — no system to generate predictable new association relationships
  • Pablo personally attends HOA and condo board meetings most evenings, consuming time that cannot scale or be delegated to the current team
  • The current attorney and paralegal require constant direction — the firm does not function autonomously when Pablo steps back
  • Zero digital presence — Just Jurist is invisible online despite serving 40 associations in one of the most HOA-dense markets in the country

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The distance between where Pablo is today — working evenings, indispensable to every decision — and where he wants to be is not a question of talent or hard work: it is a question of systems and people, and both can be built.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Just Jurist Law Firm, PLLC
Just Jurist is operating at Stage 3 — a profitable practice built almost entirely on Pablo's personal relationships, availability, and reputation rather than on repeatable systems or a self-managing team. The single most urgent constraint is the combination of zero digital presence and complete owner dependency: the firm cannot attract new associations without Pablo's personal network, and it cannot be run without him in the room. Pablo's goal — a firm that operates autonomously so he gets his evenings and his freedom back — requires building both a lead engine and a self-directing leadership structure, and neither currently exists.
