But they can be — and the roadmap to get there is clear.

1
Board meetings happen without you — your associate handles routine governance, and you show up only for the matters that genuinely require the founding attorney. Your evenings are yours again.
2

3
Just Jurist runs on systems and a team with real autonomy — Pablo reviews, directs, and decides at the level he chooses. The firm does not stop when he steps back. It grows.

This is not a fantasy. SMB Team can make this a reality for Just Jurist Law Firm.
