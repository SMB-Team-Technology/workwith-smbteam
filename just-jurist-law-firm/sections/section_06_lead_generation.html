Lead Generation

Just Jurist Law Firm has built a stable 40-association client base entirely on inherited relationships and word-of-mouth — without a single dollar of paid advertising and without an active online presence of any kind. That is a credit to Pablo's reputation, but it means every new association client depends on luck, not a system — and that is not a foundation for the autonomous firm Pablo wants to build.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Website runs on Clio Grow — a platform built for client management, not search engine visibility; competitors on custom websites will outrank it for every HOA/condo law keyword in Miami
  • PageSpeed scores: Could not be verified during research — Google API was rate-limited; scores must be run manually at pagespeed.web.dev before the next call
  • Contact form is buried in the footer — the only conversion path on the site requires a visitor to scroll through the entire page; there is no above-fold form, phone number, or scheduling tool visible on arrival
  • No video, no practice-area-specific content, and no geo-targeting pages for surrounding Miami-Dade communities (Hialeah, Coral Gables, Doral, Kendall) where condo associations are concentrated
Blind Spot: The address listed on the Just Jurist website (7550 NW 25th Street) differs from the address associated with Pablo's personal firm in attorney directories (13386 SW 128th Street). NAP inconsistency across online listings is one of the leading causes of Google Business Profile suppression — this mismatch may be actively preventing the firm from appearing in local search results.
The Positive
  • "Book a Consult" CTA is present and visible in the navigation — a clear next step for any visitor who reaches the site
  • Both attorney bios are live with credentials, law school, and years of practice — providing baseline professional trust for visitors who do find the site
The Gap
  • Any HOA board member who finds the site without a referral encounters no above-fold contact option, no chat, and no immediate reason to act — most will leave and call the next firm on the list
  • The Clio Grow platform's limited SEO capability means Just Jurist cannot compete for organic rankings; a competitor on WordPress or a purpose-built legal site will rank above it for every practice area search
  • Without geo-targeting landing pages, Just Jurist cannot capture the full Miami-Dade County demand for HOA/condo legal counsel from boards in surrounding communities who are searching for local representation
A website that cannot be found and does not convert is not a marketing asset — it is the single biggest thing keeping Pablo tied to referrals and personal relationships for new business.

  • Association Law Group (ALG) — despite their 2.9-star rating, their size and visibility gives them advantages in review count; however, LSA quality signals heavily weight star rating, meaning their poor reputation is a structural weakness in LSA rankings
The Opportunity

  • LSA leads for HOA/condo association law in Miami typically cost $75–$300 per contact — far below the annual value of a single new association retainer ($9,000+), making the ROI immediate from the very first booked call
