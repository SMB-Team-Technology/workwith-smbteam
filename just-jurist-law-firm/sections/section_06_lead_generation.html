Lead Generation

Just Jurist Law Firm, PLLC operates a profitable practice of 40 Miami condo associations built entirely on referrals and inherited books — without a single paid ad, an active Google Business Profile, or any online visibility for the keywords their ideal clients are searching right now. In a market where multiple well-funded competitors invest heavily across every digital channel, this firm is functionally invisible to every potential new association client.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Site runs on Clio Grow, a hosted legal platform with inherent SEO limitations — competitors on WordPress or custom-built sites have a structural advantage in organic rankings that no amount of content can fully overcome on this platform
  • PageSpeed scores: Not available during research (API rate-limited) — must be run at pagespeed.web.dev before the next meeting; Clio Grow-hosted sites vary widely and frequently score below 50 on mobile
  • Contact form is buried in the footer only — a visitor who does not use the above-fold booking button must scroll to the bottom of the page to find the next contact option
  • No geo-targeting landing pages for any Miami suburb — no pages for Hialeah, Coral Gables, Doral, Kendall, or Homestead, leaving search visibility for every surrounding HOA-heavy community uncaptured
  • No video content, no testimonial section, no attorney credential showcase — nothing to build trust or differentiate Pablo's 18 years of HOA/condo expertise before a prospect decides to call
Blind Spot: Clio Grow's hosted platform limits SEO at the server and technical level — even with strong content, the platform architecture prevents the custom meta structures, page speed optimization, and schema markup that drive local pack rankings. Competitors with custom-built sites are competing on a different playing field.
The Positive
  • "Book a Consult" is prominently placed above the fold and links directly to Clio's scheduling tool — the primary CTA is clear and functional
  • Bilingual English/Spanish content is present and a genuine competitive differentiator in Miami-Dade's condo association market
The Gap
  • Without a contact path above the fold beyond the booking button, visitors who are not yet ready to book a consult leave the site with no other engagement option — a second touchpoint (contact form above the fold) converts this group
  • The absence of geo-targeting pages means Just Jurist is invisible in local search for the dozens of HOA-heavy Miami-Dade communities — Hialeah alone has hundreds of condo associations; none of them see this firm when they search
  • A site with no video, no testimonials, and no credential showcase cannot compete for trust with competitors who have all three — and in a referral-to-digital transition, trust content is what converts a visitor into a call
A website that works while Pablo is in a board meeting converts every visitor into a potential next client — without requiring his personal involvement to close the deal.

The review base Just Jurist builds now directly determines LSA eligibility and ranking quality; starting that build immediately closes the gap with firms that have had months of head start.
