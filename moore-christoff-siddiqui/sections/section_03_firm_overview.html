Here Is Where Moore Christoff & Siddiqui Stands Today

Moore Christoff & Siddiqui is a ten-attorney law firm in Alexandria and Fairfax, Virginia handling criminal defense (their primary practice), family law, and civil litigation. With three co-equal partners, 11 staff, and revenue approaching $2 million, the firm has built real infrastructure — but its lead generation depends on organic traffic and referrals, with no paid advertising system in place, leaving growth exposed to competitor momentum in one of the country's most competitive legal markets.

Stage 6 is the stage where Sam can be the CEO of a firm that runs without him — generating predictable criminal defense leads in Fairfax and Alexandria even when he's deep in trial.

Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Moore Christoff & Siddiqui
Moore Christoff & Siddiqui is operating at Stage 4 — a growing law firm with real infrastructure but no predictable lead generation system driving its most profitable practice area. The most urgent gap is the absence of paid advertising in a Northern Virginia criminal defense market where three well-funded competitors are actively capturing DUI and high-value misdemeanor leads across every digital channel. Sam's goal of reaching $2.8M–$3.0M by end of 2027 is achievable — but not without a system that generates cases independently of partner availability.
