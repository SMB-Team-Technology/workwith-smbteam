Lead Generation

Moore Christoff & Siddiqui has built a 10-attorney firm approaching $2 million in revenue — but its lead generation rests almost entirely on organic Maps traffic and referrals, with no paid advertising system in place across any digital channel. Meanwhile, three well-funded competitors are actively running PPC, LSA, and social campaigns in the exact Northern Virginia criminal defense and family law markets the firm wants to own.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • No call-to-action above the fold for a consultation — visitors land on the homepage without a clear, immediate next step to contact the firm
  • PageSpeed scores were not available at the time of this research — verify at pagespeed.web.dev before the proposal call
  • Contact form is on a separate page only — a prospect who wants to reach out from the homepage must navigate to find the form, adding friction at the most critical conversion point
  • No geo-targeted landing pages for Alexandria or Fairfax — the firm's two primary markets have no dedicated pages optimized for local criminal defense searches in either city
Blind Spot: Price Benowitz operates a separate branded domain (virginia-criminallawyer.com) specifically to capture Northern Virginia DUI and criminal defense searches — a tactic that lets them rank and convert under a different brand while their main site holds its own positions. Without geo-targeted landing pages or a comparable campaign structure, Moore Christoff & Siddiqui is competing against a firm that effectively has two websites in the same market.
The Positive
  • The website is modern, mobile-responsive, and copyright 2026 — it presents the firm professionally and does not need to be rebuilt from scratch
  • Ten attorney bios are published — this depth of team presentation builds credibility and supports local SEO through individual attorney name searches
The Gap
  • A homepage with no above-fold CTA loses prospects who will not scroll — in a criminal defense market where urgency is high, the first ten seconds matter
  • Without city-specific landing pages, the firm cannot rank for "DUI attorney Fairfax" or "criminal defense Alexandria" — searches that represent the firm's highest-value case type in its two primary markets
  • Competitors with optimized landing pages for each city will continue to capture these searches while the firm's single-site structure leaves them invisible
A website that generates cases without Sam's involvement starts with a homepage that converts visitors into consultations — right now, it is not doing that job.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • Google Business Profile primary category is set to "Law Firm" rather than "Criminal Defense Lawyer" — this directly suppresses the profile's visibility in Maps results for the firm's highest-value case type, a gap Sam identified himself on the call
  • Moore Christoff & Siddiqui does not appear in the local 3-pack for any criminal defense or family law search term tested in Alexandria or Fairfax — the firm is effectively invisible in the most prominent position on Google for local searches
  • Missing secondary GBP categories including Divorce Attorney, Mediation Service, and Business Lawyer — reducing the total surface area of searches the profile can appear for
  • NAP and hours inconsistency confirmed across directories — conflicting information weakens the local authority signals Google uses to rank profiles in Maps
Blind Spot: The Fairfax office opened at the end of 2025 and likely does not yet have a fully built-out GBP with its own reviews, categories, and citation presence — meaning the firm is competing in a new, high-priority market without the local signals needed to show up there. Building the Fairfax profile from scratch now, while it is still early, is significantly easier than trying to recover ground after competitors consolidate their positions.
The Positive
  • 44 reviews at 5.0 stars is a strong foundation — a perfect rating, even at lower volume, establishes trust with prospects who do see the profile and gives the firm a credible base to build from with a systematic review generation program
The Gap
  • With the wrong primary category, the profile will not surface for "DUI attorney Alexandria" or "criminal defense lawyer Fairfax" — the searches most likely to result in a high-value retained case
  • At 44 reviews versus PNA Law's 103 and Price Benowitz's 422+, the firm is significantly outmatched on review volume — a factor that directly affects both Maps ranking and prospect click-through decisions
Medvin Law (Marina Medvin) — holds strong Maps positions in Alexandria for DUI and serious misdemeanor searches with 81 reviews at a perfect 5.0 stars, a fully optimized GBP profile under "Criminal Defense Lawyer," and a nationally recognized reputation that generates consistent organic traffic. She is the dominant organic presence in the Alexandria criminal defense market that Moore Christoff & Siddiqui is trying to own.
Fixing the GBP category and building a review generation system is one of the fastest, lowest-cost paths to more criminal defense leads — and it does not require Sam to be personally involved once the system is running.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • Moore Christoff & Siddiqui does not appear to be running Google search ads — Sam confirmed on the call that no PPC campaigns are currently active
  • With no paid search presence, the firm is absent from the top of the results page for searches like "DUI attorney Alexandria" and "criminal defense lawyer Fairfax" — the highest-intent, highest-value searches in their market
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Price Benowitz LLP — running paid search ads across both their main domain and their DBA domain (virginia-criminallawyer.com), capturing Northern Virginia DUI and criminal defense traffic at two entry points simultaneously
  • PNA Law (Patrick N. Anderson & Associates) — confirmed PPC presence in both Alexandria and Fairfax markets, running alongside their dominant LSA position to occupy multiple placements on the same results page
  • NovaDefenders — active in paid search for Northern Virginia criminal defense terms, adding a third competitor above the organic results before Moore Christoff & Siddiqui appears anywhere
The Opportunity
  • "DUI attorney Alexandria VA" and "DUI lawyer Fairfax VA" — the firm's highest-value case type, averaging $4,800 per case, in both of their primary markets
  • "Criminal defense lawyer Alexandria" and "criminal defense attorney Fairfax" — broad criminal defense terms that capture felony, misdemeanor, and reckless driving leads across both cities
  • Bidding on competitor brand names — prospects searching for PNA Law or Price Benowitz by name can be intercepted with a targeted ad before they reach the competitor's site
  • Reckless driving terms — at $1,200 average case value and high search volume in Northern Virginia's traffic corridor, reckless driving campaigns can generate consistent volume at lower cost-per-click than DUI terms

Local Service Ads

LSA Presence
  • Moore Christoff & Siddiqui has an LSA account in progress, set up in-house by the partners — but the account is not yet verified or active, meaning the firm is not appearing in the Google Screened results at the top of the page
  • Until the account is verified and the Google Screened badge is earned, the firm will not receive LSA leads regardless of budget — and every day it remains inactive is a day competitors are collecting pay-per-lead calls the firm cannot get back
Competitor LSA Landscape
  • PNA Law (Patrick N. Anderson & Associates) — confirmed active LSA presence in Alexandria and Fairfax, appearing at the very top of Google search results above all paid ads and the map pack
  • Price Benowitz LLP / Karin Riley Porter — active in LSA for Northern Virginia criminal defense, holding a top position in the placement that generates the highest-intent, lowest-friction calls
  • Medvin Law and NovaDefenders — also observed in LSA results for Alexandria criminal defense searches, further reducing the available top positions for a new entrant
The Opportunity
  • The LSA account is already in progress — completing verification is the single fastest path to appearing at the very top of Google for criminal defense searches in Alexandria and Fairfax
  • LSAs appear above everything else on Google — above paid ads, above the map pack, above all organic results — and you only pay when a real prospect calls you directly.
  • With 44 reviews at 5.0 stars, Moore Christoff & Siddiqui has the rating foundation to compete in LSA immediately upon verification — the bottleneck is completing the setup process, not the underlying credibility
Blind Spot: In-house LSA setup by partners — rather than by someone with LSA verification experience — is a common reason accounts stall at the background check and license verification stage. If the account has been sitting unverified for weeks, it is worth diagnosing exactly where in the verification process it is stuck, rather than waiting for Google to resolve it on its own.

Facebook & Instagram Ads

Ad Presence
  • No active Meta ads were observed for Moore Christoff & Siddiqui on Facebook or Instagram — the firm does not appear to be running paid social campaigns in either Alexandria or Fairfax
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Price Benowitz LLP — active Facebook presence with ongoing ad campaigns, using social media to stay visible to Northern Virginia audiences outside of search intent moments
  • NovaDefenders — active Facebook page with paid social activity, capturing awareness-stage prospects before they reach Google
The Opportunity
  • Retargeting — website visitors who did not convert are leaving with no follow-up
  • Video ads featuring the attorney — builds trust before a prospect ever calls
  • Educational content ads — capture leads earlier in the decision process
  • DUI and reckless driving awareness campaigns targeted to Northern Virginia zip codes — criminal defense clients often don't search immediately after an arrest, making social retargeting a way to stay in front of prospects during the days-long decision window before they hire an attorney
