Here's What's Possible for The Kaiser Law Group

Dan Kaiser has spent nearly three decades building a reputation that most attorneys in Flagstaff will never match — and he did it without a single paid ad, without a formal intake system, and without ever fully stepping back from the work. The question is not whether he has earned the firm he is describing. He has. The question is whether his systems are built to match what he has already built in reputation. They are not yet. But they can be — and the timeline he outlined is achievable.

1
Five new criminal defense cases arrive every month from paid ads — predictably, targeted, and qualified — and Dan's daughter is funded from day one of her career at the firm, not from Dan's personal savings.
2
Dan works 20 hours a week. His daughter handles the cases. The intake system answers at 11pm without Dan's phone ringing. He is not available 24/7 anymore — the firm is.
3
Three years from now, The Kaiser Law Group bills $3M annually with three attorneys, a full support team, and a profit plan that tells Dan exactly what he earns and what the firm is worth — a real business he could sell, pass on, or simply step back from whenever he chooses.

This is not a fantasy. SMB Team can make this a reality for The Kaiser Law Group.
