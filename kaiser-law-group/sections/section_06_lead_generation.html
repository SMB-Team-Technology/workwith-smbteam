Competitor Ad Landscape
  • DM Cantor — Phoenix-based criminal defense firm with a dedicated Flagstaff landing page. Likely running aggressive statewide PPC campaigns that capture Flagstaff DUI and drug crime searches with major-market budgets.
  • Griffen & Stevens Law Firm PLLC — Dominant in organic and map results; likely running complementary paid campaigns across criminal defense and PI given their marketing investment and 294-review brand authority.
  • Goldberg & Osborne — Statewide PI advertiser with a Flagstaff office; runs aggressive Google Ads campaigns for personal injury across Arizona markets.
The Opportunity
  • Obvious: Target "DUI lawyer Flagstaff," "drug crime attorney Flagstaff," and "domestic violence defense Flagstaff" — the three case types Dan named as his primary revenue focus, at approximately $5,000 average case value
  • Obvious: Dayparting ads to run during evening and weekend hours when DUI arrests peak — reducing wasted spend on low-intent daytime queries and concentrating budget on the moments when people actually need a criminal defense attorney
  • Non-obvious: Negative keyword lists excluding "free lawyer," "public defender," "cheap DUI" and similar low-intent terms — the exact targeting approach Dan said would make him willing to try paid ads again
  • Non-obvious: Personal injury PPC targeting "car accident attorney Flagstaff" and "personal injury lawyer Northern Arizona" to grow the PI practice Dan said he wants to expand over time
Blind Spot: The CPAs Dan experienced with his old LSA were high in part because there was no case-type filtering or negative keyword strategy. The national average CPA for criminal defense calls is approximately $137 per call — but without targeting, firms pay that price for calls from people who cannot afford representation. With proper segmentation by case type, income signal keywords, and dayparting, the real cost per qualified DUI or drug case lead drops significantly.
