Here's What's Possible for Grey Lex, LLC

Clay built Grey Lex from nothing to $300,000 in 18 months on the strength of personal skill, a relentless sales instinct, and a willingness to outwork the competition. That foundation is real — and it is also exactly what is keeping him from the next level. The version of Grey Lex that reaches $1M and runs statewide does not need Clay to be in the room for every consultation, every case, and every financial decision. It needs the systems, the team, and the structure that turn Clay's talent into something bigger than what one person can hold.

1
A trained intake team is fielding every inbound call, qualifying every lead, and re-engaging every no-show — and Clay is not involved until the client is already scheduled and ready to sign. The 50% of leads that used to go cold are now converting, and the pipeline fills itself without Clay stopping legal work to take a sales call.
2
Grey Lex is generating $750,000 to $1,000,000 per year and Clay's personal calendar has fewer obligations than it did at $300,000 — not more. A Ch. 13 attorney handles the cases Clay used to decline. A COO-level operator runs the day-to-day. Clay manages the business, not the workload, and his income is no longer limited by how many hours he personally works.
3
Grey Lex is a statewide operation — a recognized brand across Lafayette, Baton Rouge, and North Louisiana — with a replicable marketing and intake model that scales to every market Clay enters. Clay steps into the role he described: manager, not practitioner. The firm can run without him. And if he ever chooses to sell it, it is worth something because it does not depend on him showing up.

This is not a fantasy. SMB Team can make this a reality for Grey Lex, LLC.
