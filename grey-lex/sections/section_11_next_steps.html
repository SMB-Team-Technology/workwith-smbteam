Install No-Show Rescue Automation
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
When a prospect misses a scheduled consultation, there is currently no recovery sequence. That missed appointment slot represents a warm lead who engaged, booked, and then went cold — and without a re-engagement touch, that prospect is almost certainly calling a competitor next. There is no no-show rescue, no confirmation reminder, and no same-day callback trigger in place.
The Opportunity
A two-step re-engagement sequence — an immediate "we missed you" text followed by a same-day callback attempt — recovers a meaningful share of missed consultations at no incremental cost. A prospect who no-showed is still a warm lead; the firm just needs a system to reach back before the competition does.
