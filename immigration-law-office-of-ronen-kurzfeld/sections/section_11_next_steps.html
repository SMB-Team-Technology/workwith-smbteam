The current $5,000 CAD/month is competing for broad "immigration lawyer" terms against firms with 30+ years of domain authority — a campaign structure built around specific practice areas (Express Entry, spousal sponsorship, refugee claims, work permits) delivers 3–5× higher conversion rates at lower CPCs.

Create a Firm-Wide AI Use Policy
Team Quick Win
Why It Matters
AI is being used ad hoc across the firm without guardrails — in a legal context, this creates risk of fabricated AI-generated citations appearing in filed briefs, which exposes the firm to professional discipline, malpractice claims, and client harm.
The Opportunity
A simple acceptable use policy — defining what AI can and cannot be used for, and requiring verification steps for any cited case law or statute — eliminates the professional risk without eliminating the efficiency gains the team is already getting from AI tools.
