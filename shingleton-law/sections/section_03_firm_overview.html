Here Is Where Shingleton Law, PLLC Stands Today

Shingleton Law, PLLC is a Hampstead, NC solo practice less than one year old, founded by attorney Adam Shingleton with a strategic focus on non-litigious probate — an underserved market the firm is uniquely positioned to dominate through a proprietary tech platform that cuts case administration time from hours to minutes. At roughly $125,000 in annual revenue, the firm is generating cases almost entirely without paid advertising, which means every competitor with active Google ads or Local Service Ads is winning clients that Shingleton Law never even had the chance to compete for.

Stage 6 is where the firm runs without Adam — generating cases statewide, managing itself through trained staff and documented systems, and giving him the time freedom to live the life he built this firm to support.

Where You Are Today

Stage 3: Solo Practitioner
  • Growth depends almost entirely on organic search and referrals — no paid advertising system is generating a predictable case flow
  • Adam personally handles every initial consultation to maintain the firm's 90% conversion rate, leaving no room for delegation without risking revenue
  • The firm operates from a single Hampstead location while the goal is statewide NC expansion within 12 months
  • Estate planning still makes up a significant portion of current revenue despite the strategic plan to phase it out entirely in favor of probate

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The technology advantage is already built — what is missing is the marketing engine to fill the pipeline and the systems to handle the volume without Adam having to do it himself.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Shingleton Law, PLLC
Adam is operating at Stage 3 — a Solo Practitioner with a genuinely differentiated technology advantage and a high close rate, but no lead generation system outside of organic traffic and word of mouth. The single most urgent gap is the complete absence of paid advertising: Shingleton Law is not appearing in the local 3-pack for any probate keyword, has no Google Ads or LSA presence, and no Meta campaigns — while established local competitors like Blackburn & Ording and Barrington Law have years of review authority and digital visibility that Adam has yet to build. Without a paid lead generation system running now, the statewide expansion and $10M revenue goal Adam and Zach have set will not be achievable in the timeframe they are targeting.
