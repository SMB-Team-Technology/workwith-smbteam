The Positive

  • A dedicated attorney bio page for Adam Kemp is live, along with general "about" and "our approach" pages
The Gap
  • Without resolving the bot-verification wall, neither prospects nor search engines may be able to reliably reach the site's genuine assets
  • Real PageSpeed scores must be confirmed before committing to a rebuild-vs-repair scope
  • Every day the site stays hard to reach is another day of the 15-20 cases/month Adam used to sign before the hack
A website prospects and search engines can't reliably reach is a system Adam has to keep worrying about — not one that runs itself.
