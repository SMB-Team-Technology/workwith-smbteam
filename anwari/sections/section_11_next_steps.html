Quick Wins for Anwari Law Firm

Claim and Optimize Google Business Profile
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
The firm's GBP has never been claimed — 273 reviews at 4.6 stars are earning zero algorithmic credit in local pack ranking because the profile is unmanaged and cannot be optimized.
Who Is Doing It Better
Montagut & Sobral, PC — their 345 Google reviews paired with a fully claimed and managed profile gives them local pack priority over Anwari despite a lower 4.1-star rating.
The Opportunity
A claimed and optimized GBP with accurate NAP across both locations, active review responses, and regular posts can move Anwari from outside the local 3-pack to consistently inside it — capturing the leads currently going to competitors.
Launch Dari-Language Landing Pages
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters

Enroll in Google Local Service Ads
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
LSAs appear above every other result on Google — above paid search ads, above the local map pack, and above all organic listings. With 273 reviews at 4.6 stars, Anwari qualifies for Google Screened today but is not in that position.
Who Is Doing It Better
Montagut & Sobral, PC — their review volume and established firm history position them to hold LSA spots for Falls Church immigration searches; each spot they hold is a top-of-page position Anwari is not in.
The Opportunity
LSA enrollment puts Anwari at the very top of Google search results on a pay-per-call model — the firm only pays when a real prospect calls directly, and the Google Screened badge signals vetted credibility that immigration clients specifically look for when making high-stakes decisions about their status.
Move Consultation CTA Above the Fold
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
The free 15-minute telephonic consultation that converts at a high rate is not featured prominently on the homepage — website visitors are arriving from search results and leaving without finding the firm's strongest conversion offer.
The Opportunity
Making the consultation CTA the first visible action on the homepage and all practice area pages would immediately increase the percentage of website visitors who take the first step toward becoming a client — turning existing traffic into more consultations without spending more on advertising.
Implement Post-Consultation Follow-Up Sequence
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Prospects who complete a free consultation but do not sign immediately have no structured follow-up — at $4,500+ average case value, each warm lead that goes cold without follow-up represents thousands in unrealized revenue.
The Opportunity
A structured 3-touch follow-up sequence after every consultation would recover a meaningful percentage of warm leads who needed more time to decide — generating additional signed clients from leads already in the funnel without requiring any new marketing spend.
Correct NAP Inconsistencies Across Directories
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
At least one confirmed address discrepancy exists between Justia (4900 Leesburg Pike Suite 401) and the current website (5250 Cherokee Ave Suite 423) — inconsistent citations signal to Google that the firm's information is unreliable, directly hurting local search ranking.
The Opportunity
Correcting and standardizing name, address, and phone number across all directory listings — Avvo, Justia, Yelp, BBB, ReachAttorneys, Birdeye — removes a confirmed negative local SEO signal and strengthens geographic visibility for both the Falls Church and Alexandria locations.
Build Associate Attorney Hiring Plan
Team Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
Semi-retirement in 8–10 years requires the firm to run without Deeba's daily involvement — which means an associate attorney who can carry the caseload must be hired and trained well before the target date. Every year without starting is a year the timeline extends.
The Opportunity
A structured hiring plan for a first associate attorney — including compensation model, training timeline, and delegation framework — is the single most consequential long-term investment Anwari Law Firm can make toward Deeba's stated legacy goal.
Establish Practice Area Profit Tracking
Profit Quick Win
Why It Matters
The firm serves both immigration and business law clients but has no stated tracking of profitability by practice area — without this, marketing budget cannot be directed toward the cases that actually produce the highest return.
The Opportunity
A simple monthly profit and loss breakdown by practice area would identify which case types and client segments generate the best margins — enabling smarter case selection and marketing focus as the firm works toward the $1.5M annual goal.
