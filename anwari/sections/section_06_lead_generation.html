Lead Generation

Anwari Law Firm has built a 13-year reputation and a 273-review Google presence on organic search and Afghan community referrals alone — without a single dollar of paid advertising since a poor experience in 2013. The firm's competitors are actively investing across Google Ads, Local Service Ads, and social media while Anwari remains invisible on every paid channel in a market where visibility directly determines which firm a prospective client calls first.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Site design is estimated 6–8 years old with a logo copyright dating to 2018–2019 — the dated aesthetic directly contradicts Deeba's stated goal of projecting a modern, authoritative practice to prospective clients
  • PageSpeed scores: Mobile and desktop scores require manual verification at pagespeed.web.dev — API was rate-limited during research; based on site age and design patterns, mobile scores are estimated in the 30–55 range
  • No Dari-language content or Afghan community-specific landing pages — the firm's primary client differentiation is entirely invisible to prospective clients searching in Dari or seeking an Afghan attorney
  • No review widget or testimonial display — the firm's 273 Google reviews at 4.6 stars, a major trust asset, are not visible anywhere on the website
Blind Spot: Competitors may be bidding on "Anwari Law Firm" as a keyword in Google Ads, routing people who search for the firm by name to competing attorneys before they ever reach anwarilaw.com.
The Positive
  • 273 Google reviews at 4.6 stars represent substantial social proof that a modernized, conversion-optimized site could actively display and leverage
  • Website structure covers all core practice areas — employment-based, family-sponsored, asylum, green cards — the content foundation exists and needs modernization, not a complete rebuild from scratch
The Gap
  • A dated, likely slow-loading site in a market where competitors have professional, conversion-optimized properties means prospective clients who find Anwari through search are arriving — and leaving — without converting
  • Without Dari-language pages, the firm cannot capture any search volume from prospective Afghan clients who search in their native language — a niche where Anwari has no competition and currently zero digital presence
  • No review display means the firm's most powerful trust signal is working for competitors in Google results but doing nothing once a prospect actually lands on the website
A modern, authority-building website that speaks directly to the Afghan community is not just a design upgrade — it is the digital foundation that makes the self-sustaining practice Deeba is building toward actually possible.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • Google Business Profile has never been claimed by the business owner — an unclaimed profile cannot be managed, optimized, or used to post updates, respond to reviews, or maintain accurate hours and locations
  • At least one confirmed NAP inconsistency: Justia lists a different Alexandria address (4900 Leesburg Pike Suite 401) versus the current website address (5250 Cherokee Ave Suite 423) — inconsistent citations are a confirmed negative signal for local search ranking
  • No geo-targeting landing pages: with two offices (Falls Church and Alexandria) and a Northern Virginia service area, the firm has zero city-specific pages targeting Arlington, McLean, Vienna, Reston, or other high-value zip codes
Blind Spot: An unclaimed GBP creates a secondary risk most owners don't see: competitors or third parties can suggest edits to an unclaimed profile — and Google can accept them — potentially changing the firm's address, phone number, or category without Deeba's knowledge.
The Positive
  • 273 Google reviews at 4.6 stars already exceeds most local competitors by review count — once the GBP is claimed and actively managed, those reviews immediately become a stronger local pack ranking signal
The Gap
  • Every month the GBP remains unclaimed is a month competitors with claimed, actively managed profiles receive algorithmic priority in local pack results for the exact searches Anwari should be winning
  • Without Dari-language landing pages targeting searches like "Afghan immigration attorney Northern Virginia," the firm cannot capture any of the niche search volume it should dominate without competition
Montagut & Sobral, PC — their 345 Google reviews paired with a fully managed GBP gives them both a review volume advantage and active local search authority; claiming and optimizing Anwari's GBP is the first step to competing for the same local pack positions.
Claiming and optimizing the GBP is one of the fastest visibility improvements available to an established firm — and it is a prerequisite for every paid channel that follows.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • Anwari Law Firm does not appear to be running Google Search ads for "immigration attorney Falls Church," "immigration lawyer Fairfax Virginia," or employment-based immigration keywords — the firm may be getting outbid by competitors or may not currently be configured for paid search
  • Based on the transcript, no paid advertising has run since a difficult experience in 2013 — the paid search market has changed entirely in 13 years; modern campaign management, targeting, and tracking are fundamentally different from what existed then
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Law Office of Federico Serrano (immigrationabogado.com) — appeared with a dedicated landing page for "Falls Church immigration lawyer" with professional ad copy offering a free consultation, indicating active paid search investment for the firm's primary target keyword
  • Lightman Law Firm — appeared prominently for Northern Virginia immigration searches with paid landing pages targeting both family-based and employment immigration keywords
The Opportunity
  • High-intent family immigration keywords: "immigration attorney Falls Church VA," "immigration lawyer Fairfax County," "family immigration attorney Alexandria VA," "asylum lawyer Northern Virginia" — all terms with clear commercial intent and zero current Anwari paid presence
  • Employment immigration terms: "H-1B visa lawyer Northern Virginia," "employment immigration attorney Fairfax," "EB-1 visa attorney Virginia" — high-value employer-client keywords where Anwari's services are directly competitive but the firm has no paid visibility
  • Dari-language search terms: targeting Afghan community searches in Dari for immigration attorneys in Virginia — virtually zero competition; Anwari is the only local firm positioned to own this search segment entirely
  • Competitor name targeting: campaigns bidding on searches for Montagut Sobral and Rodriguez Legal would capture high-intent comparison shoppers evaluating the market before choosing a firm
Blind Spot: The free 15-minute telephonic consultations that convert at a high rate are a powerful Google Ads call-to-action — most competitors offer only generic "free consultations." A campaign leading with "Free 15-minute immigration assessment — no obligation" directly against high-intent immigration searches would convert at a premium rate compared to generic competitor ads.

Local Service Ads

LSA Presence
  • Anwari Law Firm does not appear to be enrolled in Google Local Service Ads for immigration law and does not appear to hold a Google Screened badge for its practice areas
  • With 273 Google reviews at 4.6 stars, the firm already meets the review threshold to qualify for LSA enrollment — the opportunity cost of non-enrollment is significant given what the top LSA position represents
Competitor LSA Landscape
  • Montagut & Sobral, PC — with 345 Google reviews and 35+ years of established firm history, they are strongly positioned to hold LSA placements for Falls Church immigration searches; every spot they hold is a spot Anwari is not in
  • Rodriguez Legal, PLC — their 4.9-star rating qualifies them for Google Screened placement; their near-perfect trust score creates a strong trust signal at the top of search results
The Opportunity
  • LSA enrollment would place Anwari's name, rating, and phone number at the very top of Google search results — above all paid search ads, above the local map pack, and above all organic results — for immigration searches across the Northern Virginia market
  • LSAs appear above everything else on Google — above paid ads, above the map pack, above all organic results — and you only pay when a real prospect calls you directly.
  • With 273 verified reviews at 4.6 stars, Anwari meets the review quality threshold to qualify for Google Screened — the badge signals vetted credibility that immigration clients specifically seek out when making high-stakes legal decisions about their status
Blind Spot: LSA enrollment for attorneys requires an annual background check and state bar verification — the enrollment process takes 4–8 weeks. Starting the enrollment process now means being visible before a competitor in the same position completes theirs first.
