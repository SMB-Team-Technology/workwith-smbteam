Here Is Where Berger Law Stands Today

Berger Law is Michigan's Upper Peninsula criminal defense firm — the only practice in the U.P. focused exclusively on criminal law — handling DUI, drug crimes, sex offenses, gun charges, and federal cases for clients across all 15 U.P. counties. Founded in 2021, the firm has built a genuine market position: 4 attorneys, 88 five-star Google reviews, and 15–20 new cases coming in every month. The single most important finding is that Berger Law is quietly losing an estimated $5,000–$10,000 per month in revenue it already earned — not because of a lack of leads, but because a consultation-to-hire rate that has slipped to 51% and a lead response time exceeding 20 minutes are sending signed cases to competitors.

Stage 6 is where a firm runs without its owner — and it is exactly the destination Alex described: working 10 hours a week, strategic not operational, with income that no longer depends on him personally being there.
