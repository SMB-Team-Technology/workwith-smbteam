Here Is Where The Law Office of Vernon Brownlee Stands Today

Stage 6 is where Vernon walks into every trial knowing his firm is running at full capacity behind him — not because he is managing every detail, but because the systems he has built are bringing him a steady flow of exactly the serious cases he wants to try.

The Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model

We Help Law Firm Owners Get More Profit, More Personal Freedom, And Outstanding Results For Their Clients.

Most law firms try to grow by chasing more leads. But leads alone are not the problem — and leads alone are not the answer. Here is what actually works.

Lead Generation

Leads are to a law firm what gasoline is to a car. Without them, you go nowhere. But a steady, predictable flow of leads changes everything — you get to choose your cases instead of taking whatever comes in.

Intake

But all the leads in the world are worthless without a system to turn them into paying clients. Most firms are losing cases they already paid for simply because their follow-up is slow or inconsistent.

Self-Managing Team

Once clients are coming in, you need a team — otherwise you end up working 90-hour weeks and results suffer. A self-managing team is what gives you the freedom to step back.

Profit Plan

And once the team is in place, most owners look at their bank account and ask: where did all the money go? That is what a profit plan solves — clarity on what you earn, what you keep, and how to grow both.

All four have to work together. If one is missing, you cannot have more profit, more freedom, and outstanding results for your clients at the same time.

Intake Assessment

The Law Office of Vernon Brownlee loses an estimated 2–5 signed cases per month not because of a lack of leads, but because the systems that should convert leads into signed clients are broken or missing entirely. Fixing intake means converting more of the leads the firm is already generating — without spending another dollar on marketing.

Status: RED — Critical Priority
The Problem
  • Contact form was broken by a prior agency for months — approximately 50 qualified leads received zero response during that period; current form integrity requires immediate verification before any marketing investment has a reliable path to ROI
  • No 24/7 answering service — after-hours criminal defense calls go unanswered despite arrests happening evenings and weekends; the firm is effectively closed for the 16 hours per day that produce the most urgent criminal defense inquiries
  • CallRail is configured for call tracking but not connected to Clio Grow — inbound call leads do not auto-create contact records, and follow-up depends entirely on whoever happens to take the call
  • No automated follow-up sequences — prospects who reach voicemail or submit a form receive no immediate text confirmation, no automated callback trigger, and no scheduled follow-up; the decision to hire typically happens within 24–48 hours of an arrest, and silence during that window means the case goes to whoever responded
Blind Spot: Criminal defense leads are among the most time-sensitive in any practice area. When someone is arrested or charged, they are making their hiring decision within hours — not days. Every missed after-hours call is not a delayed conversation; it is almost certainly a signed client for whoever picked up the phone first. There is no second chance in criminal defense intake.
The Positive
  • CallRail and Clio Grow are already in place — the infrastructure exists and just needs to be properly connected and automated; this is a configuration problem, not a procurement problem
  • Vernon's personal close rate is exceptionally strong — 70% of qualified, able-to-pay prospects sign when Vernon gets them on a call; the intake problem is getting leads to that call, not closing them once they are there
The Gap
  • 2–5 cases per month lost to web and intake failures × $5,000 average case value = $10,000–$25,000 in monthly revenue leaving the firm through entirely preventable gaps
  • ~25% of qualified prospects cannot afford fees with no financing option offered — at 7–8 prospects per month, adding LawPay or Synchrony could convert 2–3 additional cases monthly without a single new lead generated
  • After-hours missed calls likely represent 30–40% of all inbound opportunities in criminal defense — the most urgent, highest-converting clients call when they need help, not during business hours
Fixing intake is the single highest-ROI move available to this firm — it turns the reputation and organic presence Vernon has already built into the steady flow of serious cases he's working toward, without spending another dollar on marketing.
