Here Is Where Indiana Immigration Attorneys Stands Today

Indiana Immigration Attorneys is a first-generation solo immigration practice founded in May 2025 by Logansport native Libby Nelson — the only dedicated immigration attorney in north-central Indiana north of Indianapolis. After thirteen months in operation, the firm has earned a perfect 5.0 Google rating on 16 reviews and serves families across family-based immigration, asylum, DACA, and citizenship matters, but relies entirely on community word-of-mouth and Facebook presence to attract new clients, with no paid advertising and no predictable growth system in place.

Stage 6 is where Libby can serve twice as many families as she does today — without being the one handling every phone call, every intake, and every late-night deadline.

Where You Are Today

Stage 3: Solo Practitioner
  • Firm's only attorney personally handles all casework, consultations, and almost certainly intake
  • New clients arrive through word-of-mouth, community referrals, and Facebook — no paid or predictable lead system
  • 16 Google reviews vs. Indianapolis competitors with 100–792 reviews — nearly invisible in online search
  • Two-person team leaves zero capacity to grow without Libby working proportionally more hours

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The path from Solo Practitioner to Law Firm Owner is about building systems that let the firm serve more families — without requiring more of Libby.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Indiana Immigration Attorneys
Indiana Immigration Attorneys is operating at Stage 3 — a solo practice built on Libby Nelson's personal relationships and reputation in the community she grew up in. The most urgent gap is the absence of any predictable lead generation system: the firm is nearly invisible when north Indiana families turn to Google for immigration help, while Indianapolis competitors with hundreds of reviews and active ad campaigns capture those searches daily. Until that changes, the mission Libby came back to Logansport to fulfill — being the trusted resource her community deserves — will only reach the families who already know her name.
