Lead Generation

Couch Law Group is three weeks old and generating early revenue entirely from attorney referrals — there is no digital presence, no paid advertising, and no way for a prospective client in Jackson, Macon, Transylvania, or the surrounding counties to find this firm through any online channel. That is both the challenge and the opportunity: the market is wide open, and the firm that builds first wins.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Website is brand-new (launched approximately April 2026) — it is not indexed in any major search engine and returns no organic rankings for any practice area or city keyword
  • PageSpeed scores: Not obtainable — the site returned a 403 error during research, which typically indicates limited public availability or crawler-blocking on a new site; scores should be checked and optimized before any SEO or ad traffic is sent to the site
  • No evidence of practice area landing pages, attorney bios, or above-the-fold calls to action — the structural elements that turn a visitor into a lead are almost certainly missing
  • No contact form, no chat option, and no trackable intake path observed — a prospective client who lands on the site has no clear next step
Blind Spot: The website being new is not the same as being optimized. Many firms launch a site and assume the work is done — but a site that is not structured for conversion, not loaded with geo-targeted practice area content, and not technically fast will produce almost no organic leads even after six to twelve months of aging. The window to build it correctly is right now, before bad SEO habits get indexed.
The Positive
  • The domain couchlawgroup.com is live — the firm has a web presence to build from
  • Being new means there is no legacy code, outdated design, or entrenched bad structure to undo — the site can be built right from the start
The Gap
  • Every dollar spent on Google Ads or local SEO sends traffic to a site that is not yet equipped to convert visitors — without conversion-optimized pages, ad spend is partially wasted from day one
  • Competitors like Coward, Hicks & Siler have had decades to build domain authority in this market — without deliberate SEO investment starting now, Couch Law Group will be years behind on organic rankings
  • No geo-targeted landing pages for Cashiers, Highlands, Sylva, Franklin, Brevard, or Waynesville means the firm cannot show up for city-specific searches across the five-county footprint
A website that converts is not a marketing expense — it is the foundation every lead system sits on, and building it correctly now is what makes Jordan's $300k year possible without Jordan personally chasing every lead.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • No Google Business Profile exists for Couch Law Group — the firm has 0 reviews and does not appear in the local 3-pack for any practice area or city search in the five-county region
  • Jordan's existing Avvo attorney profile still lists Ridenour & Goss, PA as his firm — anyone who finds that profile sees the wrong firm name
  • No listings on Justia, FindLaw, Yelp, or Martindale under the Couch Law Group name — the firm is completely absent from every directory a prospective client might use to find a local attorney
  • No geo-targeting landing pages for surrounding cities — the firm cannot organically rank for searches from Sylva, Franklin, Brevard, Waynesville, or Murphy
Blind Spot: Google rewards recency of reviews, not just volume — a firm that earns 10 reviews in its first 90 days will outrank a firm with 30 old reviews in many local searches. There is a real first-mover advantage here: getting to 15–20 Google reviews before any competitor accelerates their review program positions Couch Law Group as the credibility leader before the comparison even happens.
The Gap
  • Every person in the five-county region who searches "personal injury attorney Cashiers NC" or "civil litigation attorney Highlands NC" right now sees Coward, Hicks & Siler, Henson & Hanks Law, and Ridenour & Goss — never Couch Law Group
  • Without a GBP, the firm cannot appear in Local Service Ads, cannot show on Google Maps, and cannot capture the growing number of clients who search for a lawyer on their phone while driving through the area
Coward, Hicks & Siler, PA — The dominant firm in this market with offices in Cashiers, Sylva, and Franklin. They hold 3-pack positions for civil litigation and general practice searches across the region with an established GBP showing 28+ aggregated reviews. Without a verified GBP, Couch Law Group cannot compete in any local search result.
Every week without a verified Google Business Profile is a week the five-county region's search traffic goes entirely to Coward, Hicks & Siler — and that is a week of revenue Jordan will never recover.

The longer Couch Law Group waits to establish paid search presence, the more likely those larger firms are to claim this territory first — at which point competing becomes significantly more expensive.
