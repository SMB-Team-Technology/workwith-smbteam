Here's What's Possible for Durak Law

Michal Durakiewicz has built something real over ten years — a credible practice, a perfect five-star reputation, and a community presence in Franklin that most attorneys never achieve. The question is not whether the foundation is there; it is whether the systems are built to turn that foundation into the practice he actually wants. Right now, they are not. But they can be — and the gap is smaller than it feels from inside a solo practice.

1
The firm generates a consistent, predictable flow of family law and criminal defense leads through paid systems — and Michal's job is to practice law and deliver results, not to figure out where the next client is coming from.
2
Intake is handled by a trained team member, not the attorney — new clients are being signed while Michal is in court, spending time with family, or taking the vacation he has not been able to take without the firm going quiet behind him.
3
The firm runs profitably and predictably on a documented financial plan — Michal knows exactly what comes in, what stays, and how each practice area is performing, so revenue growth actually produces the income and freedom he built this for.

This is not a fantasy. SMB Team can make this a reality for Durak Law.
