Here Is Where Durak Law Stands Today

Michal Durakiewicz has built Durak Law into a well-regarded solo practice in Franklin, TN since 2015, handling Family Law, Criminal Defense, and specialized matters including Education Law and Professional License Defense. After a decade of practice, the firm's 5.0-star Google reputation and geo-targeted landing pages are real assets — but without a paid lead generation system, every client the firm does not reach organically is being captured by a competitor who is paying to be there.

Stage 6 is where the firm generates its own leads, signs clients through a delegated process, and operates without requiring Michal to be involved in every step — so he can focus on practicing law and eventually have a life that reflects what he has built.

Where You Are Today

Stage 3: Solo Practitioner
  • Lead generation appears driven by organic presence and referrals — no observed paid advertising system in place
  • As a solo practitioner, client intake and case management likely involve the attorney at every step
  • The firm competes against multi-attorney practices and well-funded competitors with dedicated marketing infrastructure
  • Profitability is undocumented without a defined financial tracking system across six active practice areas

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours

  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between a 10-year solo practice and a firm that consistently generates, signs, and serves clients without owner dependency is bridgeable — but only with the right systems deliberately built in the right order.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Durak Law
Durak Law operates at Stage 3 — a Solo Practitioner with a strong local reputation and real digital foundation, but no observed paid lead generation system to translate that foundation into predictable growth. The most urgent gap is the complete absence of any paid acquisition channel, while named competitors including Jonathan Turner, Attorney and Rogers, Shea & Spanos are actively investing in Google Ads, LSA, and SEO to dominate the exact searches this firm needs to win.
