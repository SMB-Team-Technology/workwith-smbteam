The Positive

  • Attorney bios for all five attorneys, professional photography, and a video center are all active — content elements that build credibility and trust before a prospect ever calls
The Gap
  • Every month the blog goes without new content, the firm loses organic ground to competitors who publish consistently — search engines reward fresh, relevant content, and 17+ months of inactivity is a measurable disadvantage in a competitive family law market
  • A buried contact form means high-intent visitors who are ready to act have to work harder to find it — and in a market where emotional urgency drives decisions, friction loses cases
  • NAP inconsistencies across directories create conflicting signals for Google's local search algorithm — the firm may be leaving 3-pack rankings on the table due to data it can control
A conversion-optimized website is not just a marketing asset — it is the front door to the firm that will eventually be sold, and a poor digital first impression costs cases every single day.
