NAP Consistency Fix — Clean Up Directory Listings to Reclaim Local SEO Authority
Profit Quick Win
Why It Matters
Three different phone numbers appear across FindLaw, Avvo, and BBB — each one left over from a different call tracking number. Google uses NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across the web as a local search trust signal; inconsistency suppresses organic local rankings and maps authority with no ad spend benefit to show for it.
The Opportunity
Correcting all directory listings to match one canonical phone number is a one-time fix that produces permanent improvements to local search authority — it costs no ongoing budget and the SEO lift compounds over time as Google's entity confidence in the listing increases.
