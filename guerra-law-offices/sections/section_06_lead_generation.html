Lead Generation

Guerra Law Offices has built a $500,000 practice entirely on organic referrals, with no paid advertising, no review-generation program, and no predictable system for attracting new personal injury or real estate clients. Every named local competitor in Warwick and Providence has a verified digital presence Guerra does not yet have.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Design has been unchanged since at least May 2021 (5+ years), with the footer crediting a defunct-looking vendor
  • PageSpeed scores: Mobile 78/100 — Desktop 94/100
  • Not mobile responsive — no viewport meta tag, legacy table-based HTML structure
  • No call-to-action anywhere on the homepage, and the Contact page has no form at all — only a mailto link, phone, fax, and a static map image
Blind Spot: Stella's full attorney bio — 15+ years of experience, NY/RI/MA admissions, and her public-service background as a former RI State Representative and Woonsocket City Councilwoman — is buried on a separate About page. Visitors deciding whether to trust the firm in the first few seconds on the homepage never see it.
The Positive
  • Desktop PageSpeed performance score of 94/100 shows the underlying page loads efficiently — the issues are mobile responsiveness and conversion design, not raw speed
The Gap
  • No contact form means every visitor who isn't ready to call or email disappears with zero record — a lost lead with no way to follow up
  • No mobile responsiveness on a site serving mobile-heavy personal injury searches likely costs visitors before they ever see a phone number
  • No CTA above the fold means even interested visitors leave without a clear next step
A modern, mobile-first site with a real intake path is the first system standing between Stella and a firm that can run without her fielding every single inquiry personally.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • No confirmed online review presence — Avvo shows "Not yet reviewed" (0 reviews), Experience.com shows 0 reviews, and no Birdeye profile exists for the firm at all, despite 15+ years in practice
  • No profile found on Justia, FindLaw, or Lawyers.com/Martindale — real directory gaps versus every named competitor
  • An active NAP inconsistency: Facebook and the Juridipedia directory both list the firm as "Guerra Law Offices, LTD." at a different Providence address — a different city, suite, and legal suffix than the firm's actual Warwick location
Blind Spot: The Facebook/Juridipedia listing under a different name, suffix, and city is actively splitting whatever local search authority the firm has across two conflicting profiles — most firms never discover this without a dedicated audit.
The Positive
  • The bilingual/Spanish-language angle has no directly competing local firm identified in Warwick or Providence — a real, unclaimed local SEO wedge for Stella's personal injury and real estate practice areas
The Gap
  • Every month without a review-generation program widens the trust gap against competitors carrying 49 to 290 verified Google reviews
  • Missing directory listings mean fewer citations reinforcing local map rankings against firms with decades of directory presence
Rob Levine Law — 290 verified Google reviews at 4.9★ and a statewide brand presence dominating personal injury local search visibility in Warwick.
Local SEO is where trust gets built before a prospect ever calls — right now, that trust is being built for your competitors, not for you.
