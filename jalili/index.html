Here Is Where Jalili Law Group Stands Today

Jalili Law Group is a Thornhill, Ontario firm built primarily on criminal defense (85-90% of caseload), with family law and immigration work that often arrives bundled from a single case event, like a domestic violence matter touching all three. With a 6-7 person team, a COO already in place, and roughly $10K a month already flowing to a marketing agency, the firm has real infrastructure behind it — but its single biggest constraint right now is lead quality: the principal attorney is personally burning hours on free consultations with unqualified prospects instead of the higher-value private clients the firm needs to fund its stated $2M revenue goal and planned U.S. expansion.

Stage 6 is where the Canadian firm generates predictable profit without consuming Iman and Nasim's personal time — and it's the only foundation stable enough to fund and launch the U.S. firm you've already set in motion.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • A paid marketing agency drives real lead volume — 103 leads and $4.2K spent in July alone — but lead quality is inconsistent
  • The principal attorney personally takes free 30-minute consultations with unqualified prospects, burning hours that could go to higher-value private clients
  • A COO is already in place, but the firm still can't systematically convert its existing lead flow into the private-pay caseload needed to hit $2M
  • Three different phone numbers across the firm's own web properties fragment call tracking and dilute the marketing spend already being made

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
Closing that gap is what turns the $2M Canadian revenue goal — and the U.S. firm it's meant to fund — from an ambition into a plan.

The Law Firm Growth Acceleration Model

We Help Law Firm Owners Get More Profit, More Personal Freedom, And Outstanding Results For Their Clients.

Most law firms try to grow by chasing more leads. But leads alone are not the problem — and leads alone are not the answer. Here is what actually works.

Lead Generation

Leads are to a law firm what gasoline is to a car. Without them, you go nowhere. But a steady, predictable flow of leads changes everything — you get to choose your cases instead of taking whatever comes in.

Intake

But all the leads in the world are worthless without a system to turn them into paying clients. Most firms are losing cases they already paid for simply because their follow-up is slow or inconsistent.

Self-Managing Team

Once clients are coming in, you need a team — otherwise you end up working 90-hour weeks and results suffer. A self-managing team is what gives you the freedom to step back.

Profit Plan

And once the team is in place, most owners look at their bank account and ask: where did all the money go? That is what a profit plan solves — clarity on what you earn, what you keep, and how to grow both.

All four have to work together. If one is missing, you cannot have more profit, more freedom, and outstanding results for your clients at the same time.
