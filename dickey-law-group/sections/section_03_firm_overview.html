Here Is Where Dickey Law Group Stands Today

Dickey Law Group PLLC is a young, bilingual estate planning and probate boutique founded in 2021–2022 by husband-and-wife attorneys James and Mireya Dickey in The Woodlands, Texas — one of Houston's fastest-growing and most affluent suburbs. With 32 five-star Google reviews, an active bilingual blog, and membership in the Montgomery County Hispanic Chamber of Commerce, they have built exceptional early credibility — but the firm runs entirely on two people with no support staff and no paid lead generation system, capping growth at their personal capacity.

Stage 6 is where the firm serves its community and grows its revenue without requiring James or Mireya to be in every consultation, every intake call, and every client meeting — and for a young team building their future together, that kind of freedom is exactly what makes everything else possible.

Where You Are Today

Stage 3: Solo Practitioner
  • Lead flow depends entirely on organic search and word-of-mouth referrals — no paid acquisition system is in place
  • Both attorneys personally handle intake, casework, and firm operations simultaneously with no support staff
  • The firm has no documented processes, SOPs, or operational systems that could support future staff
  • Growth is capped at what two people can personally manage — every new client competes with every existing one

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between Stage 3 and the firm James and Mireya are building toward is not about talent or reputation — it is about systems, and those are exactly what we help build.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Dickey Law Group PLLC
Dickey Law Group is operating at Stage 3 — a Solo Practitioner stage despite having two attorneys, because both James and Mireya remain personally involved in every function from intake to client work with no support infrastructure in place. The single most urgent gap limiting their growth right now is the complete absence of a predictable lead generation system: with 100% organic traffic and zero paid advertising, the firm is entirely dependent on word-of-mouth and search rankings it does not fully control. Until that changes, the growth this talented, community-rooted bilingual firm is capable of will remain constrained by their personal capacity.
