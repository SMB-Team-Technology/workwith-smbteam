The Problem

A prospect whose first impression is a PI headline may click away before ever seeing the workers' comp or SSDI practice area pages deeper in the site.
The Positive

  • Practice area pages exist for PI, WC, SSDI, and insurance claims, and an attorney bio page is present — the structural bones are in place
The Gap
  • No blog content means no SEO authority accumulating — every month without published content is a month competitors extend their organic rankings lead
  • No video and no personal storytelling above the fold means conversion rates on existing web traffic are lower than they could be — prospects land and leave without connecting
  • Zero geo-targeting means the firm is invisible to prospects searching from Metro Jackson suburbs — lower-competition keyword sets that could generate cases at a fraction of the cost of core Jackson terms
Every prospect who lands on the website and does not call is a case that never contributes to the debt paydown — and fixing the site is the foundation that makes every other marketing dollar work harder.
