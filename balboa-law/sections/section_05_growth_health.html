Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • No website, no GBP, and no active advertising — Balboa Law does not exist online yet
  • Entering a market where top competitors have 1,000–2,400 Google reviews and multi-year PPC campaigns already running
  • Zero established lead flow; entire digital infrastructure must be built before revenue can begin
Without a predictable lead flow, the financial freedom Gerald is building toward will not materialize — and the window to establish it before competitors consolidate the market is narrowing.

Intake

  • No intake process, no intake technology, and no defined response system established at time of launch
  • Wife's role is intentionally limited — intake cannot rely on the attorney; automation and a defined escalation path are required from day one
  • In Houston PI, leads respond to speed: the firm that calls back first wins the case; there is no system yet to guarantee that response
An intake gap is not just a revenue problem — it is the single fastest way to pay for leads and lose them before they ever become cases, making the income goal impossible to reach.

Self-Managing Team

  • Gerald is the entire operation — marketing, BD, management, and firm oversight all fall on one person
  • No accountability structure, no org chart, and no one beyond Gerald to keep operations moving
  • The business model depends on automation replacing human team members, which is the right instinct — but that automation does not yet exist
The freedom Gerald is building toward — a firm that generates income without consuming his time — cannot exist until the team and automation systems are in place to replace his daily involvement.

Profit

  • Gerald has a clear first-year profit goal of $300K–$1M net — which is ambitious but shows strong financial intentionality
  • Marketing budget of $100K–$200K/year is a reasonable framework for the Houston PI market; it will need professional guidance to deploy effectively
  • No P&L, no financial tracking system, and no per-case cost visibility yet — profit plan must be built alongside the firm
Gerald knows what financial freedom looks like — the next step is building the profit structure that makes it a system, not a hope.
