Fix Website CTA and Contact Form Friction
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
The contact form is hidden inside a sliding panel drawer — not visible on the homepage. The above-fold CTA ("Discover More") directs visitors to services rather than capturing booking intent. Visitors who cannot find a way to reach the firm in under 5 seconds leave without converting.
The Opportunity
Moving the primary consultation CTA above the fold and making the contact form immediately visible — without requiring a click sequence — is the single highest-leverage website change available and directly increases the return on every future ad dollar spent.
Implement Intake Tracking and Lead Attribution
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters

The Opportunity
Proper intake tracking and lead attribution transforms every future marketing investment — the firm will know exactly what is working, what to scale, and what to cut before committing additional budget to any channel.
