Lead Generation

Supernus Business & Law Center has built a strong local reputation and an impressive 239-review aggregated rating — but that credibility is almost entirely invisible to the business owners and families actively searching online for an asset protection or estate planning attorney in DeKalb and Kane counties. The gap between the firm's quality and its digital footprint is the central lead generation problem this audit addresses.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Contact form is accessible only through the navigation menu — it is not visible above the fold, meaning a prospect who lands on the homepage and does not scroll has no obvious next step to take
  • PageSpeed scores could not be measured during research due to API limits and must be re-run at pagespeed.web.dev before the proposal call — mobile performance is critical for search ranking and conversion
  • No video content is present on the site — an attorney with national media appearances and speaking credentials is not leveraging that credibility visually, which is the fastest trust-builder available
  • Blog posts reference Kane County and Illinois broadly, but no standalone geo-targeting landing pages exist for the surrounding cities and towns Jedediah already serves
Blind Spot: The firm's headline — "Protect What You Have Built. Plan for What Comes Next." — is genuinely compelling and differentiated from the generalist messaging of Foster & Buick and Klein Stoddard. That message is currently only reaching people who already found the site. Paid ads and LSA would put it in front of business owners at the exact moment they are searching — amplifying what already works rather than replacing it.
The Positive
  • Mobile-responsive design with a clean modern layout and functioning navigation menu — a professional first impression for visitors who do find the site
  • Strong attorney bio with real credentials: former business professor, multi-million dollar entrepreneur, national media features, and a clear philosophy about practical, proactive legal planning that resonates with business-owner clients
The Gap
  • Every business owner or family in DeKalb and Kane County searching "asset protection attorney" or "estate planning attorney Sycamore" who cannot quickly find a contact option on the homepage is a potential client who bounces to a competitor
  • Without PageSpeed data confirmed, the site may be suffering ranking penalties for mobile load time — a slow-loading site is invisible in mobile search results regardless of content quality
  • No video means the attorney's unique story — entrepreneur who became a lawyer to protect people like his former clients — is never told to cold visitors, and that story is the firm's most powerful differentiator
A website that converts visitors into consultation requests is the foundation of every client protection plan Jedediah creates — it needs to work as hard as he does.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • Google-specific review counts could not be verified live during research — Birdeye aggregates 239 reviews at 5.0 stars, but the firm's actual Google review count and local 3-pack positions for estate planning, asset protection, and business law terms must be confirmed before the proposal call
  • NAP inconsistency identified: Avvo shows 735 Independence Ave while Birdeye and other directories show 212 S Main St — inconsistent address data across directories suppresses local search rankings and erodes Google's confidence in the listing
  • No active Google Business Profile posts or photos were observed, and no Martindale-Hubbell listing was found — missed signals that affect both local ranking and first-impression credibility when a prospect pulls up the GBP card
Blind Spot: The Avvo profile shows 0 client reviews while the Birdeye aggregate shows 239 — this means the firm's strongest review presence is not converting into Avvo visibility, which is one of the first places attorneys are researched by high-intent prospects. A single Avvo review campaign could meaningfully improve visibility on one of legal's highest-traffic directories.
The Positive
  • 239 aggregated reviews at 5.0 stars on Birdeye is an exceptional reputation signal — most solo practitioners in markets this size have a fraction of that review volume, and this is a meaningful competitive asset if it can be translated into Google and directory presence
The Gap
  • Until the NAP inconsistency is corrected across all directories, local search rankings for Sycamore and DeKalb County terms will be suppressed — prospects searching for what this firm offers will find it lower in results than its review quality warrants
  • Competitors with multi-attorney teams and longer web histories have more indexed content and more inbound links — without dedicated landing pages for each practice area and target city, the firm cannot close the organic authority gap
Foster & Buick Law Group LLC — Multi-attorney firm with 50+ years of indexed web history, directory citations, and a Reader's Choice award that generates local press coverage and backlinks — all of which feed into organic and local SEO authority that a newer solo firm cannot match through content alone.
Every business owner in DeKalb and Kane County who Googles "asset protection attorney" and does not find Jedediah is a client whose legacy is at risk — and an opportunity this firm cannot afford to leave on the table.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • Supernus Business & Law Center does not appear to be running Google search ads for asset protection, estate planning, business planning, or second-marriage asset protection searches — they may not be configured to show for these terms, or no campaigns have been launched
  • This covers all four practice areas: no paid visibility was observed for "asset protection attorney Sycamore IL," "estate planning attorney DeKalb County," "business attorney Sycamore IL," or "prenuptial agreement attorney Illinois"
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Klein Stoddard Buck & Lewis — does not appear to be running Google Ads for business attorney or estate planning terms in Sycamore; the local paid search market is currently quiet
  • Foster & Buick Law Group — does not appear to be running active Google search campaigns for local practice area terms; no ad copy was observed in research
The Opportunity
  • "Asset protection attorney Sycamore IL" and "asset protection attorney DeKalb County IL" — Supernus is the only specialist in this niche locally, and there appears to be no local competition bidding on these terms
  • "Estate planning attorney Sycamore IL," "will and trust attorney DeKalb County," and "business planning attorney northern Illinois" — high-intent terms where first-mover paid search advantage is available right now
  • "Second marriage asset protection Illinois" and "prenuptial agreement attorney Kane County" — unique niche terms where no competitor appears to be advertising, representing a direct pipeline to Jedediah's most differentiated offering
  • Competitor name targeting — bidding on searches for Foster & Buick and Klein Stoddard allows the firm to capture prospects who are still in the consideration phase and have not yet committed to a generalist firm
Blind Spot: Sycamore is a small market, which means CPCs for estate planning and business law are likely significantly lower than in Chicago or its suburbs — the same budget that would generate modest results in a top-10 metro can dominate DeKalb and Kane County search results. The firm that activates first in this market will be very difficult and expensive to displace once they have established Quality Score history and review-backed ad extensions.
