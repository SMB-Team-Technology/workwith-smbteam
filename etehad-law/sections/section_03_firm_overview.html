Here Is Where Etehad Law, APC Stands Today

Etehad Law, APC is a Beverly Hills personal injury and civil litigation firm with over 30 years of experience, 15 staff members, and approximately $4 million in annual revenue built almost entirely on word-of-mouth referrals. Simon Etehad has built an award-winning reputation — recognized by Super Lawyers as a top 5% firm in Southern California — but the firm has no predictable lead generation system, no intake training, no KPIs, and an owner so embedded in daily operations that he must personally approve every check to prevent fraud. The single most important finding from this audit: Etehad Law has extraordinary credentials and a strong market position, but competitors with 400 to 600 Google reviews and active multi-channel paid campaigns are pulling further ahead every month while the firm remains 100% dependent on referrals.

Stage 6 is the stage where the firm runs without you — where Simon is no longer the Chief Everything Officer, where checks don't require his daily approval, and where new cases arrive whether or not he is in the office — and that is exactly where this plan is designed to take Etehad Law.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • 100% of new clients come from word-of-mouth — "feast or famine" with no predictable pipeline
  • Simon approves every check personally and cannot step away without the firm stalling
  • No intake scripts, no KPIs, no monthly financial reporting — the firm operates reactively
  • About to commit $10,000/month to SEO alone without a full growth strategy in place

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between where you are and where you want to be is not as wide as it feels — but closing it requires a system, and right now almost every critical system is missing.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Etehad Law, APC
Etehad Law is operating at Stage 4 — a Small Business Manager with an impressive team, real revenue, and genuine market credibility, but without the systems needed to grow beyond the owner's personal capacity. The single most urgent gap is the complete absence of a predictable lead generation strategy: the firm generates approximately $4 million per year purely on referrals in a Beverly Hills market where competitors are running aggressive Google Ads, Local Service Ads, and multi-channel paid campaigns — and building review counts that are five to ten times larger. Until that gap is closed, the firm's goal of reaching $8 million by 2027 will remain entirely dependent on referral luck rather than a system Simon and Malissa can count on.
