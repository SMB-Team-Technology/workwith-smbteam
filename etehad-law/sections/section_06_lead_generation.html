Lead Generation

Etehad Law, APC has built a $4 million personal injury practice in Beverly Hills entirely on referrals — with no paid advertising running, no SEO campaign in place, and an AI visibility score of just 10 out of 100. In one of the most competitive legal markets in the United States, that means the firm is invisible to the vast majority of potential clients who are searching online right now.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • The website uses bot-blocking technology (HTTP 403 for automated tools) — this may interfere with how search engines and ad platforms crawl and evaluate the site
  • PageSpeed scores: not captured for this report — we recommend running a live check at pagespeed.web.dev for both mobile and desktop scores before the follow-up meeting
  • No confirmed above-fold CTA or contact form visible from search result previews — homepage relies on navigation rather than driving immediate action
  • Blog/news section exists but content freshness and posting frequency are unknown — stale content signals to Google that the site is not actively maintained
Blind Spot: Competitors may be actively bidding on "Etehad Law" as a branded keyword in Google Ads — capturing people who already know the firm and search for it by name. This is a paid search vulnerability that is difficult to detect without a live audit and relatively inexpensive to defend against.
The Positive
  • The site has a comprehensive page structure: dedicated practice area pages, attorney team page, case results, reviews section, and a news/blog — this is a strong foundation for SEO
  • Multiple practice area landing pages confirmed (personal injury, employment law, nursing home abuse, car accident) — good SEO signal structure already partially in place
The Gap
  • A well-structured site with no traffic strategy delivers zero leads — the architecture exists but the engine is not running
  • If page speed and mobile performance are substandard, Google's algorithm will actively deprioritize the site in local search rankings regardless of content quality
  • Every month without a live, conversion-optimized website working alongside paid campaigns is a month where competitors convert leads Etehad Law could have won
A website that converts visitors into callers is the foundation Simon needs to eventually step back — without it, no marketing campaign will perform to its potential and the revenue ceiling stays where it is.

The Gap

  • Every week without a systematic review generation strategy is a week where the gap between Etehad Law and its competitors widens further
Setareh Law — 400+ five-star Google reviews and consistent top placement in Beverly Hills PI searches. They are capturing the organic local traffic that Etehad Law's 30 years of earned credibility should be winning.
Dominating local search is the first step to a firm that generates leads without Simon's personal involvement — and it starts with fixing the foundation that is currently working against you.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • Etehad Law does not appear to be running Google Ads for personal injury or employment law keywords in Beverly Hills — the firm may not have active campaigns configured, or may be getting outbid by better-funded competitors
  • Per the discovery call, past sporadic ad spend "yielded no results" — this is consistent with running ads without a managed strategy, proper landing pages, or conversion tracking
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Setareh Law — active Google Ads presence targeting Beverly Hills PI keywords with "Ranked #1" positioning and "No Fee Unless We Win" messaging; capturing high-intent car accident and personal injury searches
  • Omega Law Group — active Google Ads with "No Fee Unless We Win" and 24/7 availability angle; running across personal injury, car accident, rideshare accident, and truck accident keywords in the LA market
  • GoJeff Law — confirmed paid presence targeting "Top Beverly Hills Personal Injury Lawyers" searches; competing for the same high-value PI keywords
The Opportunity
  • "Personal injury attorney Beverly Hills," "car accident lawyer Beverly Hills," and "nursing home abuse lawyer Beverly Hills" — high-intent, high-value keywords where the firm has no current paid presence
  • "Employment lawyer Beverly Hills" and "wrongful termination attorney Beverly Hills" — secondary practice area with significant search volume and lower CPL than PI
  • Competitor name bidding: "Setareh Law" and "Omega Law Group" as defensive keywords captures prospects already in the decision process who are comparison shopping
  • Spanish-language campaigns: Beverly Hills/LA has a large Spanish-speaking population — Spanish PI keywords are significantly less expensive and face less competition than English equivalents
Blind Spot: The $10,000/month SEO contract the firm is about to sign will take 6–12 months to show meaningful results. Google Ads produces leads in the first 30 days. Committing $10K/month to SEO-only without running paid ads simultaneously means passing up an entire year of cases that could be generating revenue right now.
