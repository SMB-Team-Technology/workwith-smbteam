Lead Generation

Truman Law currently has no paid advertising on any digital channel and does not appear in Google search results for any of its practice areas in Surrey — leaving every digital lead opportunity to established competitors. In one of the most competitive legal markets in British Columbia, the firm's complete invisibility online is its single most urgent growth constraint.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • No above-fold contact form — the only immediate action options are a phone number and a "Get Started Today" button that links to a separate contact page, creating avoidable friction for high-intent visitors
  • PageSpeed scores: Not available during research (API rate limited on May 28, 2026) — must be verified at pagespeed.web.dev before the call; based on site characteristics (WordPress, no WebP image optimization detected, standard plugins), mobile performance is likely mid-range at best
  • No video, no blog content, and no testimonial section — limited trust signals for prospective clients who arrive organically and need reassurance before calling
  • No city-specific or practice area landing pages — the entire site uses a single homepage to compete against competitors who have built dedicated pages for every search term and every neighborhood
Blind Spot: Competitors like Cube Law Corporation and Alpine Legal Services have built geo-targeted landing pages specifically for Surrey searches (e.g., cubelaw.ca/real-estate-lawyer-surrey/, alpinelawyers.com/real-estate-lawyer-surrey/). These dedicated pages dramatically outperform generic homepages in both organic rankings and paid ad conversion rates — meaning Truman Law would need purpose-built landing pages to compete effectively in either channel.
The Positive
  • The website has a clean, professional design with Harkirat's bio and credentials featured prominently — giving it genuine credibility when prospects do land on it
  • Mobile-responsive layout confirms the site displays correctly on the devices most prospective clients use to search for legal services
The Gap
  • Without an above-fold conversion point, high-intent visitors who arrive on the homepage leave before making contact — the site is generating no measurable inquiry volume from digital traffic
  • Without geo-targeted service pages for Surrey neighborhoods and neighboring cities (Burnaby, Langley, Delta), the site cannot compete for the location-specific searches that make up the majority of local legal queries
  • Without confirmed PageSpeed data, mobile load performance is an unknown risk — a slow mobile experience loses a significant portion of visitors before they ever see the firm's credentials
A website that converts visitors into consultations is the foundation of everything else — and right now, trumanlaw.ca is not built to convert at the volume Truman Law needs to grow.

Local SEO

The Problem

  • The firm does not appear in the local 3-pack for any tested search term: "real estate lawyer Surrey," "business lawyer Surrey," "private lending lawyer Surrey," or any variation — zero local search visibility across all practice areas
  • A NAP inconsistency is circulating across multiple directories: the old address (7130 120 St, Unit 109, Surrey) still appears in some databases while the current address is 302B, 7327 137 St, Surrey — this inconsistency actively suppresses local SEO trust signals
  • No geo-targeting landing pages exist for neighboring communities (Burnaby, Langley, Delta, Abbotsford) — missing an entire category of location-specific organic traffic
Blind Spot: The Clearway Law profile for Truman Law is unclaimed — showing 0 reviews, no practice areas, and no photos. This is a visible signal to both prospective clients and to Google's algorithm that the firm's online presence is unmanaged, and it is actively undermining the firm's credibility in directory search results.
The Positive
  • A 5.0-star rating across all observed reviews demonstrates that the clients who do find Truman Law are highly satisfied — the quality of service is there, and the review foundation is strong even if the volume is low
The Gap
  • Without entering the local 3-pack, Truman Law captures none of the high-intent local searches driving the most qualified real estate and business law leads in Surrey
  • Competitors with 5–6× the review count are receiving preferential placement in every local search — the review gap compounds each month without intervention, making the 3-pack harder to crack
Keystone Law Group — 131 Google reviews at 4.9 stars. They appear at the top of local search results for real estate, business law, and corporate matters in Surrey — the exact searches where Truman Law should be competing and winning.
Every week that the review gap grows wider is a week that Truman Law's path into the 3-pack gets harder — and the 3-pack is where most Surrey clients make their first decision about which firm to call.
