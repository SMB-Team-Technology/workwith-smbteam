Here Is Where Rex Legal Stands Today

Michael Rex is a solo personal injury attorney in Denver, CO, running a boutique PI firm built almost entirely on referrals — with 41 five-star Google reviews, a compelling personal story as a TBI survivor, and genuine courtroom credentials as a Super Lawyer and Million Dollar Advocates Forum member. The single most important finding: with caseload down to 15–20 matters after stopping a costly LSA campaign, and competitors like Bachus & Schanker running aggressive multi-channel campaigns backed by nearly 2,000 Google reviews, Rex Legal has no predictable system to generate the consistent 25+ caseload that would unlock the admin hire — and the path to $500k personal income — that Michael needs.

Stage 6 is where Michael needs to be to stop trading personal hours for income — where the firm runs on systems, cases come in consistently, an admin handles the operations, and the $500k personal income goal becomes a reality rather than a target.
