Lead Generation

The firm does not appear to be running paid ads on any channel, and its website is currently absent from Google's search index, eliminating it from both organic and paid discovery entirely.

Blind Spot: Competitors are almost certainly bidding on branded keywords in Google Ads. When a referral who has heard Nkiru's name searches "NK Law Office Calgary," a competitor's paid ad may appear at the very top of the results — above where the firm's own website should be. With the site currently not indexed, there is no organic result to fall back on either.
The Positive
  • Clean, modern professional design with a hero section, service cards, and a three-step process visualization — strong trust signals for visitors who do land on the site
  • Attorney bio section features Nkiru Anyanwu's credentials (LL.M, Lancaster University), personal story, and community focus — a compelling differentiator when prospects read it
The Gap
  • A website that Google cannot find delivers zero marketing value regardless of its design quality — every potential client searching for legal help in Calgary is finding competitors instead
  • The broken blog and missing search indexing mean even referrals who are given the website address may lose confidence when they cannot find the firm through a Google search
  • Without a conversion path above the fold, visitors who do land on the site leave without contacting the firm — converting traffic to inquiries requires reducing that friction
When NK Law Office has a fully optimized, indexed website with above-the-fold conversion — every referral Nkiru earns becomes a client who actually shows up.
