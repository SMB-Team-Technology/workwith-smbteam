Add After-Hours Lead Capture
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Personal injury cases happen at all hours. Accident victims often search for attorneys late at night or on weekends — within hours of the accident when emotional urgency is highest. Calls that go to voicemail during off-hours are routinely lost to the first firm that answered a competing search.
The Opportunity
An after-hours call routing system or automated intake form that captures lead information and triggers a rapid next-morning follow-up converts the highest-intent PI prospects before they have a chance to call another firm. This is a structural fix that works 24/7 without adding to Jake's personal workload.

An intake coordinator is the structural fix that breaks this conflict.
Who Is Doing It Better
Carabin Shaw — multi-attorney firm with a dedicated intake team that responds faster, follows up more consistently, and converts a higher percentage of inquiries than any solo attorney managing their own leads can replicate.
The Opportunity

Establish Case-Level Profitability Tracking
Profit Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
Without knowing the revenue, time investment, and true cost per case by type, Jake cannot identify whether his current case mix is optimized for the highest return on attorney time — which is the firm's most constrained and valuable resource.
The Opportunity
A basic case-level profitability framework — tracking settlement value, attorney hours, and acquisition cost by case type — reveals which cases produce the highest net income per hour of Jake's time. For a truck accident specialist, this data typically shows a dramatic difference between trucking and standard car accident cases that justifies deliberate case selection and marketing investment decisions.
