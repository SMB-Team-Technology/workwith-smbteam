Lead Generation

The Bradley Law Firm, PLLC has built real market credibility over 28 years — but that credibility is nearly invisible to the people searching online today for estate planning, elder law, and probate help. With no paid advertising observed across any digital channel and no geo-targeting SEO strategy in place, the firm is leaving an entire pipeline of new clients entirely to chance while competitors invest systematically in the same market.

  • PageSpeed scores: Not retrieved — the site's security configuration blocked automated page analysis; scores must be verified manually at pagespeed.web.dev before the proposal call

Blind Spot: Competitors are actively building Germantown-specific landing pages that intercept people who search for attorneys by city and practice area. Without matching pages, the firm cedes these high-intent local searches to rivals who invested in the structure Bradley Law has not yet built.
The Positive
  • Attorney bio confirmed at a dedicated /about/ page — builds credibility for first-time visitors who want to know the attorney before calling
  • Blog present with legal content indexed by Google — provides the foundation for an SEO content strategy that is not yet being leveraged
The Gap
  • A website without practice area landing pages for Germantown, Collierville, Bartlett, and surrounding cities cannot rank for the local searches Anthony's ideal clients are running — it generates referral confirmation clicks, not new clients
  • Without geo-targeted pages, the site cannot rank for high-value terms like "VA pension attorney Germantown TN" or "probate attorney Shelby County" that represent some of the most profitable case types the firm handles
  • No clear above-fold CTA confirmed — visitors who do reach the site may leave without taking any action, sending paid ad traffic and organic visitors away with no follow-up mechanism
A fully optimized website is the engine Anthony cannot work around — it either generates new clients 24/7 or it does not, and right now it appears not to be doing so.

Local SEO

The Problem

  • No geo-targeted landing pages for surrounding cities confirmed — competitors have dedicated pages for Germantown, Bartlett, Collierville, Cordova, and Memphis-proper; Bradley Law does not appear to match this structure
  • NAP inconsistencies identified across directories — two different phone numbers (901-682-2030 and 901-441-8215) and a possible address discrepancy ("Corporate Edge Dr." vs. "S. Corporate Edge Dr.") are diluting Google's local ranking confidence for the firm
Blind Spot: Inconsistent Name, Address, and Phone data across directories is a silent local SEO penalty — Google's local ranking algorithm requires consistent NAP signals across the web to assign map pack positions. Every inconsistent citation is reducing the firm's map pack visibility without Anthony knowing it.
The Positive
  • 136 Google reviews is the highest verified count among comparable local competitors — this is a genuine competitive asset, particularly for LSA qualification and map pack trust signals
The Gap
  • Without Germantown-specific and suburban-city landing pages, the firm cannot rank for the highest-intent local searches where buying decisions are made — this gap is costing Anthony cases every month
  • A 3.9 star average compared to Burdette's 4.6/5.0 means that when prospective clients compare firms side by side in the map pack, they will consistently choose the higher-rated competitor
Snider & Horner, PLLC — has invested in a portfolio of geo-targeted landing pages at kevinsnider.com specifically targeting Germantown searches (kevinsnider.com/germantown-estate-attorney/, /germantown-probate-attorney/, /germantown-wills-attorney/wills-trusts/), giving them a structural advantage in the exact local searches Anthony's ideal clients are running.
Fixing local SEO means the firm shows up when estate planning and elder law clients are actively searching — without spending a dollar on paid ads, just by building the structure competitors already have.
