Lead Generation

The Law Offices of Mark Daniel Melnick has historically relied on Google Ads and LSAs as its primary lead generation channel — but both campaigns are currently dark following a website malware incident, while well-funded competitors continue to dominate paid search, local SEO, and organic rankings across every criminal defense and DUI keyword in Van Nuys and the San Fernando Valley. Every day without active campaigns is a day Dan's competitors capture the clients who would otherwise call him.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • The website was recently cleared of a malware flag — but Google's trust recovery after a security incident can take weeks to months, meaning organic traffic and ad eligibility may still be suppressed even after technical clearance
  • PageSpeed scores: Could not be retrieved during research (rate-limit error) — manual verification required at pagespeed.web.dev. Based on the site's older WordPress architecture, mobile scores for similar builds typically range 30–55; a score below 50 means Google actively deprioritizes the site in mobile search rankings
  • The homepage title tag reads "CRIMINAL DEFENSE ATTORNEY – Top criminal defense attorney" — no city name, no geo-targeting, no differentiation; this is a missed opportunity in a market where every click is contested
  • The firm uses melnickdefense@gmail.com as its public-facing contact email — a non-branded address that reduces perceived professionalism and trust compared to competitors using @melnickdefense.com
Blind Spot: The malware remediation has a long tail. Even after Google's Search Console shows the site as "clean," ranking recovery can lag by weeks — and if ads were paused during the same window, the combined suppression effect on inbound lead volume is compounding. The site may appear fine to a visitor but still be underperforming in search visibility during this recovery window.
The Positive
  • The site has indexed practice-area content pages (DUI, drug possession, assault, domestic violence, theft, and others) — a foundational content structure that can be optimized rather than rebuilt from scratch
  • Dan's 44-year experience is referenced throughout the site — an extraordinary credibility signal that, if better positioned on the homepage and above the fold, would convert more visitors
The Gap
  • A slow, generic website with no geo-targeted landing pages means every dollar spent on Google Ads or LSAs lands on a page that does not maximize conversion — paid leads are being wasted
  • No contact form above the fold, no visible CTA confirmed, no capture mechanism for after-hours visitors — prospects who cannot reach anyone immediately leave and call a competitor
  • Without a redesigned, conversion-optimized website with geo-specific pages for San Fernando and Burbank, Dan cannot effectively shift his case mix to the local misdemeanor volume he wants
A website that works like a 24-hour intake team — capturing leads, building trust, and routing inquiries — is the first building block of the "autopilot and profit" model.

  • Without geo-targeted landing pages for Burbank and San Fernando, Dan cannot rank locally for the courthouse-specific searches that would feed exactly the misdemeanor and DUI case mix he wants
