Website Audit

The Problem
  • The website is completely inaccessible — whaley.law returns an "SSL certificate has expired" error on both HTTP and HTTPS, blocking all visitors and preventing Google from crawling or indexing the site
  • PageSpeed scores: Could not be run — pagespeed.web.dev returns an error state because the site is unreachable. Scores must be re-evaluated after the SSL is restored. A site that Google cannot crawl is effectively unranked.
  • No confirmed contact form, live chat, or above-the-fold CTA is visible — the site's conversion infrastructure is unknown and unverifiable in its current state
  • No blog content, no geo-specific landing pages, and no video content were observed in any cached or indexed content — the site has no content engine producing organic search signals
Blind Spot: An expired SSL certificate does not just block visitors — it signals to Google that the site may be abandoned or compromised. Every day the SSL remains expired, the firm's organic rankings erode further and the crawl debt compounds. What was a quick fix in week one becomes a months-long recovery project if ignored.
The Positive
  • Stewart has an established domain (whaley.law) with some indexed content and directory backlinks — once the SSL is restored, the existing authority can be built on rather than starting from scratch
  • Attorney bio and contact information appear consistently in directory listings and search snippets — the brand foundation exists even while the website is down
The Gap
  • Every potential client who tries to visit whaley.law sees a security warning and leaves — these are warm leads that the firm is actively losing to competitors right now
  • Without a functioning website, no paid advertising can launch — landing pages, tracking pixels, and conversion forms all require a live, secure site
  • Dallas County and Clark County were identified on the call as underserved geographic markets with few attorneys — the firm has no landing pages targeting these areas, leaving a geographic opportunity completely uncaptured
A website that works is not optional — it is the foundation that every other marketing system depends on, and without it, the path to the financial stability and freedom Stewart is building toward cannot even begin.

At 18 reviews, Whaley Law is not even in the competitive range for 3-pack placement, regardless of how good the actual legal work is. The Gap

Lemley Law Partners — 102+ reviews at 4.9★, consistent 3-pack placement for criminal defense and drug possession searches, and a growing brand identity built around fixed fees and professional intake. They are occupying the map pack positions Whaley Law should be competing for.
Fixing local SEO — starting with reviews and the GBP profile — is the fastest path to organic leads that do not require Stewart to personally chase every new client.
