Google's crawler cannot reach the site either, meaning organic rankings are degrading every day the SSL is expired. This is not a future problem — it is an active, ongoing loss of clients and visibility.
Who Is Doing It Better
Ludwig Law Firm — fully functional, actively indexed website capturing all the organic traffic and local search visibility that Whaley Law should be receiving for traffic violations and criminal defense in Little Rock.
The Opportunity
Fixing the SSL certificate (identified on the call as a 10Web action item) restores the firm's digital front door — making it accessible to clients, indexable by Google, and ready to support paid advertising campaigns. Everything else in this roadmap depends on this step being completed first.
Launch Google Ads for Traffic Tickets
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
Traffic tickets are high-intent, high-volume searches — people who just received a ticket are actively looking to hire an attorney right now. Whaley Law does not appear to be running ads for any of these searches, while Ludwig Law Firm and Thompson Law Firm PLLC are actively capturing this traffic with paid placements.
Who Is Doing It Better
Ludwig Law Firm — running Google Ads with messaging "expert, affordable traffic violation defense — over 43 years of experience," complete with a dedicated phone line for traffic cases appearing at the top of every relevant search.
The Opportunity
At $1,100 per traffic ticket case and a high case volume, even a modest Google Ads campaign generating 5–8 additional cases per month creates immediate revenue. Traffic ticket searches in Little Rock have a lower CPL than criminal defense, making this one of the highest-ROI paid channels available to Whaley Law.
Update GBP Cover Photo to a Professional Headshot
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
The Google Business Profile cover photo is not currently a professional headshot — which means the first impression a prospect gets when they see the firm in the map pack is not optimized for trust or click-through. Updating the GBP cover photo to a professional headshot of Stewart takes less than a day and costs nothing beyond a photo session. It improves click-through rates from the map pack, signals an active and credible firm, and directly addresses the specific quick win identified on the call.
Review Generation Campaign — Close the Gap
Lead Gen Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
18 reviews at 4.1★ is the single biggest factor keeping Whaley Law out of the local 3-pack and out of LSA eligibility. Review volume and recency are among the top-weighted ranking signals in Google's local algorithm. At the current count, the firm is not in the competitive range regardless of how well the rest of the digital presence is built out.
Who Is Doing It Better
Lemley Law Partners — 102 reviews at 4.9★ at the Little Rock location alone. That volume is not accidental — it reflects a systematic review request process that compounds every month as they continue to generate new clients.
The Opportunity
A systematic campaign reaching out to past satisfied clients can generate 10–20 new reviews in the first 60 days, diluting the old negatives from the previous firm and moving the firm toward the volume threshold for 3-pack and LSA eligibility. Each batch of new reviews improves visibility and compounds future results.
Implement After-Hours Call Coverage
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Traffic ticket clients often need an attorney urgently — court dates are approaching, and they need help now. When Stewart is in court or unavailable after hours, calls that could become clients are going to voicemail and immediately dialing the next attorney who picks up. Every missed call is a lost case.
The Opportunity
An after-hours answering service or virtual legal receptionist provides 24/7 basic intake coverage for a fraction of what even one lost traffic case costs. At $1,100 per traffic ticket, two additional captured cases per month more than pay for a full month of answering service fees.
Raise Felony Defense Pricing Toward Market Rate
Profit Quick Win
Why It Matters
Charging $7,000 for felony defense when peers charge $30,000 for the same work means every felony case Stewart takes generates 23 cents on the dollar of its market value. The underpricing does not attract more clients — it simply caps the income from the cases that require the most of his time.
The Opportunity
Moving felony pricing to $12,000–$15,000 (still significantly below the market rate competitors charge) effectively doubles the revenue from every felony case without adding a single new client. For a goal of $1M annual revenue, pricing what the work is actually worth is one of the fastest levers available.
First Part-Time Support Hire
Team Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
Every hour Stewart spends on intake calls, billing, scheduling, and admin is an hour not spent on billable work or business development. As lead volume increases from marketing, the absence of any support staff will become the bottleneck that limits how much of that volume actually converts to retained clients.
Who Is Doing It Better
Lemley Law Partners — trained intake staff and defined intake processes allow them to respond to new leads, convert prospects, and manage client communication at hours and volumes that no solo practitioner can match personally.
The Opportunity
A part-time virtual assistant or intake coordinator working 10–15 hours per week frees Stewart from the administrative load that consumes his time, provides basic coverage during court appearances, and creates the operational foundation needed before any full-time hire makes financial sense.
Geographic Expansion: Dallas County and Clark County Pages
Lead Gen Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
Dallas County and Clark County were identified on the call as underserved geographic markets with few local attorneys and a Stuttgart office already in place — but the firm has zero web presence targeting searches from those counties. Attorneys in those areas are either not advertising or referral-only, which means low competition for any firm willing to build a local search presence there.
The Opportunity
Location-specific landing pages targeting "traffic ticket attorney Dallas County Arkansas" and related searches offer a faster path to first-page organic rankings than competing in the more crowded Little Rock market — and the Stuttgart office provides a credible local presence to support those pages.
