Here Is Where Whaley Law Stands Today

Stewart Whaley runs a solo criminal defense and traffic law practice out of two Arkansas offices — Little Rock and Stuttgart — handling traffic tickets, professional license defense, DWI, and felony criminal defense entirely on his own, without a single support staff member. Revenue has doubled year-over-year to $30,000 per month, driven by referrals, a Justia directory listing, and a single paid ad in the Arkansas State Board of Nursing periodical. The single most urgent problem is that Stewart's website is completely offline due to an expired SSL certificate — making the firm invisible to Google, inaccessible to new clients, and unable to capture any of the digital demand that competitors are actively harvesting.

Stage 6 is where the firm generates income whether Stewart is in the office or not — and reaching it is exactly what this roadmap is designed to do, so he can work a normal week, take real vacations, and stop carrying the financial weight of the practice entirely on his own.
