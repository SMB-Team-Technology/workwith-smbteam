Implement After-Hours Intake Coverage
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Personal injury cases are frequently signed on the day of the accident or within 24 hours. Without an after-hours intake system, accident victims calling at night or submitting forms on weekends are waiting until the next business day — by which time a competitor with 24/7 availability has already signed them.
The Opportunity
An after-hours intake protocol — whether via answering service, text response automation, or both — converts the leads already arriving at the firm's website into signed cases, without any additional marketing spend. This is revenue that already belongs to the firm, currently being lost to slow follow-up.

Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
No confirmed automated response is triggered when a prospect submits the homepage contact form. Leads who submit and receive no immediate acknowledgment frequently assume the form was not received — and call a competitor before the attorney gets back to them.
The Opportunity
An automated text and email response within 60 seconds of form submission keeps the prospect engaged, confirms receipt, sets expectations for a callback, and dramatically improves the lead-to-consultation conversion rate — using leads the firm is already generating today.
Hire and Train a Dedicated Intake Specialist
Team Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
