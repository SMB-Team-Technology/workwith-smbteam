Website Audit

The Problem
  • Built on GoDaddy Website Builder — limits SEO extensibility, technical optimization, and landing page flexibility compared to platforms built for lead generation
  • PageSpeed scores: Not available — scores could not be retrieved during research due to rate-limiting; manual verification recommended before the proposal call
  • Contact form is located on a separate dedicated page, not visible above the fold on the homepage — a prospect who arrives and wants to act must navigate to reach the form, and most will not
  • No live chat present — visitors who arrive after business hours or prefer immediate digital contact have no way to connect in real time
  • No attorney video on the homepage — a high-trust element that stronger competitors use to build rapport before a prospect ever calls
Blind Spot: The firm's primary URL (ebbert.law) currently resolves to a GoDaddy "Launching Soon" placeholder page with no firm content — any prospect who types that URL directly encounters a blank holding page. All firm traffic routes through ebbertlaw.com, but any link, print material, or verbal reference using the shorter URL sends prospects to a dead end.
The Positive
  • Modern design — website reads as current and professional; no obvious aesthetic issues that would erode trust
  • Mobile responsive and active blog with recent content (last post May 14, 2026) — signals an engaged, active practice to Google and to visitors
  • Attorney bio present with detailed background — Eric Ebbert's 30-year experience and credentials are visible to prospects researching the firm
  • Geo-targeting landing page confirmed for Farragut, TN estate planning — demonstrates awareness of local SEO strategy
The Gap
  • A website that requires prospects to navigate to a contact form is losing conversions every day — the homepage should function as a lead capture tool, not just an informational brochure
  • GoDaddy Website Builder constraints limit the ability to create the practice-area-specific landing pages and technical SEO optimizations needed to compete in paid search
  • Without PageSpeed scores, the full performance gap is unknown — but the platform choice suggests mobile speed optimization is likely a risk area
A website that converts visitors into consultations — not just informs them — is the foundation of every paid campaign. Getting this right is what makes every marketing dollar work harder.
