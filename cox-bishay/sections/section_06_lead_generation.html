Website Audit

The Problem
  • Homepage headline reads "Welcome to the Law Firm Of Cox & Bishay, LLP" — a purely passive welcome statement with no value proposition, differentiator, or reason for a prospect to stay on the page
  • PageSpeed scores: UNAVAILABLE — Google PageSpeed API returned HTTP 429 (quota exceeded) on multiple attempts; scores must be confirmed manually before final presentation
  • No contact form exists anywhere on the site — the only conversion path offered to visitors is a list of direct phone extensions, which can only be used during business hours
  • No geographic landing pages for surrounding Chester County communities (Exton, Downingtown, Malvern, Phoenixville, Coatesville, Kennett Square) — the firm serves county-wide but the website only targets one city
  • No blog, no homepage testimonials, no video — the site has no content to drive organic traffic or give visitors a reason to choose Cox & Bishay over the competitors they found first
Blind Spot: The Yelp listing for the firm shows 120 N. Church St. as the address — different from the website's 137 N. High St. Any prospect who clicks through from Yelp and uses that address for directions may arrive at the wrong location, creating a negative first impression before a consultation ever happens.
The Positive
  • Individual attorney bio pages exist for both Kimberly Cox and Rami Bishay, with detailed credentials, professional history, and recognition — the foundations of trust are present, they are just not visible from the homepage
  • Rami Bishay's credentials are strong: Avvo 9.6 Superb rating, Super Lawyers Rising Star, Top Family Law Lawyer recognition, and a $360/hr billing rate that signals market positioning — these are the kind of proof points that convert skeptical prospects
The Gap
  • A website that cannot convert visitors is a billboard that charges for views but produces no clients — every dollar spent on advertising flows out without a return until this is fixed
  • Without geo-specific landing pages for Chester County communities, the firm cannot rank organically for the surrounding markets it already serves
  • The absence of any lead capture form means the website cannot function as a marketing tool even when paid advertising drives traffic directly to it
A rebuilt website with real conversion paths is what turns 35 years of reputation into 35 years of digital credibility — visible to every family in Chester County who is searching for help right now.
