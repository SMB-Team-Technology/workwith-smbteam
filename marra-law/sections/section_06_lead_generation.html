Lead Generation

The Law Offices of Joseph A. Marra, PLLC has built one of the strongest individual reputations in Westchester County elder law over 38 years — but that reputation is not connected to any system for predictably attracting new clients. With no paid search, no local service ads, and no paid social observed across any practice area, the firm is leaving the entire digital front door to competitors who are actively investing.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • The site returns a 403 error to automated research tools, indicating bot-blocking or CDN protection — this means the website's performance and content cannot be fully audited without a manual review
  • PageSpeed scores: Unable to retrieve — Google PageSpeed API returned a 429 rate-limit error at time of research; scores unavailable and should be pulled manually before the proposal call
  • Contact form visibility above the fold could not be confirmed; the primary CTA is phone-only ("Free 10-minute consultation over the phone") — a website visitor who prefers online contact has no clear path
  • BBB directory lists the firm under the category "DUI Lawyer" — a significant category mismatch given the firm's primary practice in estate planning and elder law that creates confusing signals for both search engines and prospects
Blind Spot: The firm's 403 anti-bot protection may be inadvertently blocking Google's crawlers or PageSpeed scoring tools — which means the site's actual search performance and indexability may be weaker than the firm realizes. This should be confirmed with a technical SEO review.
The Positive
  • Strong credentials are displayed online — AV Preeminent rating, 10.0 Avvo "Superb" rating, J.D. from St. John's and LL.M. from NYU, and 42+ years of experience create meaningful trust signals for a prospect who finds the site
  • Dedicated practice area pages exist for estate planning, probate, and estate administration, providing some SEO structure for core service areas
The Gap
  • A website that cannot be audited by research tools is likely also difficult for search engines to fully crawl — any technical issues blocking visibility are costing the firm leads it cannot see or measure
  • With no above-fold online contact option confirmed, visitors who prefer form submission over phone — a growing segment — have a higher drop-off risk
  • No confirmed landing pages for surrounding Westchester communities (Mount Vernon, New Rochelle, White Plains, Pelham, Bronxville) — the firm serves these markets but may not be capturing search intent from residents in those towns
A website that cannot be found, audited, or converted is not an asset — it is a missed opportunity every day Joe is not there to take a referral call.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • Marra Law does not appear in the local 3-pack for primary searches including "elder law attorney Yonkers NY," "estate planning attorney Yonkers NY," "probate attorney Yonkers NY," "divorce attorney Yonkers NY," or "personal injury attorney Yonkers NY" — all five are dominated by competitors
  • No confirmed Google Business Profile posts — an active posting cadence is a ranking signal that competitors are likely using and Marra Law is not
  • The BBB directory categorizes the firm as "DUI Lawyer" — a NAP category mismatch that creates inconsistent signals about the firm's practice areas across the web
  • Two phone numbers appear across directories ((914) 964-6806 and (914) 505-8449) without consistent usage — potential NAP inconsistency that dilutes local search authority
Blind Spot: The firm has dedicated geo-targeting pages for Westchester County and Yonkers, but no confirmed pages for nearby towns like Mount Vernon, New Rochelle, Pelham, or White Plains — these communities are within the firm's natural service area and represent uncontested local SEO opportunities that competitors are likely not targeting for Marra Law's specific practice mix.
The Positive
  • 147 Google reviews at 4.7 stars as of June 2026 is the strongest individual review count among single-location competitors in the Westchester elder law market — more than Enea Scanlan (101), Meyer & Spencer (57), and Amoruso & Amoruso (21)
The Gap
  • A 147-review profile that does not appear in the local 3-pack is a reviews-to-visibility disconnect — the reputation exists, but it is not translating into search placement because the supporting signals (citations, posts, category alignment) are not optimized
  • Every month without 3-pack placement is a month where prospects searching for "estate planning attorney Yonkers" choose from competitors before they ever see Marra Law's name
Enea, Scanlan & Sirignano LLP — dominates Westchester elder law visibility through a combination of Westchester Magazine sponsorships, Super Lawyers designation, and dual-office presence. They are building brand authority in print and digital while Marra Law's search presence remains largely organic and unoptimized.
Every week the 3-pack is held by competitors, Joe is personally fielding referral calls because that is the only intake channel that is working — and that is not a firm that runs without him.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • Marra Law does not appear to be running paid search ads for elder law, estate planning, probate, estate administration, divorce, family law, or personal injury terms in Yonkers and Westchester County searches — the firm may not have active campaigns configured, or may be getting outbid by competitors with dedicated ad budgets
  • With estate plans averaging $6,000 per matter and roughly 10 signed per month, paid search for estate planning represents one of the most predictable ROI channels available to this firm — flat-fee pricing makes cost-per-acquisition math straightforward
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Ettinger Law Firm (trustlaw.com) — appears prominently in elder law and estate planning searches via a dedicated national brand domain, indicating a substantial and well-organized paid search investment across the New York market
  • Stephen Bilkis & Associates — runs broad Westchester County legal advertising across probate, family law, divorce, and personal injury with aggressive regional reach through the 1-800-NY-NY-LAW brand
The Opportunity
  • "Elder law attorney Yonkers," "estate planning attorney Westchester County," "Medicaid planning lawyer NY," and "probate attorney Yonkers" — high-intent searches currently going to competitors
  • "Estate planning attorney near me" and "estate administration lawyer Yonkers" — geo-modified searches where a properly structured campaign with Joe's credentials in the ad copy would convert strongly
  • Competitor name terms (Ettinger, Enea Scanlan) — capturing prospects who search by competitor name but are still in the comparison phase before committing
  • Neighboring community terms ("estate planning attorney White Plains," "elder law attorney New Rochelle," "probate lawyer Mount Vernon") — adjacent markets where Marra Law currently has no digital presence
Blind Spot: Estate planning's flat-fee structure ($6,000 average per matter) makes Google Ads ROI unusually predictable for this firm — unlike contingency or hourly matters, the revenue per signed case is fixed and known. A firm running 10 estate plans per month from referrals alone could realistically add 4–6 paid-ad cases at a positive ROI within the first 60–90 days of a well-managed campaign.
