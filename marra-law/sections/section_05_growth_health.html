Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • No paid advertising observed on any channel — Google Ads, LSA, and Meta Ads all absent for a firm handling 10+ estate plans per month
  • Marra Law does not appear in the local 3-pack for elder law, estate planning, probate, divorce, or personal injury searches in Yonkers and Westchester County
  • New clients depend entirely on referrals and 38 years of reputation — there is no system generating a predictable flow of new inquiries
Without a predictable lead system, Joe cannot step back from the firm — because the moment he does, the referral pipeline stops being managed.

Intake

  • Clients are going months without follow-up when staff depart or take leave — a direct threat to the firm's reputation with executors and beneficiaries
  • Documents arrive by email and print, sit on desks, and are never uploaded to Clio — creating gaps in matter records and attorney accountability
  • Clio client portal adoption is low, and matter status is rarely updated — leaving neither clients nor attorneys with a clear picture of where cases stand
Every client who goes uncontacted for months is a risk to the reputation Joe has spent 38 years building — and a case that may never refer again.

Self-Managing Team

  • The firm has 4 attorneys and support staff, but departures and illness are creating capacity shortfalls that no system currently fills
  • Joe is simultaneously managing a law firm and serving as Town Judge — his personal bandwidth is the firm's primary operational constraint
  • Attorney productivity is hard to measure due to a hybrid billing model, making it difficult to know which cases are actually profitable
A firm where the owner's absence creates immediate operational breakdown is not a firm yet — it is still a job.

Profit

  • Revenue was not discussed on the call — there is no clear picture of what the firm earns versus what Joe takes home
  • Aged probate and estate administration matters that have extended past their one-year target are tying up both staff time and revenue
  • A hybrid billing model across practice areas makes it difficult to identify which work is most profitable and where time should be focused
Profit without a plan is not profit — it is whatever is left over, and that number fluctuates with every staff change and backlogged matter.
