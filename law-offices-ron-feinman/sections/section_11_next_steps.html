Google Business Profile Optimization
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
The firm's GBP is not appearing in the local 3-pack for any observed elder law or estate planning search in Lynchburg. No active photos or posts were observed on the profile — an incomplete GBP signals lower relevance to Google and reduces click-through rates even when the listing does appear.
The Opportunity
Fully optimizing the GBP — adding attorney photos, practice area descriptions, correct primary and secondary categories, Q&A responses, and a weekly post cadence — can improve local pack ranking visibility without any ad spend. Paired with the review campaign, these two moves together are the fastest path to organic lead generation.
NAP Consistency Cleanup
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
At least five different entity names for the same firm are in circulation across online directories — "Law Offices of Ron Feinman," "Virginia Elder Law, PLC," "One World, Legacy and Estate Planning, LLC," "Law Office of Ron and Paul Feinman, Esq.," and "Law Office of Ron Feinman." Google cannot confidently surface a business it cannot consistently identify, and NAP inconsistencies are one of the most common suppressors of Google Business Profile ranking.
The Opportunity
A citation audit and standardization across all active directories removes a direct barrier to GBP ranking at no additional ad cost. Once citations are consistent, the review campaign and GBP optimization compound in impact — these three moves together are the foundation of local SEO authority.
