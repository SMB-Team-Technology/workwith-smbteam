Lead Generation

The Antoine Law Firm has built an exceptional legal reputation on referrals and retained public entity relationships — but referrals are not a growth system, and the Newark and NYC markets are being captured every day by competitors who are investing in digital channels this firm has not yet entered. The gap between what the firm has earned and what it is capturing online is the single largest untapped revenue opportunity available right now.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Only 5 pages indexed on the entire site (Home, Counsel, Practice Areas, Contact, Privacy) — no blog, no geo-targeted landing pages, no practice area detail pages to rank for any keyword
  • PageSpeed scores: Could not be retrieved during research — must be run manually at pagespeed.web.dev. The site blocks automated access, which itself suggests a non-standard technical configuration that may affect search engine crawling.
  • No marketing headline, no CTA above the fold, no contact form on the homepage — first-time visitors have no immediate path to engage
  • No video content, no blog, and no content marketing of any kind — the firm leaves no digital footprint between visits
Blind Spot: A website with only 5 indexed pages cannot rank for any competitive keyword in the Newark or NYC legal market — even with strong backlinks and reviews. Google needs content to rank, and there is no content here to rank. The firm's Super Lawyer recognition and Cornell credentials exist nowhere on the internet except legal directory pages the firm did not create.
The Positive
  • The site has a dedicated attorney bio page and a Practice Areas page — the basic structural elements exist to build on
  • NAP (name, address, phone) is consistent across all observed directory listings — no citation cleanup required before SEO work begins
The Gap
  • A prospect searching for a personal injury attorney in Newark will not find this firm — 5 indexed pages with no keyword content means zero organic search presence
  • The lack of a contact form on the homepage means any visitor who does arrive has to navigate to a separate contact page — a barrier that reduces conversions at every step
  • With no geo-targeting, the firm's New York office at 305 Broadway generates zero website traffic from NYC PI or discrimination searches — an entire market is invisible to potential clients
A website that cannot be found is not an asset — it is a missed opportunity compounding every month. Building a site that works while Wilson is not working is one of the clearest paths to the freedom he is describing.
