Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • Every new PI case comes from word-of-mouth — 1 to 4 per month with no predictable system in place
  • No paid advertising observed across any channel — Google, LSA, or Meta — for any practice area
  • A 5-page website with no blog, no landing pages, and an estimated near-zero Google review count leaves the firm essentially invisible online
Without a predictable lead system, the legacy firm Wilson wants to build will always be one slow month away from a revenue crisis.

Intake

  • Wilson personally handles all intake outside of PI pre-suit — a single point of failure that cannot scale
  • PI pre-suit intake is delegated to a paralegal who is still in training — quality is inconsistent and creates malpractice risk
  • A reactive workflow with missed deadlines is documented — there is no defined intake process or follow-up system in place
A firm that runs itself starts with an intake process that does not require the owner to be present — and right now, Wilson's presence is the process.

Self-Managing Team

  • Wilson has a paralegal, legal assistant, and associate on staff — the building blocks of a self-managing team exist
  • But Wilson is still the "Chief Everything Officer," spending 50–60% of his time on admin that his team could handle with proper training and systems
  • The Smokeball implementation is stalled and the associate needs basic system training — accountability structures are not yet in place
The team is there — but they are not yet built to run without Wilson, which means he cannot step back regardless of how much revenue grows.

Profit

  • $492K in gross revenue produced only $60K in personal distributions — a $432K gap that Wilson cannot explain due to bookkeeping issues
  • No financial visibility into which practice areas are profitable, what overhead is costing, or where the money is going
  • There is no profit plan in place — income is discovered, not designed, which means the $250K personal distribution goal has no path to get there
Wilson cannot build a firm that funds his freedom without first knowing where his money is going — the financial clarity piece has to come before anything else.
