Build a Written PI Intake SOP
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters

Inconsistent intake creates malpractice risk, loses cases, and keeps Wilson pulled back into a process he should have fully delegated.
The Opportunity
A written PI intake SOP — covering the first contact, qualification questions, document collection, and follow-up timeline — gives the paralegal a clear standard to work toward, reduces malpractice risk, and creates a training tool that eliminates Wilson from the intake quality loop. This is a half-day project with months of compounding benefit.

Build a Practice Area Profitability Dashboard
Profit Long-Term Play
